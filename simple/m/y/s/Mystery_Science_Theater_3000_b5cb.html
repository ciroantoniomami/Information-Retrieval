Mystery Science Theater 3000

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Mystery Science Theater 3000 or MST3K for short was a comedy television show, that was originally made in the U.S. state of Minnesota from 1988 to 1999. It was orignally made for a local station in Minnesota, then it moved to Comedy Central, and later The Sci-Fi Channel. The show was about a man (originally Joel Robinson, played by Joel Hodgson, the show's creator, and later Mike Nelson, played by Micheal J. Nelson, the show's head writer), who is put in a spaceship by an evil scientist and forced to watch some of the worst movies ever made. Two robots (Crow T. Robot and Tom Servo) watch the movies with him. While the movie is shown, the three make jokes and comments about the movie they watch.
