My Opera Community

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The My Opera Comunity is an online community which provides support from the Opera Browser. It also has casual social community services.

[edit] History

The My Opera Community started in August 2001. It currently has over 500,000 member, which are from all over the world.[1]

[edit] Upgrades in 2005

In September, 2005 the My Opera Comunity added a some improvments.[2] These improvments included most of the current features with are currently available.
