Monty Python

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Monty Python is a group of British actors who have acted in several comedy movies and had a television show, Monty Python's Flying Circus which was orignally between 1969 and 1974, and is still well-known and watched around the world.

They are: John Cleese, Eric Idle, Graham Chapman, Terry Gilliam, Terry Jones, and Michael Palin

The movies are: The Life of Brian, And Now for Something Completely Different, The Meaning of Life, and Monty Python and the Holy Grail.
