Monument

monument
The Taj Mahal, commissioned by the Muslim Mughal Emperor Shah Jahan, as a mausoleum for his wife, Arjumand Banu Begum.

A monument is a statue, building, or something else created to commemorate a person or important event. They are often designed as artistic objects to improve the appearance of a city or location.

Nelson's Column in Trafalgar Square, London, commemorates Admiral Horatio Nelson.

Corcovado Hill in Rio de Janeiro, Brazil with Jesus Christ the Redeemer statue

The "Monument to the Discoveries" in Lisbon, Portugal, commemorating famous Portuguese explorers.
