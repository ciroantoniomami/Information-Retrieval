Monica Lewinsky

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Monica Samille Lewinsky (born July 23, 1973 in San Francisco, California) is an American woman who, in 1997, became famous for being part of a scandal between her and former President of the United States of America, Bill Clinton.

She worked in the middle 1990´s as an intern at the White House and the Pentagon.

Recently, Lewinsky has started a business designing handbags and purses for her company, "The Real Monica".
