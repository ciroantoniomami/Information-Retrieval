Moses (Michelangelo)

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Moses is a statue that was made by Michelangelo. It is a statue of Moses from the Bible. The statue is unusual because Moses has horns on his head.

[edit] See also

The following are other statues by Michelangelo:

  • David
  • Pieta
