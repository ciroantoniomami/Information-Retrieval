Mount Rushmore

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The Mount Rushmore National Memorial with carvings of the heads of (from left) George Washington, Thomas Jefferson, Theodore Roosevelt, and Abraham Lincoln.

Mount Rushmore is a famous mountain in the United States in South Dakota. It has the heads four of America's greatest Presidents carved on it, George Washington, Thomas Jefferson, Theodore Roosevelt, and Abraham Lincoln. Gutzon Borglum carved the heads on the mountain. After Borglum's death his son, Lincoln finished it. The carving started in 1927 and ended in 1941.
