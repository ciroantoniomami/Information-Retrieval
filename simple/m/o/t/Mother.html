Mother

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Mother is a female parent. Mother is the opposite of father. The mother and father are parents. A mother gives birth to the child. To make a child the father must place his penis in the mother's vagina, thus having sexual intercourse.

In American English, other common words for mother are "Mom," "Mommy," Momma" and "Ma."

In British English, "Mum" and "Mummy" are used instead of "Mom" and "Mommy."
