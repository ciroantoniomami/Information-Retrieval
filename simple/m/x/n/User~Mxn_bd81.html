I am Minh Nguyễn, a Vietnamese-American student at Stanford University. [1] I live in Loveland, Ohio, United States. If you want to learn more about me, you can go to my user page at the English Wikipedia or to my weblog (online journal), which I update often.

I am a bureaucrat (super-administrator) for the Vietnamese Wikipedia and the Vietnamese Wiktionary, and I am an administrator for the Vietnamese Wikibooks, the original Wikisource, and the Meta-Wiki.

English and Vietnamese are the first languages I learned, but I know English much better than Vietnamese. In high school, I also studied Spanish.

I don't write much for the Simple English Wikipedia, but sometimes I remove vandalism here.
