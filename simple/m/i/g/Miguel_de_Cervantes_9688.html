Miguel de Cervantes

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

A painting of Cervantes
Cervantes is on Spain's 50 cent Euro

Miguel de Cervantes Saavedra (September 29, 1547 – April 23, 1616) was a Spanish writer. His most famous book was Don Quijote de la Mancha.
