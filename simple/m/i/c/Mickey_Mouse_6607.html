Mickey Mouse

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Mickey Mouse is one of the most popular cartoon characters in the world. He was created by Walt Disney and Ub Iwerks in 1928. He is one of the Walt Disney company's mascots and has appeared on a few Disney special cartoons, named as Fantasia, which stands for Fantasy on Spanish.
