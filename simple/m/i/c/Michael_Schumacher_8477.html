Michael Schumacher

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Michael Schumacher (2002)

Michael Schumacher (born 3 January 1969 in Hürth, near Cologne) is a German is one of the best Formula 1 driver. He has won seven world championship titles and has the most Grand Prix victories in the world. Michael's younger brother Ralf is also a Formula 1 driver. When he was small, his father gave him a little kart. He started getting interest from that time.
