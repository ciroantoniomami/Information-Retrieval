Michael Owen is the forward for Newcastle United FC in England. They compete in the F.A. Premier League. He also is on the English national soccer team, in which they compete in the World Cup, and other events. He was born December 14, 1979 in Chester, Cheshire. He has scored a total of 36 international goals since the previous World Cup in Germany.
