Ministry

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The term ministry can refer to the following:

  • Ministry (government department), a department of a government
  • Ministry (collective executive) (often the Ministry), the complete body of government ministers (whether or not they are in cabinet) under the leadership of a prime minister
    • The Ministry, also known as the Aireacht, the cabinet of the government established by the revolutionary Irish Republic of 1919-1922.
  • Religious ministry, a type of activity conducted by members of various faiths
  • Ministry (band), an industrial metal band
  • Ministry of Sound, a well known nightclub in London, sometimes referred to as the Ministry.
  • Ministry of Darkness, a professional wrestling faction led by the Undertaker in the WWE during 1999.
  • Ministry (magazine), a magazine for pastors published by the Seventh-day Adventist Church
