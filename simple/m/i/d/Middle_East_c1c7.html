Middle East

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Middle East with Turkey

The Middle East or Western Asia is an area of land and group of countries in the continent Asia. The countries are linked because they share history and most of the people of these countries are Muslims.

Some Middle Eastern countries include:

  • Armenia
  • Azerbaijan
  • Bahrain
  • Egypt
  • Iran
  • Iraq
  • Israel
  • Jordan
  • Kazakhstan
  • Kuwait
  • Kyrgyzstan
  • Lebanon
  • Oman
  • Qatar
  • Saudi Arabia
  • Syria
  • Tajikistan
  • The United Arab Emirates
  • Turkey
  • Turkmenistan
  • Uzbekistan
  • Yemen

And the areas called the West Bank and the Gaza Strip.
