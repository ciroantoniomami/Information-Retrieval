Midi-Pyrénées

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Midi-Pyrénées is marked in red

Midi-Pyrénées is a region in the south of France. With over 45.000 square kilometers, it is the biggest region of mainland France. There are 8 French departments in it. Its capital is Toulouse. About 2.5 mililon people live there, 850.00 of them in the urban area of Toulouse.
