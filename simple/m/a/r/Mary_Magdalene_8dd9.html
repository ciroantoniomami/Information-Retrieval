Mary Magdalene

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Mary Magdalene is a woman in the New Testament. She is one of the first witnesses to the resurrection of Jesus. Mary was forgiven of her sins when she washed the feet of Jesus and wiped them clean with her hair. She was envied for being loved so by Jesus.
