Marie Antoinette

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Marie Antoinette (November 2, 1755 in Vienna – October 16, 1793 in Paris) was an Archduchess of Austria and a Queen of France and Navarre.

[edit] Birth

Marie Antoinette was born in Vienna, Austria, on November 2, 1755. Her parents were Francis I, Holy Roman Emperor and Maria Theresa of Austria. She had two older brothers who were also Holy Roman Emperors:

  • Joseph II, Holy Roman Emperor
  • Leopold II, Holy Roman Emperor

[edit] Marriage and Children

Marie Antoinette was married in Versailles, France, to Louis XVI of France on May 16, 1770. Marie had three children.

[edit] Death

Marie Antoinette was executed in Paris, France, on October 16, 1793. She was buried in the Saint Denis Basilica.
