Maria Esther de Capovilla

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Maria Esther de Capovilla from Ecuador is the Guinness world record holder for oldest person. She was born on September 14, 1889 when the artist Vincent van Gogh was still alive. She died on August 27, 2006 at the age of 116 years old.
