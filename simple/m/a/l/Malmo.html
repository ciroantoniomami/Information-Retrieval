Malmo

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Malmö view

Malmo (or Malmö) as it is known in Swedish is the third largest city in Sweden. About 265,000 people live there. It is in the south west part of the Sweden, by the sea. The Öresund bridge goes from Malmö to Copenhagen.

Malmö has the bigest Middle Eastern community in Sweden. It is warm in the summer. In winter it is not quite as cold as in other parts of the country.
