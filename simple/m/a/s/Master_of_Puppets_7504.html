Master of Puppets

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Master of Puppets is Metallica's third and most famous album (with the exception of the black album). It was released by Elektra Records on February 21 1986. The album has 8 songs that have changed today's metal and music society. It still has several songs that are still played on the radio.

[edit] Track listing

  1. "Battery" (Hetfield, Ulrich) – 5:12
  2. "Master of Puppets" (Burton, Hammett, Hetfield, Ulrich) – 8:36
  3. "The Thing That Should Not Be" (Hammett, Hetfield, Ulrich) – 6:37
  4. "Welcome Home (Sanitarium)" (Hammett, Hetfield, Ulrich) – 6:27
  5. "Disposable Heroes" (Hammett, Hetfield, Ulrich) – 8:16
  6. "Leper Messiah" (Hetfield, Ulrich) – 5:40
  7. "Orion" (Burton, Hetfield, Ulrich) – 8:28
  8. "Damage, Inc." (Burton, Hammett, Hetfield, Ulrich) – 5:29
