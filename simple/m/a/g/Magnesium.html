Magnesium

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Magnesium is a chemical element that is a type of metal. Its chemical symbol is Mg, and its atomic number is 12. It is the eighth most abundant mineral in the earth's crust. It is found in large deposits of magnesite, dolomite and other minerals.
