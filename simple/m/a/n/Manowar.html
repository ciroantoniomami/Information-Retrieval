Manowar

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Manowar is an American heavy metal band from Auburn, New York, which formed in 1980.

[edit] Members

  • Eric Adams - vocal
  • Joey DeMaio - bass guitar / guitar / keyboard / classical guitar
  • Karl Logan - guitar / keyboard / classical guitar
  • Scott Columbus - drums and percussion

[edit] Albums

  • Battle Hymns (1982)
  • Into Glory Ride (1983)
  • Hail To England (1984)
  • Sign Of The Hammer (1984)
  • Fighting The World (1987)
  • Kings Of Metal (1988)
  • The Triumph Of Steel (1992)
  • Louder Than Hell (1996)
  • Hell On Wheels (Live, 1997)
  • Hell On Stage (Live, 1999)
  • Warriors Of The World (2002)
  • Gods Of War (2007)
