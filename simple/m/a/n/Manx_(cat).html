Manx (cat)

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

This is an image of a Manx cat.

The Manx is a breed of domestic cat. It either has no tail at all or it has a very short tail. This is because of a natural difference in spine length. The back legs are also longer than the front legs. The breed comes from the Isle of Man, where it was found as early as three hundred years ago.
