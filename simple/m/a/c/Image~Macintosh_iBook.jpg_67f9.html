Macintosh iBook Beauty shot of the last to current iBook - Early 2003. Photo is public domain. The specs of this iBook are (for those interested):

  • RAM: 384 MB
  • CPU: 900 MhZ G3
  • OS: Mac OS X 10.4.3 and Mac OS 9.2.2
  • VRAM: 32 MB
  • Screen size: 14.1 inches
