Murcia

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Murcia (red dot) on a map of Spain
Old bridge over the river

Murcia is the capital of the autonomous community of Murcia, in Spain, and her biggest city with a population of 410,000 in the city and 570,000 in the metropolitan area. The municipality is the seventh of Spain. The city is in the Segura Valley, in a land called la Huerta de Murcia, which is a very important agricultural center with oranges, lemons, tomatoes, onions, etc.; the rich kitchen garden typical of the Mediterranean lands. The city has a Baroque cathedral and a university.
