[edit] No flaming

The complaints against this article have nothing to do with fundamentalistic creationists, at least I haven't seen anybody comment who would fit that label. The problem with this article is not because of mutation (whatever kind), natural selection or evolution, but because this article is about stuff from the Marvel Comics Universe. As a proper encyclopedia, this article should thusly be titled somewhere along the lines of marvel comic mutants, marvel universe, section: mutants, or otherwise. For the time being I'll just put up a link to "mutation", and hope that some X-men fan will help out and remove the content to a correcter named article.

[edit] Major rewrite

The page has been moved from Mutant to Mutant (Marvel). In keeping, I rewrote the entire article based on first saying what a mutant was and then (to try and show notability) what their significance is. As a 40+ year collection of works, Marvel has used their mutants with one enduring theme: Racism and bigotry. The books serve well as a collection of opinions on all forms of Bigotry (Race, sexual orientation, even social position) so that is where I took the focus of the article. All inwiki was removed as unapplicable with the exception of the en.wiki article that explains mutants in the marvel universe in much more depth and detail. -- Creol 20:19, 5 November 2006 (UTC)
