Mendoza Province

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Mendoza is a Province of Argentina, located in the region called Cuyo. Its borders are San Juan in the north, San Luis and La Pampa in the east, Neuquén in the south, and Chile to the west.

It's the major wine producer of Argentina and has the highest hill of The Americas, Aconcagua.

[edit] Population

Potrerillos is a village at Mendoza Province near Andes

Mendoza has a population of 1,579,651 inhabitants (2001). Spanish is the main language but many people speak English, Italian and Portuguese, too.
