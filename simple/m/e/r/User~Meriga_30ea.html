[edit] My interlingual phrase of the week

Since I know a lot of languages (though English is native) at an intermediate level (like a [insert language here] 1st Grader), I will put a phrase of the week in a language and then translate it to English. I am not perfect, however, so I don't guarantee accuracy.

In English:

We are together, and we will never break apart.

In German:

Wir sind zusammen, und wir brechen nie ausseinander.
