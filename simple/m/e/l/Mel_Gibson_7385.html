Mel Gibson

Mel Columcille Gerard Gibson (b. January 3, 1956) is an American-Australian actor. He is from the United States, but his family moved to Australia in 1968, when he was 12. He was born in Peekskill, New York, United States.

Some of Mel Gibson's most famous movies are:

  • Mad Max (1979)
  • Gallipoli (1981)
  • The Bounty (1984)
  • Lethal Weapon (1987)
  • Braveheart (1995)
  • The Passion of the Christ (2004) which he directed.
