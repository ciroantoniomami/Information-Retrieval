Meiosis

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Meiosis is the splitting of a cell to produce gametes, the reproductive cells.

Meiosis is needed for sexual reproduction. In testicles, meiosis produces sperm, which are the male reproductive cells. In ovaries, meiosis produces ova, which are the female reproductive cells.
