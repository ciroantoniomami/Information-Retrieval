Metallica

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

A Metallica concert in London

Metallica is an American thrash metal/heavy metal band that Lars Ulrich and James Hetfield started in 1981. Since then they have become one of metal's most popular and successful bands and have sold over 90 million CDs worldwide.

[edit] Discography

  • 1983- Kill 'Em All
  • 1984- Ride the Lightning
  • 1986- Master of Puppets
  • 1988- ...And Justice For All
  • 1991- Metallica (often called the as "The Black Album")
  • 1996- Load
  • 1997- ReLoad
  • 1998- Garage, Inc.
  • 1999- S&M
  • 2003- St. Anger
