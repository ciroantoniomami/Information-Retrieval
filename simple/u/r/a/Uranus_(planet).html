Uranus (planet)

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Uranus and its moons

Uranus is the seventh planet from the Sun in the Solar System. It is a gas giant. It is the third largest planet in the solar system. It has 27 moons. The five main moons are Miranda, Ariel, Umbriel, Titania and Oberon. It is tilted on its axis so much that it is sideways.
