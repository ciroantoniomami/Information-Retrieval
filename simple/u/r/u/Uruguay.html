Uruguay

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Uruguay is a small country in South America. The language spoken there is Spanish. Its capital and largest city is Montevideo. Uruguay is bordered by two large neighbors, Brazil and Argentina. The only country in South America that is smaller than Uruguay is Suriname. The land there is mostly flat. They have many farms there.
