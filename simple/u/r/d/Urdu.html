Urdu

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Urdu is the name of one of the languages spoken in the Indian subcontinent. It is mainly spoken in India and Pakistan. It is an official language of Pakistan and in the Indian states of Andhra Pradesh, Jammu and Kashmir, Delhi, and Uttar Pradesh. About 104 million people speak the Urdu language. Many people who came from Pakistan to America still speaks their language (urdu). For example abbas ali speaks urdu and hes living in MO, USA
