Uncyclopedia

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Uncyclopedia is a website which is a parody of Wikipedia. The information there is not true. It is meant as a joke. Users make fun of things by creating pages about them in the same way users can create and edit pages in a wiki. For example, the page on George W Bush says that he is the same person as the Evil Emperor in the movie Star Wars. Some of the articles on Uncyclopedia may offend some people.
