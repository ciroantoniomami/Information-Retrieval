Category:Unprintworthy redirects

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Redirects in this category have been deemed unsuitable for inclusion in a printed Paper Wikipedia. While they may help in online navigation, they wouldn't be of much use in a hard-copy book.

To add a redirect to this category, put {{R unprintworthy}} after the redirect. Other Templates also use the category, see first (blank) sorted section.

This category is a self-reference.
