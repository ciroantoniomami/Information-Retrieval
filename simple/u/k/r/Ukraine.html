Ukraine (Україна [ukrajina]) is a country in Europe. Russia is East of Ukraine, Belarus is in the North, Poland and Slovakia are in the West, Hungary, Romania, Moldova and self-proclaimed Transnistria are to the South West and the Black Sea is to the South.

Ukraine is a republic. It is part of the Commonwealth of Independent States. The capital of Ukraine is Kiev (Киев by Russian [Kijef] or Київ - Kyiv [Kyjif] by Ukrainian).

[edit] Official language

The official language of Ukraine is Ukrainian (українська мова [ukrajin's'ka mova] by ukrainian), but ca. 30-50% used Russian in every day life.

[edit] Ukraine divided

Ukraine is divided into 24 oblasts, Autonomous Republic of Crimea (Автономна Республіка Крим [avtonomna respublika krym] by Ukrainian) and two cities with special status - Kyiv and Sevastopol in Crimea.

[edit] The largest cities of Ukraine

Kiev (the capital from 1934), Kharkiv (the capital of Soviet Ukraine in 1917-1934), Dnipropetrovsk, Donetsk, Odesa, Zaporizhia, Lviv (last one before 1939 - the polish territory).

[edit] Presidents of Ukraine:

Kravchuk Leonid Makarovych (1991-1994), Kuchma Leonid Danylovych (1994-2005), Yushchenko Victor Andriyovych (from 2005).

[edit] Modern History

In the Soviet epoch Ukraine was a Soviet Socialist Republic in Soviet Union. Independence day - 24 August 1991. President elections: 01 Dec. 1991, July 1994, Oct.-Nov. 1999, Oct.-Dec. 2004. Parliament elections: Mar. 1994, Mar. 1998, Mar. 2002, Mar. 2006. Constitution of Ukraine was adopted by Parliament (Verkhovna Rada) 28 Juny 1996 with changes 08 Dec. 2004.

[edit] Orange "Revolution"

The political festival in the time of president election in autumn-winter 2004 supported candidate Victor Yuschenko.
