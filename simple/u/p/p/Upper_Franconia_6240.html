Upper Franconia

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Upper Franconia (Oberfranken) is a part of the German state Bavaria. It has an area of 7,230,00 km² and about 1,200,000 people. It borders the German states of Sachsen and Thuringia, and also the Czech Republic. Bayreuth is its capital.
