U2 (band)

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

U2 is an Irish rock band. There are four members of the band.

Band members:

  • Lead singer Bono (stage name of Paul David Hewson ,born May 1960),
  • Lead guitarist The Edge (stage name of David Howell Evans, born August 8, 1961)
  • Bassist Adam Charles Clayton, better known as just Adam Clayton (born March 13, 1960)
  • Drummer Larry Mullen
  • The Band also makes many collaborations with Brian Eno.

The band was formed in Dublin, Ireland during October 1976 when Larry Mullen put a poster on his school's poster board seeking musicians for a new band.

U2 released an album called How to Dismantle an Atomic Bomb in the year 2004.
