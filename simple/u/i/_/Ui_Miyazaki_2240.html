Ui Miyazaki

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Ui Miyazaki (宮崎羽衣, Miyazaki Ui?, born August 23 1981 in Tokyo city) is a female Japanese voice actor from Tokyo, Japan.

[edit] Anime cast in

  • Ar Tonelico as Orica Mestomille
  • D.C.S.S. ~Da Capo Second Season~ as Aisia
  • Gift ~eternal rainbow~ as Kirino Konosaka
  • Hime-sama Goyojin as Na-na
  • Izumo: Takeki Tsurugi no Senki as Suzaku
  • Magical Kanan as Emi Kojima
  • Ray as Aka Ribbon
  • Strawberry Panic! as Remon Natsume
  • Sumomomo Momomo as Iroha Minamoto
