Trombone

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

A trombone

Trombone is a brass horn musical instrument. It is similar to a trumpet, expect the player pulls on a long part, called the slide, to make music. It also sounds deeper than a trumpet, and is usually said to be one of the bass clef instruments. This is because most people have an easier time playing notes from the bass clef, but some who are good at playing high notes play in the treble clef instead.
