Tottenham Hotspur F.C.

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Tottenham Hotspur Football Club, is an English football club.

The club plays in the FA Barclaycard Premiership. Their current stadium is White Hart Lane. The captain of Tottenham Hotspur is centre back Ledley King. Martin Jol is the Manager.
