To Love-Ru Troubles (To LOVEる -とらぶる- in Japanese) is a shônen manga. Its creators are Kentaro Yabuki (drawing) and Hasemi Saki (story).

[edit] Story

Yuuki Rito is a boy who is in High School. He is 15 years old and loves his classmate Haruna Sairenji. But he is too shy to say it to her. One day, a naked girl appeared in his bath. Her name is Lala, and she is an alien, the princess of the planet Deviluke. She attempts to escape from aliens who want her to return on planet Deviluke. But she doesn't want to and says that she wants to marry Rito. It's not really true but little by little, she really falls in love with him. He doesn't feel the same for her and instead prefers the shy Haruna. All becomes more complicated when the King, Lala's father, asks him to protect his daughter from the other pretenders, saying that otherwise, he would destroy the Earth.
