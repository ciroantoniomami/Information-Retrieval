Tomb Raider

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Tomb Raider is a computer and video game series. Originally they were made by Core Design. It was sold in 1996 for PC, PlayStation and Sega Saturn.

The main character of the game is a woman called Lara Croft. She is an archeologist and explores ancient places, searching old relics, often fighting a greater danger to the world.

Since 2001, Paramount Pictures has made two films like the game, starring Angelina Jolie.
