Toontown

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Toontown is a online video game where a lot of people play at one time. Players live life as a toon. Players can make a duck, bear, rabbit, dog, cat, horse, or monkey.

[edit] What the game's purpose is (object)

Players try to live life as a toon, playing games and making new friends. But it's not all paradise. Evil robots called COGS have taken over, and "gags" (cartoon fun) are used to defeat them. There isn't a purpose that players have to go by. A player can choose to be the top racer or to have the most "laffpoints". Many people like it because of this.

[edit] Gag types

All gags have different shape, have a different ability, or they need a certain skill level. Here are the types:

  • Toonup is a gag which lets players heal other players. This is an important gag.
  • Trap is a gag which "traps" the cogs, like quicksand.
  • Lure is a gag which freezes the COGS.
  • Sound is a gag which blasts the cogs with noise.
  • Throw is a basic starting gag. All toons have it.
  • Squirt is a gag that players also start with. It hits more than throw, but doesn't do as much damage.
  • Drop is a gag that does a lot of damage but hits the least.

[edit] Cogs

COGS are evil and have 4 types.

  • Sellbots- The lowest in the "cog terms". Includes the Senior VP.
  • Cashbots- Have a little more respect but are still pretty weak. Includes the CFO Cashbot.
  • Lawbots- Have almost full respect. Includes the Chief Justise (or CJ).
  • Bossbots- Pretty obvious that these are the highest cogs. The boss is not currently known
