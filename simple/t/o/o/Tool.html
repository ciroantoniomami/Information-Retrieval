Tool

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

For the name of the band, see Tool (band).

Tools are objects used to make doing work easier.

Some examples are: hammer, wrench (also called a spanner), hoe, saw, and shovel. Even very basic things like forks and spoons, and pens and pencils are tools. The bludgeon (a stick or rock used to smash things) was the very first tool made by humans. The knife is also one of the first tools humans ever made. Also can be a slang term for a stupid person who follows other people's commands.
