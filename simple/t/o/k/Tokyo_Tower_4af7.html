Tokyo Tower

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Tokyo Tower by day
Tokyo Tower by night

Tokyo Tower is a tower in Shiba Park, Minato-ku, Tokyo, Japan (35°39′31″N, 139°44′44″E). It is 333 m (1093 ft) tall, making it the world's highest self-supporting steel tower and the tallest structure in Tokyo.
