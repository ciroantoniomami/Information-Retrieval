Toy Story

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Toy Story was the first Disney/Pixar animated movie with its first release in 1995.

It is a children's film about toys that are owned by a young boy called Andy. Andy's favourite toy is Woody, the cowboy. Woody is also the main character in the movie. Woody is a rival of Buzz Lightyear, Andy's new space ranger toy.
