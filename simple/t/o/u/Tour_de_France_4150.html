Tour de France

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Lance Armstrong wears the yellow shirt.

The Tour de France, or Tour of France, is a famous (well known) bicycle race. It is held in France, every summer. It is among the most famous, and best paid, bike races in Europe. Recently, it was in the media (news), because some athletes (people who play sports) were using doping (They took drugs that weren't allowed to become faster and stronger). It goes through France, Italy and Spain. The winner is the one who finishes the race in the least accumulated time.
