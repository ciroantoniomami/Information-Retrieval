Toucan

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

A picture of a Keel-billed toucan.

Toucans are tropical birds.

[edit] Description

They have huge, colored beaks, colored feathers around their eyes and face, and black feathers on their body. They have large bodies and wings that are almost the size of their body. Their feathers are sleek and smooth.

[edit] Habitat and diet

They live in the tropical rainforests of South America, mostly in the country of Brazil. They live in the upper part of the trees in the rainforests.
