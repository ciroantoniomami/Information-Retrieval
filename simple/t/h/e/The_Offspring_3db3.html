The Offspring

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The Offspring is a punk group from Garden Grove, California. They formed in 1984.

[edit] Formation

  • Dexter Holland (singer)
  • Noodles (guitarist)
  • Greg K (bassist)
  • Atom Willard (drummer)

[edit] Discography

  • The Offspring (1989)
  • Baghdad (1991, EP)
  • Ignition (1992)
  • Smash (1994)
  • Ixnay on the Hombre (1997)
  • Americana (1998)
  • Conspiracy of One (2000)
  • Splinter (2003)
  • Greatest Hits (2005, compilation)
