The Incredible Hulk

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Wax statue of The Incredible Hulk at Madame Tussaud's Wax Museum in London, England.

The Incredible Hulk is a popular superhero created by Marvel Comics. There have been movies, video games, television shows, toys and other things made about this character.

The Incredible Hulk is the alter-ego of Bruce Banner, a mild-mannered scientist. One day when Bruce was working he was exposed to Gamma radiation. When he gets mad, he turns into the Hulk. The Hulk is huge and green skinned. When he turns into the Hulk all his clothes except his purple shorts rip. The Hulk has a lot of strength and is almost invincible.
