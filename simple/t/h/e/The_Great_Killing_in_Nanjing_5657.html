The Great Killing in Nanjing

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The Great Killing in Nanjing is an incident that took place in Nanjing, China during the Second World War. After the seige of Nanjing by the Imperial Japanese army, thousands of people were killed. The number of the people killed is thought to be 50,000~300,000.
