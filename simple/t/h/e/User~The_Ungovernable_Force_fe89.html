I also work on the non-simple English wikipedia. This is my page there.

My main interests are politics, music, and culture.

I am an anarchist, listen to punk and study anthropology. Most of my edits will involve anarchism and punk.

I'm a native english speaker. If some of what I say is confusing, please tell me--it will take a while for me to get used to writing in a more simple style.
