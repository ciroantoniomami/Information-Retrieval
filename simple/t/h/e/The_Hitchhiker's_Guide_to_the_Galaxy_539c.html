The Hitchhiker's Guide to the Galaxy

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The Hitchhiker's Guide to the Galaxy is a book written by Douglas Adams. It was first played on the radio as a series in 1978, and after that it became a collection of five books. In 1981 it was made into a television series, and in 2005 a film of the story was made.

The five books in the series are:

  • The Hitchhiker's Guide to the Galaxy
  • The Restaurant at the End of the Universe
  • Life, the Universe and Everything
  • So Long, and Thanks for all the Fish
  • Mostly Harmless
