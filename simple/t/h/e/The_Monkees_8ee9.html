The Monkees

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The Monkees were a rock band. The band was created originally for a comedy television show of the same name which aired on NBC, from 1966 to 1968. The members of the group were Davy Jones, Michael Nesmith, Peter Tork, and Micky Dolenz. They had hits with "Last Train to Clarksville", "I'm a Believer", and "Daydream Believer".
