The Sims 2

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The Sims 2 is the sequel to The Sims. It is a simulation game made by Maxis and EA Games.

The game features a full 3D graphics engine. Like the first Sims game, it has many expansion packs.

[edit] Expansion Packs

These are addons to the game which add new items, new places and new features. They are listed in the order that they came out in the shops. They are meant to come out 2 times a year.

Game Name Released Features
The Sims 2: University March 2005 Sims can go to university
The Sims 2: Nightlife September 2005 Sims have cars and can go downtown
The Sims 2: Open for Business March 2006 Sims can own businesses which serves as an alternative source of income.
The Sims 2: Pets October 2006 Sims can have pets.
The Sims 2: Seasons February 2007 There is now weather and seasons which affects your sims in ways such as in temperature.

(*) To be released.

There are meant to be 7 expansion packs in total. The evn numbered expansion packs (e.g Nightlife - EP 2,Pets - EP 4) apparently bring something from the original Sims game whilst odd numbered expansion packs (University - EP 1, Open for Business - EP 3 and the soon to be released Seasons - which will be EP 5) bring something completely new to the Sims 2 game. It is rumoured that after all 7 expansion packs are released, The Sims 3 will be released in Spring 2009.

[edit] Stuff Packs

These are addons which add only items. They are meant to be released 4 times a year, between expansion packs.

Game Name Released Theme
The Sims 2: Holiday Party Pack November 2005 Christmas
The Sims 2: Family Fun Stuff April 2006 Fantasy, Underwater, Tropical
The Sims 2: Glamour Life Stuff September 2006 Luxurious items
The Sims 2: Celebration Stuff April 2007 Party items
