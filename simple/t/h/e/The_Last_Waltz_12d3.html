The Last Waltz

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The Last Waltz was The Band's final concert which took place on Thanksgiving (November 26) in 1976 at the Winterland Ballroom in San Francisco, California. It was also made into a movie by Martin Scorsese, that came out in 1978, other than The Band some of the other musicians were:

  • Ronnie Hawkins
  • Dr. John
  • Bobby Charles
  • Neil Young
  • Neil Diamond
  • Joni Mitchell
  • Paul Butterfield
  • Muddy Waters
  • Eric Clapton
  • Emmylou Harris
  • Van Morrison
  • Bob Dylan
  • Ringo Starr
  • Ron Wood
  • Stephen Stills
  • Carl Radle
