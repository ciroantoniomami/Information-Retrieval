Thierry Henry

Thierry Henry (born August 17 1977 in Les Ulis, Essonne, France) is a French football player (footballer). He plays for Arsenal. He is considered one of the world's best's players.

Henry started his career at the small clubs in France. He had played for the Italian team, Juventus. In 2001, he joined Arsenal of England, where he became one of the best players in English Premier League. He has been named the Football Writers' Association Footballer of the Year three times and the French Player Of The Year four time since 2001.

On the international team, Henry has scored 6 goals in World Cup competitions. Only one French player, Michel Platini, has scored more goals than Henry.
