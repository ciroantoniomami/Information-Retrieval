[edit] Copy-paste from en:wiki

User, please do not just copy and paste articles over from English Wikipedia. You may think you are helping by adding more information, but the whole point of Simple English is to have pages that use simpler English. Using the English article as a starting point and then simplifying it is a good idea and many editors do it. But it is not helpful to just leave it there unsimplified. I am going to revert to an earlier version of "page name". Thank you.
