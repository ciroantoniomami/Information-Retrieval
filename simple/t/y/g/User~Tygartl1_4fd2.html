Hi! My name is Lindsey. I like Wikipedia a lot and I think that Simple English Wikipedia is a good idea. I still do some work over at en:wikipedia but I generally feel that I can be more helpful over here. I will sometimes watch the "new pages" page to help stop vandalism and to make new pages better. Lately, I've been doing quite a bit of category cleanup. I also enjoy adding pictures to pages whenever possible.

Simple English Wikipedia now has 15,485 articles. Great work everybody!

If you think I've made a mistake, please let me know.
