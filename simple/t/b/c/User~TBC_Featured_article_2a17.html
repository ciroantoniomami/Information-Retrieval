Featured Articles in Wikipedia

Featured articles are what many users think are the best articles on the Simple Wikipedia. To request an article to be featured, please add it under this section.

A featured article usually needs to include the following things:

  • Citations and external links
  • Use pictures when needed to, usually with captions
  • Written using simplified english
  • At an appropriate length
