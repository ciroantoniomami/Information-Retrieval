Tirana

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

A view of Tirana

Tirana (Albanian: Tiranë or Tirana) is the capital and largest city of Republic of Albania. Its population is estimated officially at 353,400 in 2003. Other people think there may be up to 1 millon people who live in the city. Founded in 1614, it became Albania's capital city in 1920.
