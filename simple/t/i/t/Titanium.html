Titanium

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Titanium is a very strong metal. Its one of hundreds of Chemical Elements in the peoriodic table of the elements. Its symbol is Ti, and it is used in making the strongest and lightest parts of modern fighter super planes.
