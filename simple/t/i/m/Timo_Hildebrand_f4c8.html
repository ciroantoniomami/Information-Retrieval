Timo Hildebrand

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Timo Hidebrand (born April 5 1979 in Worms, Germany) is a German footballer. He is now the second goalkeeper of the German national football team. Hildebrand has played for VfB Stuttgart since 1999. In 2007, he will play for FC Valencia.
