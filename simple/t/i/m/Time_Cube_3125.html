Time Cube

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Time Cube is a website made by Gene Ray. It explains his "Time Cube" theory, which describes the planet Earth as being a cube, and time as having four "corners". Many scientists reject this theory, and they claim that the site makes no sense and cannot be tested.

The Time Cube website is written in what might be seen as an angry voice (Gene Ray says all scientists are evil, but is never racist), and some people think that the TimeCube.com website is difficult to understand.
