TVXQ

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

TVXQ in 2006

TVXQ (Tong Vfang Xien Qi) is a five-member South Korean boyband active in their native country and Japan. Its members are U-know Yunho, Hero Jaejoong, Micky Yoochun, Xiah Junsu, and Max Changmin. The group is popular in East Asian countries including Taiwan and China. They debuted in 2004 and belongs to Korean record company SM Entertainment. They are known by different names. In Korea, they are known as Dong Bang Shin Ki (abbreviated DBSK) and Tohoshinki in Japan.

To date, the group released two Japanese studio albums and three Korean studio albums, all of which debuted at #1. Their third album, O - Jung.Ban.Hap, was released in 2006 and sold over 330,000 copies. It is their most successful album to date. The album has continued to chart in 2007 along with the group's 2005 album Rising Sun. Their first Japanese album Heart, Mind, and Soul performed modestly peaking at #25 on the Oricon Chart. The album sold less than 20,000 copies. Their second album Five in the Black showed improvement. The album peaked at #2 and sold over 26,000 copies in its first week of release.
