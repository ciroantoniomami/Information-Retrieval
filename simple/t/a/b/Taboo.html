Taboo

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

In a society, a taboo is something that society frowns upon. People talk about taboos only reluctantly. Actions that have been declared to be a taboo are forbidden to be taken. People will only do them in secret. Talking about taboos may lead to embarassing someone.
