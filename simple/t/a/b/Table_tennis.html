Table tennis

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Table tennis, also known as Ping Pong (a trademarked name), is one of the most popular sports in the world, with many players in many countries. It is played by two or four people in a table. To play this game, people use rackets and a small ball.

  • 乒乓球 (Ping Pang Qiu) is the official name for the sport in China, Hong Kong and Taiwan.
  • 卓球 (Takkyu) is the official name for the sport in Japan.
