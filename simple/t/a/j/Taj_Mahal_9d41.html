Taj Mahal

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The Taj Mahal.

Taj Mahal is a beautiful tomb built in the 17th century by Moghul ruler Shah Jahan in memory of his wife. It is in the city of Agra, India. Many people think it is one of the most beautiful buildings ever built. The Taj Mahal is slowly being destroyed by acid rain.

It is an example of Murghal architecture - a mixture of building design ideas from Indian, Persian (old Iran), and Muslim art customs.
