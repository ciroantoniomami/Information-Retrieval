Tajikistan is a country in Asia.

[edit] Geography

Tajikistan is one of the new countries in central Asia. It is west of China, north of Afghanistan, east of Uzbekistan and south of Kyrgyzstan.

A map of Tajikistan

Tajikistan is landlocked and it is in the middle of the continent of Asia.

Its total area is only about 143,100 km². It is slightly smaller than Wisconsin, US. Tajikistan’s borders are 3,651 km long.

The climate is midlatitude continental, with hot summers and mild winters.

Dushanbe railway station

Almost all of the country (85 percent) is mountainous terrain with river-walleys running across, however high altitude mountains of Pomir are in the eastern part of the country, (which is beginnings of Hymalayas in the west). The Pomir Mountains also called `the roof of the world´. The climate there is semiarid to polar. The mountains cover an area of about 120,000 km². There are other countries within the mountains range. The mountains are between 3600 to 4400m high.

[edit] History

The land that is now Tajikistan has been lived in continuously since 4,000 BC. It has been under the rule of various empires throughout history, mostly the Persian Empires.

In the year 800, Islam came to Tajikistan.

In 1868, Tajikistan became a Russian Colony. It later became a part of the Soviet Union On September 9th of 1991, after long periods of mass protests against Soviet Government, Parliament of Tajikistan declared independence from Soviet Union, and held first presidential elections. Rahmon Nabiev who run the country during Soviet Union in the late 70s and early 80s, become president. But soon his unability to bring any much needed reforms to country, sparked mass protests in the capital city, Dushanbe. To which government responded by organising pro-government demonstration, mainly made of old Communist Party loyalists and population of southeast of the country bused to the city. That mascarade didn't work, so government of Rahmon Nabiev openly armed this pro-government demonstrants, which promted the Opposition to arm themself. After all this bloody civil war broke out. [edit] Political information

Tajikistan is a President's Republic. The capital city is Dushanbe. The official language is Tajiki-Persian.

Tajikistan is also a member of the Commonwealth of Independent States, the CIS. Tajikistan is number eight in size of the CIS.
