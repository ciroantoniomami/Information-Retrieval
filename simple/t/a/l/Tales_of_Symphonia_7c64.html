Tales of Symphonia is a video game released in 2004 for the Nintendo Gamecube. It is a fantasy RPG.

[edit] Story

The main character Lloyd Irving is sent away from his hometown, along with his friends Genis Sage and Colette Brunel. Genis' sister Raine and the warrior Kratos join them on their journey. Eventually, Colette begins turning into an angel, and Lloyd learns that she is in charge of saving the world. Two parties want her to stop, however: the Desians, bad guys that enslave humans, and Sheena, a woman that wants to kill Colette. Sheena and Colette save each other from the Desians, and become friends. Eventually, they get ready to save the world, but Kratos betrays Colette and the others to Cruxis, the angels who control the Desians. Lloyd and the others manage to escape, and go to a different world called Tethe'alla. There, they make three new friends called Zelos, Presea, and Regal. Kratos still wants to find them however, and the Cruxis leader, Mithos, is very angry. Lloyd's group finds out that if they want to save the world, they need to have Sheena gain the power of the eight Summon Spirits, which are magical creatures. After using the power of the summon spirits, the problem gets worse, and Lloyd and Kratos need to team up to save the world from being consumed by the Giant Kharlan Tree. Yuan uses sleeping gas on everyone, which is foreshadowed in the cutscene where Lloyd says he had suddenly become tired. After this, Lloyd awakens in the middle of the night to Yuan and Kratos, along with two of Yuan's henchman, and that is where Lloyd learns that Kratos is his father. Kratos dives in front of Lloyd to stop him from being hurt. Kratos then tells Lloyd that there is a ninth Summon Spirit by the name of Origin, but Lloyd has to fight Kratos to get its help, because Origin is sealed by Kratos himself. Once the Summon Spirit helps Lloyd wield the eternal sword, they venture to Derris Kharlan and there is one last conflict with Mithos.
