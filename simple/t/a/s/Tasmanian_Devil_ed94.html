Tasmanian Devil

A Tasmanian Devil (Sarcophilus harrisii) is a meat eater mammal that looks after babies after they have been born that lives in Tasmania, a state of Australia.

It is the largest carnivourus marsupial in the world. They sleep during the day and are awake during the night (they are nocturnal animals)
