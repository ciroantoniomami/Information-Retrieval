Tattersalls

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Tattersalls is the name of a company in Australia that helps people to bet money. Tattersalls has made lots of money. A woman named Helen Harvey owned Tattersalls and had lots of money. When she died, she gave the money to her friend Martin Bryant. When Martin Bryant went to jail after police say that he killed people in the Port Arthur massacre, Martin Bryant had to give all of the money to people who were killed or injured there. Martin Bryant does not have any money anymore.
