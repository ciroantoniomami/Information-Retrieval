Taco

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Tacos with barbacoa.
A plate of tacos.

A taco is a traditional Mexican delicacy that consists of a tortilla that is rolled or folded around a filling. Grilled beef, picadillo, fish, chicken or pork are some common fillings. Tacos are popular in the United States. Many tacos consist of meat and vegetables.
