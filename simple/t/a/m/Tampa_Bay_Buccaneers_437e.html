Tampa Bay Buccaneers

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The Tampa Bay Buccaneers are a American Football team in the National Football League (NFL). They are a part of the Southern Division of the National Football Conference. The Buccaneers are from Tampa, Florida. The team was founded in 1976.
