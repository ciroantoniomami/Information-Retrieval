Tampa Bay Devil Rays

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The Tampa Bay Devil Rays are a Major League Baseball team that plays in St. Petersburg, Florida.

[edit] History

The Devil Rays played their first game on March 31, 1998 against the Detroit Tigers. They have been one of the most unsuccessful teams in all of the Major Leagues.
