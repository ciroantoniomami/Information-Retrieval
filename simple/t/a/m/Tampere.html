Tampere

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Tampere Railway Station

Tampere (Swedish: Tammerfors) is the third largest city in Finland. It is the capital of Pirkanmaa region.

Tampere was founded in 1779. As of 2005, there are 204,385 inhabitants. The current mayor is Jarmo Rantanen.
