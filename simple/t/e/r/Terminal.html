Terminal

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

A terminal is an endpoint of a connection. People talk about ferry terninals, or terminals at airports. Sometimes a train station is called a terminal. Buses may also be parked in terminals, especially the long-distance buses.


When the word is used as an adjective, it means leading to death. A disease may be terminal.
