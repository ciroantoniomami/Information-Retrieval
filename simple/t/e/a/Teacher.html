Teacher

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

A teacher is a person who gives information. A teacher often works in a classroom. There are many different kind of teachers. Some teachers teach young children, in schools called elementary or primary schools. Others teach older children in middle, junior high, and high schools. Some teachers teach adults in more advanced schools (for example, colleges and universities).
