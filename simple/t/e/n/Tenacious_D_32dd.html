Tenacious D

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Tenacious D in 2006. Left to right: Kyle Gass and Jack Black

Tenacious D is a band. The members of the band are the actor Jack Black and his best friend Kyle Gass. The band started in Los Angeles in California. Their songs have lots of swearing.
