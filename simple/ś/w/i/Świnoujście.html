Świnoujście

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Świnoujście is a town in Pomerania, northwestern Poland, with about 42,000 people (2006). It is situated on the Swina river, which flows into the Baltic Sea. In Świnoujście is the the largest and longest beach in Poland. Świnoujście is also a health resort and has a modern harbour.
