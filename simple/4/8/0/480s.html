480s

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Centuries: 4th century - 5th century - 6th century

Decades: 430s - 440s - 450s - 460s - 470s - 480s - 490s - 500s - 510s - 520s - 530s

Years: 480 481 482 483 484 485 486 487 488 489 490

Events

  • 481 - Clovis I becomes king of the Franks upon the death of Childeric I (or 482)
  • 481 - Baekje, Silla, and Daegaya form an alliance against Goguryeo.
  • 488 - Theodoric the Great becomes king of the Ostrogoths.
  • 488 - Kavadh I succeeds Balash in Persia.
