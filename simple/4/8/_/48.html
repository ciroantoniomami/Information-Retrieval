48

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

[edit] Events

  • Roman Emperor Claudius invests Agrippa II with the office of superintendent of the Temple in Jerusalem.
  • probable date of the Apostolic Council. Saint Paul begins his first mission (approximate date).
  • According to Christian legend, Martha travels to Avignon.
  • Vitellius is a Roman Consul.
  • The Silures, with the help of Caratacus, revolt against the Romans in Britain.
  • The Hsiung-nu empire dissolves.
