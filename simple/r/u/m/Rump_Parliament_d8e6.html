Rump Parliament

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The Rump Parliament was the name of the English Parliament that followed the Long Parliament, after Pride's Purge of December 6 1648 had removed those Members of Parliament that were against the plan to try King Charles I for high treason.
