Ruwer (municipality)

Verbandsgemeinde Ruwer
Verbandsgemeinde Ruwer in Germany

Ruwer is a municipality (a convention community called Verbandsgemeinde Ruwer) on the river Ruwer near Trier in Rhineland-Palatinate, Germany.
