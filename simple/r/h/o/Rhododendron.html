Rhododendron

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

A typical Rhododendron (R. fortunei)

Rhododendron is a large genus of flowering plants. Most of them display their flowers quite aptly. What gardeners know as Azalea is in fact a kind of Rhododendron. In the disputed territory of Kashmir it is the state flower of Azad Kashmir which is currently administered by Pakistan[citation needed].

Many people have Rhododendron in their garden or flower pot.
