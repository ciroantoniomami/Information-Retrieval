Ravioli

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Lemon dill shrimp ravioli

Ravioli is a type of Italian pasta dish. It is usually two layers of pasta dough with a filling between the two layers. Fillings may be meat or vegetables. People eat this kind of food with sauce or melted butter.
