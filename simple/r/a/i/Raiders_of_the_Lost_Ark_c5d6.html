Indiana Jones and the Raiders of the Lost Ark is an action movie that came out in 1981 from Paramount Pictures. It stars Harrison Ford as the main character, Indiana Jones. The movie has two sequels, Indiana Jones and the Temple of Doom (1984) and Indiana Jones and the Last Crusade (1989). The movie was directed by Steven Spielberg (also from Jaws), and produced by George Lucas (who also made Star Wars and American Graffiti).
