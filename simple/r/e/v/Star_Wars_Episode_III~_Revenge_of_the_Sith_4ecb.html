Star Wars Episode III: Revenge of the Sith was a movie released in 2005. Episode III was one of the most anticipated movies of 2005. This movie is the last movie that George Lucas will make in the Star Wars series.

Spoiler warning: The text below is about parts of the story or how it ends.

The movie shows the events that led to the transformation of Anakin Skywalker to Darth Vader of the Original Trilogy. It also shows how Palpatine turned the Galactic Republic into the Galactic Empire as well as showing the destruction of the Jedi.
