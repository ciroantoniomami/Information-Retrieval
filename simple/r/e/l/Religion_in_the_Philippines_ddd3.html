Religion in the Philippines

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

It has been suggested that the text below be merged (added to) the Philippines page. (Discuss)

Most of the people in the Philippines are Christians. About 92% of the people are Christians. Most people in the Philippines belong to the Roman Catholic faith (83%). A small percentage of the people are Protestants (9%), or Muslims (5%). There are also some Buddhists, Hindus and other religions (with 3%). .
