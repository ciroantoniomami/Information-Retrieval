Regions of England

The region in England is also named the Government Office Region. It is the highest level layer of local government in England.

[edit] List of regions (see map)

  1. London
  2. South West
  3. South East
  4. West Midlands (region)
  5. North West
  6. North East
  7. Yorkshire and the Humber
  8. East Midlands
  9. East
