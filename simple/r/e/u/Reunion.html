Reunion (east of Madagascar and Africa)
Satellite view of Reunion

Réunion (French: La Réunion) is an island and overseas département (département d'outre-mer, or DOM) of France. It is in the Indian Ocean east of Madagascar, about 200 km (125 miles) southwest of Mauritius.
