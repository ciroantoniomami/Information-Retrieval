Riyadh

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Riyadh at night

Riyadh (Arabic: الرياض‎ ar-Riyāḍ) is the capital city of Saudi Arabia. It is located in Ar Riyad Province in the Najd region. It is located in the center of the Arabian peninsula on a large plateau and is has a population of 4,260,000 people (around 20% of the nation's population).
