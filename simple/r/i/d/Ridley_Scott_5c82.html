Ridley Scott

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Image:Ridley.jpg

Ridley Scott (born November 30, 1937 in South Shields) is a famous British film director. He has made some of the most popular and successful films ever made. Some of the films he has directed are:

  • The Duellists (1977)
  • Alien (1979)
  • Blade Runner (1982)
  • Legend (1985)
  • Thelma & Louise (1991)
  • G.I. Jane (1997)
  • Gladiator (2000)
  • Hannibal (2001)
  • Black Hawk Down (2001)

He also directed an important and expensive television commercial for Apple Computer called "1984".
