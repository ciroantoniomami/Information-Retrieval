Roxette

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Roxette was a band formed in 1986 and is pop group from Sweden, with Per Gessle and Marie Fredriksson. They sang the song "It Must Have Been Love", first as a Christmas song in 1987 and then as an "ordinary song" in 1990 when it became a big hit. In 1991 they sang the song "Joyride", which also became a big hit. These songs are some of the band's greatest hits.
