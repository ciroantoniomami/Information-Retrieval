Robert Duvall

Robert Duvall is an American actor. He was born January 5, 1931 in San Diego, California. Duvall won an Academy Award for Best Actor for his role in Tender Mercies (1983). He has appeared in many other films including:

  • To Kill A Mockingbird (1962)
  • M*A*S*H (1970)
  • The Godfather (1972)
  • Apocalypse Now (1979)
  • The Apostle (1997)
