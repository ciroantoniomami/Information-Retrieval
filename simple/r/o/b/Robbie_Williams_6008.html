Robbie Williams

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Robbie Williams (February 13, 1974) was an English singer from the group Take That. He later went on to a solo career. Many people consider Angels, released in 1997, to be his best song. He is more popular than the other members of Take That.
