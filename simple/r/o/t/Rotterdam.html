Rotterdam

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Rotterdam is the second largest city in the Netherlands. About 588,000 people live there. The biggest city is Amsterdam. In the urban area, there are about 1.1 million people.

Rotterdam also has one of the biggest, and busiest ports in the world. Its the biggest port in Europe. The ports of Hamburg and possibly Marseille have a similar size.
