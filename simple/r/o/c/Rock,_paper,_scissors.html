Rock, paper, scissors

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Rock , paper, scissors is a famous hand-game. Rules apply:Paper covers rock, scissors cut paper, scissors is defeated by rock.

In some countries, such as Canada have considered this hand game a sport because of the intesity of the game, betting and strategy.
