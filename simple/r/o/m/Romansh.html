Romansh

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

This language has its own Wikipedia Project.
See the Romansh edition


Romansh (also spelled Rumantsch, Romansch or Romanche) is one of Switzerland's four national languages. (The other three are French, German and Italian.) Fifty thousand people in the canton of the Grisons use it as their native tongue.
