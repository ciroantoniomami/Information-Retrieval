Romano Prodi

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Prodi at the 2006 G8 summit

Romano Prodi (born August 9, 1939 in Scandiano, Italy) is an Italian politician, leader of a left-wing coalition named L'unione (The Union).

He has been Head of the Italian Government since 1996 and was re-elected a second time in 2006, when he defeated Silvio Berlusconi, leader of the right-wing coalition named Casa delle Libertà (House of freedom).
