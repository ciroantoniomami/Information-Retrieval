Slur

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Slur is to talk very fast and mix words together in doing so.

Here is a list of a few common words said with easy "slurred" speaking in English, along with the normal written form:

  • alotta = a lot of
  • coulda = could have
  • didja = did you
  • dunno = do not know
  • fulla = full of
  • gimme = give me
  • gonna = going to
  • hafta = have to
  • izzy = is he
  • kinda = kind of
  • n'ku = thank you
  • outta = out of
