Screenplay

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

A screenplay or script is the writen instruction about what happens in a movie or television program. The screenplay for a movie has everything that happens in the movie. It includes the plot, the dialogue of the characters, and all the actions of the characters. The person who writes the screenplay is called a screenwriter. A screenplay for television is called a teleplay.
