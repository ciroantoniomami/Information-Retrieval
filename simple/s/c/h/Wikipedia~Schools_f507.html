[edit] Welcome!

Welcome to Wikipedia! A Wikipedia is an online encyclopedia which anyone can edit, including you! There are Wikipedias in many languages, with lots of people working to make them better. This is the Simple English Wikipedia, where the articles are easy to understand.


To find out more about Wikipedia and how you can make changes to it, take the student tutorial.

To find information in this Wikipedia, type what you are looking for in the search box on the left, or click one of the subjects below.
