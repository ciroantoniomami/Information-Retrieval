Scissors

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Scissors are used for sewing, in the kitchen, and for paper.

Scissors are a tool used for cutting thin material like:

  • paper
  • cardboard
  • metal foil
  • thin plastic
  • food
  • cloth
  • rope
  • wire
  • hair
  • nail

Knives are better than scissors for some uses. Unlike a knife, scissors have two sharp edges. Some types of scissors, like children's scissors, are not very sharp. Children's scissors and are often protected with plastic. These are called "Safety Scissors".

Scissors can be made for left-handed or right-handed people. Using scissors made for the wrong hand is very hard for most people.
