Sioux Falls, South Dakota

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Sioux Falls (pronounced "soo falls") is the largest city in the U.S. state of South Dakota. As of the 2000 census, it had a population of 123,975. Sioux Falls is the county seat of Minnehaha County.It is in the eastern part of the state.

The city was incorporated in 1876.
