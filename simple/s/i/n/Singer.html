Singer

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

A singer is a person who sings as a job. Most singers are famous for their songs. Some singers also play music while singing. Singing is important in films.

Types of singing vary and there are many different genres and styles. For example, opera combines high power and volume with soprano notes in a varying amount of languages, but pop is middle range singing combining a catchy melody and beats.

A good singer will combine a large number of different aspects into the performance. Good breathing techniques, a large vocal range, feeling the song through emotional language and breathing, and also acting to provide a song with meaning. A good singer can pick up a song very easily and often practise excessively.

Singers can act as a solo, a group or a choir. Solo's usually vary in styles, as do group, choirs can include gospel, church music and even pop songs where there are usually two groups of singers, soprano and alto. Sopranos have a higher range and altos have a lower range. Combining these to can create various harmonies which add textures to songs and make them better for the ear. What is singing? Singing is the creation of musical tones by the human voice with or without words. Singing is a natural form of expression found in cultures and societies throughout the world. Singing is usually accompanied with one or more instruments. If you want to have a carrier in singing when you are older you should start young. This means having singing lessons and being able to read music and the rest. Some of the reasons that people sing are to express the way they are feeling at the time, or something bad could have happened that they want to share, or something good could have happened. Singing is art, just a different way of expressing it. Singing does not have to be done on your own. You can be in a duo or even in a choir . Singing sometimes reflects the mood you are in.
