[edit] Bureaucrat

Hello Simon, thanks for agreeing to take over as bureaucrat. You can make sysops using the Special:Makesysop page. There are instructions on using this at m:Bureaucrat. Basically, the job involves making admins after consensus is reached at Wikipedia:Administrators. You can't desysop people, so leave a note at m:requests for permissions if you need to do this. Good luck. Angela 02:01, 23 Oct 2004 (UTC)
