[edit] Sources

  • Internet Dictionary Project using a Basic English Word List put in using how to do it at Basic English Translators
  • Words not present in Basic English or VOA Special English that are needed to talk to a person with an English mother language.

[edit] Word List Group Short Forms

  • BE 850: Basic English 850 Words
  • BE S350: Basic English Subsequent 350
  • BE IW: Basic English International Words
  • BE IWS: Basic English International Science Words
  • BE GS100: Basic English General Science 100 Words
  • BE PC50: Basic English Physics-Chemistry 50 Words
  • BE VS100: Basic English Verse 100 Words
  • BE EC50: Basic English Economics 50 Words
  • VOA: VOA Special English VOA Special English Word Book
  • EML: Needed to talk to a person with an English Mother Language
  • SoundSpel: Variant spelling option. For further information go to: en:SoundSpel and SoundSpel (TM)
