Smile

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

A ball with a smiley face.
A natural smile with teeth showing.
A girl smiling.

A smile is a facial expression a person makes when they are is happy. People smile for many reasons e.g. told a funny joke or smiling for a picture to be taken.
