Sea of Japan

The Sea of Japan (East Sea) is a sea in the western Pacific Ocean. It is between Korea to the west, Russia to the north and Japan to the east and south.

[edit] Arguments about the Name

South Korea want to change the name of to the "East Sea". Many maps and books now use both names.
