Search engine

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

A Search engine is a web site on the World Wide Web (www) of the Internet that helps us to quickly and easily find the web pages we want.

We can put in words on the subject that we are interested in, and the search engine will find pages for that have those words in.

Some examples of search engines are:

  • Google
  • AltaVista
  • Dogpile
  • Yahoo
  • HotBot
  • Yooci
  • Globepage
