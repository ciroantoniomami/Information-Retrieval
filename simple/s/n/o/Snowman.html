Snowman

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

a snowman

A snowman is a statue of a man made of snow. Snowmen are usually made when people make three balls of snow that are different sizes. They will stack the balls on top of each other to make a snowman, and stick some things in him to make him look more like a man.
