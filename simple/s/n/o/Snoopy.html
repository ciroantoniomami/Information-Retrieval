Snoopy

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Snoopy is the name of Charlie Brown's pet beagle in the comic strip Peanuts, by Charles M. Schulz. Born on the Daisy Hill Puppy Farm, Snoopy started out as just a dog, but eventually grew into a key figure in the comic strip. He often had wise things to say about the humans around him.
