Snakes on a Plane

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Snakes on a Plane is a 2006 movie starring Samuel L. Jackson, about snakes overrunning a plane. The snakes take over the plane, and Samuel L. Jackson's character Neville Flynn kills them all.
