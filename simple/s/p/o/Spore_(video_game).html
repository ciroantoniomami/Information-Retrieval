Spore (video game)

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Spore is a computer game made by the person who made The Sims and SimCity. It consists of six phases of gameplay, the tide pool, creature, tribal, city, civilization and space phases. It is a game in which the player is able to create and evolve a species from a germ into a galactic superpower. It is due to be released in late 2007.
