Spider-Man

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Spider-Man is a comic book made by Marvel Comics. Spider-Man is a super hero with spider powers. His real name is Peter Parker. He is just an average man. He works for the daily bugle were his job is to take pictures of himself as spider man. He got his powers when he was bitten by a radioactive spider. Back in 2004 spider man came out in theaters. He fought the green goblin in that movie. In 2005 spider man 2 came out. In this movie he battles doctor octopus and finally reveals his secret identity to Mary Jane. Now on may 4 2007 spider man 3 is coming out. In this movie he fights himself as well as the sandman. It promises to be the best one yet.
