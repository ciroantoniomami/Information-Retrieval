I am a 17 year old girl. On the Internet, I often say that I am a female dragon. I like to write. I mostly write stories based on other people's stories. I also draw, mostly with either a pencil or pen, but I do visit an oekaki board once in a while. I also keep a LiveJournal, but I do not add to it very much. Sparky the Seventh Chaos is what everyone calls me on the Internet. You can look at my art, writing, and LiveJournal by clicking on the links in my External Links section.


In the Wikipedia, I like work on pages about video games, animals, and things that are not famous. I also like adding U.S. measurements to pages with only metric, and metric to pages with only U.S. measurements, using this conversion website. I also like to correct spelling mistakes.

I came to this website from the main English Wikipedia. I also occationally contribute to Wikiquote. My user name is the same as it is here.

[edit] Philosophy

I do not subscribe to any one religion or philosophy; rather, I mix ideas from a whole bunch. In short, I think:

  • Evolution is a fact of life (And those who confuse evolution with abiogenesis are misinformed)
  • I like the idea of reincarnation
  • I think of karma in a slightly modified way from the normal belief
  • I think there's a paradise/Heaven
  • I don't think there's a Hell
  • I'm agnostic about any sort of god in the conventional sense
    • This does not include beings who simply happen to be all-powerful; next idea explains that
  • I believe in infinite multiple universes, or in other words in an infinitely large Multiverse
    • This leads to all-powerful beings as a necessity of infinity, just not necessarily our own universe.
      • Although... since everything's possible...
        • *head implosion*
