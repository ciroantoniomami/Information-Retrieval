Stephen Hawking

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Stephen Hawking is wheelchair-bound because of ALS.

Stephen William Hawking, CH CBE FRS (born January 8 1942) is an English theoretical physicist and mathematician. He is one of the world's leading theoretical physicists. Hawking is professor of mathematics at the University of Cambridge (a position that Isaac Newton once had). He suffers from ALS or Lou Gehrig's Disease, he can not move or talk very well and he uses a wheelchair to move and a special computer to talk for him.
