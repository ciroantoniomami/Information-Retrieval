Steinach

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Steinach is a German ski city in the south of Thuringia. Larger cities nearby are Coburg and Sonneberg. The population of Steinach is about 5,000. The mayor of the city is Ulrich Kurtz.
