Steve Ballmer

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Steve Ballmer (born March 24, 1956 in Detroit, Michigan), is the CEO of the Microsoft Corporation. He is the 24th richest person in the world. He is known to yell loudly to give his thoughts. He needed surgery in 1991 after yelling "Windows, Windows, Windows" in Japan. He is also known for viral videos (videos which are famous online), such as "Monkey boy" where he is dancing and yelling uncontrollably for 45 seconds. Another video is where while he is sweating alot, yelling "developers, developers, developers" until the audience starts clapping.
