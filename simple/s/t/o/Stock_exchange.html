Stock exchange

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

A stock exchange, share market or bourse is a place where many people go to sell and trade company stock, which is the money a company has earned through shares.

The New York Stock Exchange, one of the biggest stock exchanges in the United States.
