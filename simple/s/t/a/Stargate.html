Stargate

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

A drawing of the stargate from the movie Stargate.

Stargate is a 1994 science fiction movie. It starred Kurt Russel. Stargate also refers to the television series Stargate SG-1 and its spin-off Stargate Atlantis, or to Stargate Infinity. The Stargate universe is big. It includes books, games, toys, or comic books.

[edit] Plot

Spoiler warning: The text below is about parts of the story or how it ends.

At Giza scientists find a big metalic ring. Archeologist Doctor Daniel Jackson is called by the US Air Force to translate hieroglyphics on the ring.
