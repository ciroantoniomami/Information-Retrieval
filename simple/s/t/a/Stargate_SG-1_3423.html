Stargate SG-1 is a science fiction television program. It takes place after the film Stargate. The show started on July 27, 1997 on the TV channel Showtime, from 1997-2002, and then on the TV channel Sci Fi, from 2002-2007. The show ended with the episode, "Unending" on March 13, 2007.
