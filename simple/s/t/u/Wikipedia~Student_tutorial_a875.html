Wikipedia:Student tutorial

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Welcome to Wikipedia! Wikipedias are places where many people are working together to make encyclopedias in many languages. We only use very simple English words and simple ways of writing here, so our articles are much easier to understand.

Anybody can make changes or make new articles for Wikipedia. Try it out now! Just click the 'Change this page' link at the top of the page and add something below the line. You can then click 'Save page' to save your changes or 'Show preview' to show what they would look like. When you are changing other pages, it's good if you put a brief explanation of what you've done in the 'Edit summary' box.
