Don't be scared

There are lots of things to remember when making changes to Wikipedia. The most important thing is to not be scared of making these changes. Don't worry about making mistakes, we can easily fix them.

If you need help when making changes, first visit the Help Centre. If you are still stuck, ask your teacher, or another member on their talk page. You can also ask an administrator. Remember that not everyone will be online, so don't expect an instant reply!
