St. Basil's Cathedral

St. Basil's Cathedral is a very famous cathedral in Moscow, Russia. It is in the area called Red Square. The cathedral is very magnificent. it has 8 onion shaped towers all of great importance. there is a ninth tower in the middle forming a eight point star.
