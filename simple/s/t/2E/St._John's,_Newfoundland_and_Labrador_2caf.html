St. John's, Newfoundland and Labrador

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

St. John's Harbour, Newfoundland

St. John's is the capital of the Canadian province of Newfoundland and Labrador. It is also the largest city in the province, with 99,182 people living there in 2001.

Close to St. John's is Cape Spear, the easternmost point in North America.
