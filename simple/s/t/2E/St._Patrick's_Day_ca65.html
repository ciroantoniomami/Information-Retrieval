St. Patrick's Day

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Saint Patrick's Day is the feast day of Saint Patrick, the patron saint of Ireland. It is celebrated all over Ireland and everywhere in the world where Irish people or their descendants live. New York City has one of the biggest parades. It is a very Irish festival and it involves a lot of feasting and celebrations.
