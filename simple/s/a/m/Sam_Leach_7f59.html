Sam Leach

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Sam Leach was the manager and promoter of many English bands which came to be known as the Mersey Beat. Between 1961 and 1962 Sam organised 49 concerts for The Beatles. Brian Epstein then took over the job from Sam Leach.

Sam now promotes an entertainer from Australia named Vincent Ruello. Sam is also writing a movie script about his life with The Beatles and has published a book named Birth of The Beatles. He is also involved with The Last Memento Of The Beatles
