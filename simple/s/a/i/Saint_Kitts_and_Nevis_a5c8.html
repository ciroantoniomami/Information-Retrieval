Saint Kitts and Nevis

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Where Saint Kitts and Nevis is
St. Kitts and Nevis Map
St. Kitts and Nevis Flag

Saint Kitts and Nevis is a nation made up of two small islands in the Caribbean Sea. It is the smallest country in the Western Hemisphere. Basseterre, located in the main island, is the capital city.
