Saint Peter

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

A picture of Saint Peter

Saint Peter is one of the twelve apostles of Jesus. He is frequently talked about in the Bible.

It is unknown when Peter was born. But the date of his death is in about the year 64. He died on the cross in Rome. Some books say that Peter died head downwards.

Peter was married. The name of his wife is unknown. He is thought to be the first pope.
