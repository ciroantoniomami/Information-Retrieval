Super Smash Bros. Melee

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Super Smash Bros. Melee (SSBM) is a video game where players can choose from many people from the previous Nintendo games and have them fight each other. Players can fight alone or team up with someone else against a friend. This game is a sequel to Super Smash Bros. for the Nintendo 64. SSBM is for the Nintendo GameCube.


[edit] Characters

The characters that can be choosen are:

Mario Luigi Bowser Princess Peach Ganandorf
Falco Fox Ness Ice Climbers Captain Falcon
Kirby Samus Zelda/Shiek Link Donkey Kong
Young Link Pichu Pikachu Jigglypuff Yoshi
Mewtwo Mr. Game and Watch Marth Roy Dr. Mario

[edit] Items

Items from different games can fall from the sky. These items can be used in various (different) ways. Here are some of them:

  • Mushroom
  • Warpstar
  • Pokeball
  • Lip's Stick
  • Metal Box
  • Egg

Those are just some of the items players can get. There are many more.

[edit] Battle Arenas

Players can battle in different arenas. Here are the arenas that can be choosen from:

Icicle Mountain Flat Zone Peach's Castle Rainbow Cruise Final Destination
Mushroom Kingdom Sucom Pokemon Stadium Pokefloats Kongo's Jungle
Jungle Japes Past Kongo's Jungle Yoshi's Island Yoshi's Story Past Yoshi's Story
Fountain of Dreams Green Greens Past Whispy Woods Hyrule Temple Great Bay of Termina
Corneria Venom Brinstar Brinstar Depths Onett
Fourside Mute City Big Blue Battlefield  

Some arenas and characters players need to unlock by playing the game.
