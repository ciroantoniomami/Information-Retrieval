Summer Glau (born July 24, 1981 in San Antonio, Texas) is an American dancer and actress. She best known for playing as River Tam in the science fiction television series Firefly and movie Serenity.

She has appeared in several other series and movies including:

Television
  • Angel
  • The 4400
  • The Unit
  • CSI: Crime Scene Investigation
Movies
  • Sleepover (film)
  • Mammoth (film)
