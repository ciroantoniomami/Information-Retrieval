Solar eclipse

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Photo taken during the 1999 eclipse.

A solar eclipse happens when the Moon passes between Earth and the Sun. It will then block the rays f the sun. This can only happen during a New Moon.

A total eclipse is when all the rays are blocked, if only some rays are blocked (dark moon part in the sun), the eclipse is called partial
