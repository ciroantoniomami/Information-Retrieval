Solomon

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

the Queen of Sheba Meeting with Solomon

Solomon was a King of Israel. He is written about in the Bible, and he is the author of several Old Testament books. He wrote Ecclesistes, Song of Songs and much of Proverbs.
