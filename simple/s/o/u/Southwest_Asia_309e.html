Southwest Asia

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Southwest Asia or Western Asia is the part of Asia that includes the Middle East.

[edit] Territories

Southwest Asia is in most uses meant to include:

  • Armenia
  • Bahrain
  • Cyprus
  • Sinai (eastern Egypt)
  • Gaza Strip
  • Iran
  • Iraq
  • Israel
  • Jordan
  • Kuwait
  • Lebanon
  • Oman
  • Pakistan (Balochistan only)
  • Qatar
  • Saudi Arabia
  • Syria
  • United Arab Emirates
  • West Bank
  • Yemen
  • The Asian portions of
    • Azerbaijan
    • Georgia
    • Turkey (Asia Minor or Anatolia)

In addition, it is sometimes widened to include:

  • Afghanistan
  • The African portion of Egypt
  • The European portions of
    • Azerbaijan
    • Georgia
    • Turkey (Eastern Thrace or Rumelia)
  • Adjacent parts of Central Asia (Turkmenistan, Uzbekistan, Tajikistan)
