Southeast Asia

Southeast Asia or Southeastern Asia is the southeastern part of Asia. These are the countries in Southeast Asia:

  • Brunei
  • Myanmar (formerly Burma)
  • Cambodia
  • East Timor
  • Indonesia
  • Lao PDR (Laos)
  • Malaysia
  • Philippines
  • Singapore
  • Thailand (formerly Siam)
  • Vietnam
