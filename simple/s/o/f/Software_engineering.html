Software engineering

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Software engineering is writing programs for computers. A software engineer is often someone who has trained to do this at a college or university. Software engineering is very difficult work. Most often it is done by a team of people. It often takes the team a long time to make the programs that they are asked to write.
