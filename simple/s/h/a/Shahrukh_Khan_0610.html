Shahrukh Khan

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Shahrukh Khan (born November 2, 1965) is a Bollywood Indian actor.

He has won many awards. He is Muslim and studied in a Muslim school. He is married to Guari and has two children; Aryan his son and Suhana his daughter. He also has a sister named Shehnaz Lalarukh. He has a master's degree. He is one of the biggest stars in "Bollywood".
