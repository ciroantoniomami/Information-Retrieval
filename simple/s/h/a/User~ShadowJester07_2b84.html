[edit] Into the Brain of a Mad Man

I was born in Evanston, Illinois, and raised in Chicago Illinois. Currently, I am attending Loyola Academy, where I am studying to pursue a career in either Aerospace or Bio-Chemical engineering. Similar to many of my senior peers, I currently do not know which college I will attend in the near future.

In my spare time, I enjoy playing golf and rugby. I used to participate in "school oriented" sports, such as, Hockey, Baseball, Basketball, Football, and Wrestling, but I retired after to an succumbing to an impetuous injury. Other than that, I often spend significant portions of time playing video games, browsing the Internet, and concocting poignant arrangements of music.

I was given the nickname, Joe Kick Ass, by a former friend after a stellar performance during an over-hyped game. Although the nickname would become archaic after I left the sports scene, it was rekindled after I spearheaded my high school to at a championship title at local competition. Shadow Jester is a name I thought up of to best describe my character; flippant, comical, and possibly a mere juxtaposition of miscellaneous particles.

[edit] Music

At the age of seven, my parents forced me to take up the saxophone. Although I have been playing for many years, it was only within the past five years that my musical prowess has really taken form. I managed to expand my dexterity to many other instruments, most notably the guitar. I'm currently attempting to master the art of composing music, by utilizing various MIDI-maker programs. Beyond that, I enjoy listening to all sorts of music, especially Classic Rock, Psychedelic Rock, Hard Rock, Rap, Jazz, and Classical.
