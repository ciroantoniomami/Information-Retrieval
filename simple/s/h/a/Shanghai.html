Shanghai

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Shanghai is the biggest city in China. It is on the banks of the Yangtze River.

Shanghai was not a major city until the 1800s, when it became a major trading port with western countries. Plus it has lots of celibration during the chinese new year. Budism takes its role in Shanghai.
