Shaka Zulu

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The only known drawing of Shaka.

Shaka Zulu (sometimes spelled Tshaka, Tchaka or Chaka; born around 1781 – died around September 22 1828) was a chief of the Zulu tribe in what is now South Africa. He made the Zulu a very strong nation.
