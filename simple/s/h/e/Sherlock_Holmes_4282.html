Sherlock Holmes

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Sherlock Holmes painted by the artist Sidney Paget, in The Strand magazine.

Sherlock Holmes is a character from books made by Sir Arthur Conan Doyle. Many of the stories were made into films and television dramas.

Sherlock Holmes is a detective (He tries to find out who did a crime).
