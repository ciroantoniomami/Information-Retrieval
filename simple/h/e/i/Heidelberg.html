Heidelberg

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Heidelberg in Germany
Heidelberg. View of city, bridge and castle

Heidelberg is a city on the Neckar River in the south-west of Germany, in the state of Baden-Württemberg. It has about 145,000 inhabitants and a well-known university. The Oldtown and the castle of Heidelberg are well-known tourist attractions of Germany.
