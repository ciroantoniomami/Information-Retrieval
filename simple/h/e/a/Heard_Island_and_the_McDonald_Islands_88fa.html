Heard Island and the McDonald Islands

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Heard Island and the McDonald Islands are uninhabited, barren islands located in the Indian Ocean, between Madagascar and Antarctica. The islands were discovered in 1833 by the British sailor Peter Kemp. They have been part of Australia since 1947.
