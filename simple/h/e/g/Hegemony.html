Hegemony

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Hegemony (pronounced he'gem.ə.ni or hə'dʒɛ.mə.ni) is the dominance of one group over other groups that, for instance, the dominant party can dictate the terms of trade to its advantage.
