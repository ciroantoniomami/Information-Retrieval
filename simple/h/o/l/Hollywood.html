Hollywood

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Hollywood sign

Hollywood is a part of Los Angeles, California, USA. It is famous around the world as a place where movies and television shows are made. It has many different attractions such as the Hollywood Walk of Fame and is the home to many celebrities. It has many different attractions to visit such as Hollywood walk of fame, Universal studios and the famous Hollywood sign. This attracts a lot of tourists to the area. The walk of fame was created in 1958 and the first star was placed in 1960 for artists working in the entertainment industry. It's a sidewalk along the Hollywood Boulevard, Los Angeles, California and the USA. There are more than 2000 five-points stars on the walk featuring the names of celebrities.
