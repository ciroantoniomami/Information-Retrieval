Hobart

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Hobart from the Eastern Shore

Hobart is the capital city of Tasmania, a state of Australia. It is located along the banks of the Derwent River and at the base of Mount Wellington. It has a population of approximately 225,000.
