Honda

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Honda is a large company from Japan. It is well known for making cars and motorcycles. Although Honda started in Japan, it now has many factories in other countries like Canada, Brazil, Thailand and China.

Honda makes lots of cars, but Toyota is a much larger company. Honda is known for being more experimental or "wild" than Toyota. Toyota is known for being more conservative or "boring". For example, Honda is even working on making a new airplane with a lot of new technology, and also on cars that work from fuel cells instead of traditional gas.

Some of the cars Honda makes are:

  • Honda Accord
  • Honda Ridgeline truck
  • Honda Civic, a traditional old-fashioned motor or hybrid motor
  • Honda Insight, uses a hybrid motor
  • Honda Fit (this car is called Honda Jazz in some parts of the world)
  • Honda CRV SUV
  • Honda Odyssey Minivan

Some of the cars Honda has made in the past are:

  • Honda Prelude, a sport coupe
  • Honda S500

Honda also makes a lot of motorcycles and ATVs. Honda is also known for participating in a very wide range of motorsport events, including Formula 1, MotoGP, LeMans, IRL and others.
