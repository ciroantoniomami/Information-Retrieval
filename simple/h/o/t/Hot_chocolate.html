Hot chocolate

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Hot chocolate with a marshmallow

Hot chocolate (which is also called hot cocoa, or drinking chocolate) is a kind of drink. Usually it is served hot. It is made from milk, chocolate or cocoa powder. Sometimes sugar is added to it. It became popular in Europe after chocolate was introduced from the New World
