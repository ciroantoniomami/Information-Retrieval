Homosexuals in Nazi Germany

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Nazi Germany leader Adolf Hitler persecuted (treated badly using "anti-homosexual" laws) homosexuals almost as much as he did Jews. He later also sent them to death camps.
