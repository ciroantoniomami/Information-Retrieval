Horatio Gates

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Horation Gates (1726 - 1806) was an American general in the American Revolutionary War. Gates fought for the Continental Army and is credited with the decisive American victory at the Battle of Saratoga in 1777. However, Gates was also responsible for the loss to British General Charles Cornwallis at the Battle of Camden in 1780.
