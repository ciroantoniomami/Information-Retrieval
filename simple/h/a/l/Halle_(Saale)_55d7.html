Halle (Saale)

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Paulus Church in Halle

Halle (also called Halle an der Saale) is the largest town in the German Bundesland of Saxony-Anhalt. It lies in the southern part of the state, on the river Saale. Halle is a German city in the state of Saxony-Anhalt. It has about 234,000 inhabitants (2006).
