Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows will be the seventh and last book in J. K. Rowling's Harry Potter series. On 1 February 2007, it was said that the book will be released in English-speaking countries around the world at 00:01 on 21 July 2007. In the United States, the book will be released at 00:01 local time.
