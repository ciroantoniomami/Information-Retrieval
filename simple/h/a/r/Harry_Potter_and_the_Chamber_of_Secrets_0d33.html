Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets by J. K. Rowling is the second novel (published in 1998) in the Harry Potter series of books. It takes place during Harry's second year at the Hogwarts School; Harry and his friends Ron Weasley and Hermione Granger combat the evil Lord Voldemort who has taken Ron's sister Ginny captive in the Chamber of Secrets and has frightened the school. To reach her, Harry, Ron and Hermione face series of challenges, ending with Harry saving Ginny. The movie verison was released in 2002.
