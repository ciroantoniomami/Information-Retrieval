Harry Potter and the Philosopher's Stone

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Harry Potter and the Philosopher's Stone is the first book in the Harry Potter series by English author J.K. Rowlings. It was published in 1997 and a movie version was released in 2001. In the United States the book and movie are called Harry Potter and the Sorcerer's Stone.

[edit] Plot

Spoiler warning: The text below is about parts of the story or how it ends.

The story is about a boy named Harry Potter, who finds out that he is a wizard. He learns out that his parents were killed by a evil wizard when he was a baby, and that he was almost killed with them. Harry goes to Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry to learn to become a wizard. He makes friends there. He finds out that Voldemort wants to come back by using a magical stone called the Philosopher's Stone. He and his friends go on a quest to destroy the stone and stop Voldemort from returning.
