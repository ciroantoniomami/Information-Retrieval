Hawaiian Islands

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

From west to east, Hawaii is made up of the following Hawaiian Islands:

  • Niihau
  • Kauai
  • Oahu
  • Moloka'i
  • Lana'i
  • Kaho'olawe
  • Maui
  • Hawaii

Hawaii has been a state since 1959.
