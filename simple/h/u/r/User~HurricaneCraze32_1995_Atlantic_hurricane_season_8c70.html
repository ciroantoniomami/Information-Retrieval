First storm started: June 3, 1995
Last storm ended: November 3, 1995
Strongest storm: Hurricane Opal - 916 mbar, 155 mph winds
Number of storms: 31 (including 3 weak storms)
Major hurricanes (Cat. 3+) 7
Cost of damage: US$11.5 billion
People killed: 115

The 2005 Atlantic hurricane season was the time from June 1 to November 30, 2005 when hurricanes officially formed. Storms can form before and after these dates.
