2006 FIFA World Cup

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Map of competing countries and current standings

The 2006 FIFA World Cup was a football (soccer) sporting event that was held in Germany from June 9 to July 9, 2006. 32 teams took part from many countries. Italy won the trophy after beating France in the final on penalties.
