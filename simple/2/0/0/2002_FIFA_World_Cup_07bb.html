2002 FIFA World Cup

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Map of competing countries and current standings

The 2002 FIFA World Cup was a football (soccer) sporting event that was held in South Korea and Japan from May 31 to June 30, 2002. 32 teams took part from many countries. Brazil won the trophy after beating Germany in the final on penalties.


30 June 2002
20:00
Brazil 2–0 Germany International Stadium, Yokohama
Attendance: 69,029
Referee: Pierluigi Collina (Italy)
