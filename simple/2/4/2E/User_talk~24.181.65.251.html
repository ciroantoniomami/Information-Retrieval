[edit] A Goofy Movie

We can't use Roxanne, we can't use Robert 'Bobby' Zimmeruski, we can't use Stacey, we can't use Principal Mazur, and we can't use Jason Marsden. We like Dana Hill, Gosalyn Mallard, Honker Muddlefoot, Bubba the Caveduck, Dr. Beatrice Brute, Magica De Spell, and Poe De Spell.

[edit] Winnie the Pooh and a Day for Eeyore

We can't use Piglet and Tigger. We like Gosalyn Mallard, Honker Muddlefoot, Dr. Beatrice Brute, Bubba the Caveduck, Kessie, Maleficent, and Diablo.

[edit] DuckTales the Movie: Treasure of the Lost Lamp

This is title Darkwing Duck the Movie: The Lost Lamp for direct to video not DuckTales. We can't use Scrooge McDuck, Huey, Dewey and Louie, Webby Vanderquack, Bentina Beakley, Duckworth, Merlock, and Dijon. We like Darkwing Duck, Gosalyn Mallard, Honker Muddlefoot, Bubba the Caveduck, Tootsie the Triceratops, Bongo (Darkwing Duck), Congo (Darkwing Duck), Dr. Beatrice Brute, Magica De Spell, and Poe De Spell.

[edit] Mickey's Magical Christmas: Snowed in at the House of Mouse

We can't use episode of The Nutcracker and Mickey's Christmas Carol, we can't use characters of Ludwig Von Drake. We like episode of Mickey's Rival, The Pied Piper, Old King Cole, King Neptune, The China Shop, Peculiar Penguins, Lullaby Land, Birds in the Spring, Father Noah's Ark, Music Land, Three Little Pigs, The Big Bad Wolf, Santa's Workshop, and The Night Before Christmas, we like Gosalyn Mallard, Honker Muddlefoot, Dr. Beatrice Brute, Bubba the Caveduck, Magica De Spell, and Poe De Spell.

[edit] Mickey's House of Villains

This is only title Mickey's House of Gosalyn Mallard not Villains. We like characters & titles Gosalyn Mallard, Dr. Beatrice Brute, Honker Muddlefoot, Bubba the Caveduck, Tootsie the Triceratops, Magica De Spell, Poe De Spell, Mickey's Rival, Mickey's Rival Returns, Music Land, Donald On Ice, The Pied Piper, Father Noah's Ark, Santa's Workshop, and The Night Before Christmas.

We like Gosalyn Mallard, Honker Muddlefoot, Bubba the Caveduck, and Dr. Beatrice Brute.

[edit] The Lion King II: Simba's Pride

We can't use actor for Nathan Lane. We like actor and character for Quinton Flynn, Gosalyn Mallard, Honker Muddlefoot, Bubba the Caveduck, and Dr. Beatrice Brute.

[edit] The Lion King 1 1/2

We can't use Nathan Lane. We like Quinton Flynn, Gosalyn Mallard, Honker Muddlefoot, Bubba the Caveduck, and Dr. Beatrice Brute.

  • Fun With Music - We can't use Professor Owl and Ludwig Von Drake. We like Gosalyn Mallard, Honker Muddlefoot, Bubba the Caveduck, and Dr. Beatrice Brute.

This is title The Ten Caballeros. We like Daisy Duck, Gosalyn Mallard, Honker Muddlefoot, Bubba the Caveduck, Dr. Beatrice Brute, Magica De Spell, and Poe De Spell.

This is title Mickey, Minnie, and Mortimer's Judgement Day release in January 11, 1941. We like Mickey Mouse, Minnie Mouse, Mortimer Mouse, Donald Duck, Daisy Duck, Goofy, Gosalyn Mallard, Honker Muddlefoot, Dr. Beatrice Brute, Huey, Dewey and Louie, Webby Vanderquack, Bubba the Caveduck, Magica De Spell, and Poe De Spell.
