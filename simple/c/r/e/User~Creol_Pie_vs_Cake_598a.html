Pie vs. Cake is an on going battle at the website Red vs. Blue. It started on October 6, 2006 with a comic posted on the site featuring two of the staff members of Rooster Teeth Productions threatening bodily harm to each other over which was better: Pie or Cake[1]. The theme has been carried over in many other comics as well as many members of the site choosing sides in the battle. January 25 (also the start of Winter-Een-Mas) has been officially declared Pie Vs Cake day.


Gus likes cake. Geoff likes pie. Both pie and cake can have filling, but cake has frosting where as pie can have whipped cream. The battle evoled as other deserts have started their own factions within the staff and are likely to have similar factions amongst the users as well. Seemingly, Matt likes cookies, and Jason likes brownies. And for Burnie, he just happens to have an odd fetish for fish-carrot pancakes.
