Cheese

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

A cheese platter with many types of cheese.

Cheese is a solid food made from milk. It can be soft or firm. It is made by removing water from milk. People may store cheese in a refrigerator, to make sure it does not go bad.

Cheese is rich in proteins and fats. It also contains vitamin A , calcium and phosphorus.

[edit] How it is Made

Cheese is usually made using milk. Cheese makers usually cook the milk in a large vat and add salt and a substance from the stomach of young cows called rennet, which curdles (makes solid) the cheese. Some makers do not add rennet and curdle it in other ways. Other ingredients are added and the cheese is usually aged for a short time.

[edit] Some Types of Cheese

There are many different ways to classify cheeses. Some ways include:

  • How long the cheese was aged
  • The texture of the cheese (hard or soft)
  • How the cheese was made
  • What type of milk was used to make the cheese (what animal it came from)
  • How much fat is in the cheese

Different types of cheese include:

  • Cream cheese
  • Processed cheese
  • Cheese Curd
  • Blue cheese
  • Swiss cheese
  • Mozzarella
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Limburger cheese
  • Brie
  • Gouda
  • Cheddar
