Chester

Chester is a city in the north-west of England. It is almost 2000 years old and was started by Romans. A person from Chester is called a Cestrian.

Chester is the capital of the county Cheshire. It lies on the river Dee.

It is near to the country Wales, and is home to almost 120,000 people. It is just south of Liverpool.
