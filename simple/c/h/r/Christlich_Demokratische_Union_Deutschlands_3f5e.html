Christlich Demokratische Union Deutschlands

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The CDU house in Berlin

The Christlich Demokratische Union Deutschlands (CDU) (English: Christian democratic union of Germany (CDU)) is one of the two German people's parties. It understands itself as „Christian democratic, liberal and a conservative party of the center “.

The CDU places the acting Chancellor of the Federal Republic of Germany with its Federal leader Angela Merkel.
