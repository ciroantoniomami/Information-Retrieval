Chancellor of the Exchequer

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The Chancellor of the Exchequer in a political office in the United Kingdom. The Chancellor of the Exchequer, sometimes to shortened to The Chancellor, is responsible for Britain's money and economy. The Chancellor now is Gordon Brown.

Other well-known Chancellors of the Exchequer include Robert Peel and Winston Churchill.
