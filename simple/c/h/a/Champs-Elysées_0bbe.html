Champs-Elysées

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia


The Champs-Elysées is a big and very famous street in Paris.

It links the Arc de Triomphe and the Place de la Concorde. It is a very popular place: there are famous cinemas, bars, restaurants... It is here than the Tour de France ends.
