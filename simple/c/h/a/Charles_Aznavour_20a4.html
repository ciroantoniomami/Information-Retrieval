Charles Aznavour

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Charles Aznavour (born May 22, 1924) is an Armenian-French singer, songwriter and actor. Besides being one of France's most popular and long lasting singers, he is also one of the most well-known French singers in other countries. He has appeared in more than 60 movies, wrote more than 1000 songs (including 150 in English, 100 in Italian, 70 in Spanish, and 50 in German). He has sold over 100 million albums. Aznavour started his final tour in late 2006.
