Call of Duty

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Call of Duty is a first person shooter video game. It is about World War II. It was created by Activision for Gamecube, Xbox, Playstation, Playstation 2, and the PC. It is rated Teen for blood and violence. There is very little blood but the whole game is violent. There have been many games along these lines the earliest of which was Doom also the Medal of Honor series and Wolfenstien.

[edit] Controls

The controls are fairly simple

mouse Aim and shoot
R Reload
Q Lean left
W Move forward
S Move back
A Turn left
D Turn right
F Use
C Crouch
Ctrl Lay down
Spacebar To stand up or jump
1,2,3,4 Weapon selection
Tab Mission objectives
