Catherine of Medici

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Catherine of Medici (April 13, 1519 – January 5, 1589) was the wife of Henry II of France.

She was the daughter of Lorenzo II de' Medici and was born in Florence, Italy.

Catherine was married to Henry II of France when she was 14 years old. Their children were:

  • Francis II of France
  • Elizabeth of Valois
  • Charles IX of France
  • Henry III of France
  • Margaret of Valois

Catherine is buried in the Saint Denis Basilica.
