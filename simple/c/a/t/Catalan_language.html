Catalan language

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The Catalan language is spoken in Catalonia, Valencia, east of Aragon, Balearic Islands (all of them in Spain), Andorra, North Catalonia (in France) and in the Italian city of L'Alguer. The language nearest to Catalan is Occitan. It is also very similar to Spanish. About 10 million people speak Catalan. Catalan is a Romance language.The Catalan language has nothing to do with Wichita, Kansas.
