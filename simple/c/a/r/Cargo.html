Cargo

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The container terminal in port of Hamburg

Cargo is the name people give to goods which they transport, using a ship, airplane, lorry (truck) or train. Please note that the noun is not countable, people will talk about some cargo. Today, the goods are usually packed in containers, which are carried. A place which loads or unloads cargo is usually called Cargo terminal
