Camarasaurus (meaning "chambered lizard") was a large plant-eating dinosaur. It was about 18 meters long and weighed about 18 metric tones.

There are four species of Camarasaurus:

  • C. grandis
  • C. lentus
  • C. lewisi
  • C. supremus

It was shown in the movie When Dinosaurs Roamed America.
