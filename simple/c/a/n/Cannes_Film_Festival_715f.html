Cannes Film Festival

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The Cannes Film Festival is an event held every year in France and features films from around the world. Famous people come to show up and support their films. The best films win prizes, including the top prize, the Palm d'Or. Past winners of the Palm d'Or (along with the year they were made and the country they are from) are:
