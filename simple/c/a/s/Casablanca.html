Casablanca

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

For other uses, see Casablanca (disambiguation).
A view on the Boulevard de Paris in central Casablanca

Casablanca (classical Arabic name: الدار البيضاء, "the white house") is a city in western Morocco, located on the Atlantic Ocean.

With a population of 2.95 million (September 2004 census), Casablanca is Morocco's biggest city.
