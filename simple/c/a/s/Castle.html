Castle

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

A castle in Spain

A castle is a big building that was built to protect the king or lord and his family who lived there. They were built a long time ago, during the Middle Ages. They are very strong and some are still around today. Many castles are very beautiful and a lot of people visit them.

Most castles have towers and walls.

Some famous castles in Britain are:

  • Edinburgh Castle
  • Windsor Castle
  • Warwick Castle
  • Tower of London
