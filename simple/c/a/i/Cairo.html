Cairo

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

A map of Egpyt. Cairo is the light green spot.

Cairo is the capital and largest city in Egypt. It has a population of 15.2 million people who live there. It is located on the river Nile. The great pyramids of Giza are located on the outskirts of Cairo. It is located on the river Nile. The great pyramids of Giza are located on the outskirts of Cairo.
