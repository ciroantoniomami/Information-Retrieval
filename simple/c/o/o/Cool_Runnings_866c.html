Cool Runnings

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Cool Runnings can refer to:

  • A common Jamaican term similar to that of "peace out"
  • Cool Runnings is a movie released in 1993 by Disney, starring Leon, Doug E. Doug, and John Candy. Partly based on a true story about a Jamaican bobsled team who beats the odds to make it to the 1988 Winter Olympics, hence its title—a pun.
