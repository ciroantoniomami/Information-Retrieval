Contract bridge

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The word bridge is also used for a game.

Played with 52 cards all over the world.

The main competition is the Bermuda Bowl.

Italy and North America won it many times.

At the beginning of november 2004, we know the new world champion : Italy.

At the end of december 2004, we know the new (kind of technical ATP) world leader : Lorenzo Lauria (remembering about Giorgio Belladonna, the previous 1995 leader).
