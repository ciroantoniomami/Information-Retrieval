Constance of Arles

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Constance of Arles (973 - July 25, 1034) was the third wife of Robert II of France. They were married in 1003 after Robert had got a divorce from his second wife, Bertha.

They had several children:

  • Henry I of France
  • Adela of France - is the mother of Matilda of Flanders, who was the wife of William I of England
  • Robert I of Burgundy
  • Eudes of France
  • Constance of France

She was buried in the Saint Denis Basilica with Robert II of France.
