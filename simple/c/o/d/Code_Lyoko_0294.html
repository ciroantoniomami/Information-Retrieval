Code Lyoko

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Code Lyoko is a French animated televion series.

[edit] Story

Code Lyoko is about five teenagers who are juggling with their normal school life and a virtual world called Lyoko.

[edit] Main characters

Jeremie Belpois: Jeremie is a very clever boy. He is the best of his class, and even of the school, in all the subjects, except sports. He is also a computer genius. It is him who have reboot the supercalculator who contain Lyoko (see 3x: X.A.N.A. awakens). In the factory, he is the superviser : he virtualizes the Lyokowarriors and the vehicles, he says them the Life Points they still have and the position of the tours and the ennemis. We have never seen him on Lyoko, but he goes twice. First time, it failed and he was stuck in the supercalculator. The second time, we didn't see him, but according to Odd and Ulrich, his appearence was "ridiculous". He maybe went a third time, but it's not sure : in X.A.N.A. awakens, he should be scanned in order to remember the existence of Lyoko after a "back to the past", but we don't know if it was only scanned or completely virtualized.

Ulrich Stern: Ulrich is one of the Lyokowarriors. On Lyoko, he is a samurai, and has a katana. He can use Triplicata in order to create two clones of himself, and supersprint to run fastly. When he uses both in the same time, it's called Triangulaire. On Earth, he is very mysterious, and don't often speak about himself. He has a crush for Yumi, but they never admit it.

Yumi Ishiyama : Yumi is a Japanese girl. She's the one who don't live in the college. She has a crush for Ulrich. She often wears black clothes. She has a brother : Hiroshi.

Odd Della Robia : Odd is a funny boy. He wears purple clothes. He always thinks about food, and is very greedy.
