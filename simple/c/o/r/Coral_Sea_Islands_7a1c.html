Coral Sea Islands

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

A map of the islands

The Coral Sea Islands (or Coral Sea Islands Territory) is a group of islands on the Great Barrier Reef, in Australia. None of them are inhabited permanently, but some have automatic weather stations or lighthouses on them. They are located in the Coral Sea, northeast of Queensland.
