Cornwall

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

A map showing where Cornwall is in England (coloured red)
St Piran's Flag

Cornwall (Cornish: Kernow) is a county in Britain. Tintagel Castle is in Cornwall. Truro is the capital city of Cornwall.

Some people in Cornwall can speak Cornish, a very old Celtic language of Cornwall, which is related to Welsh. Some people in Cornwall consider themselves Cornish and not English.
