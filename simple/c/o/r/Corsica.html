Corsica

Corsica
The administrative region of Corse is in red

Corsica (French: Corse; Corsican: Corsica) is the fourth largest island in the Mediterranean Sea (after Sicily, Sardinia, and Cyprus). It is located west of Italy, southeast of France, and north of the island of Sardinia. 285.000 people live on Corsica. Its capital is Ajaccio.
