Cosmetics

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Various cosmetics

Cosmetics (also called makeup, make up, or make-up) are products used to make the human body look different. Often cosmetics are used to make someone more attractive to one person, or to a culture or sub-culture. All cosmetics are temporary. They need to be renewed after a certain time. Cosmetics include lipstick, powders, and lotions amongst other things.

In the Western culture, women are the main users of cosmetics. Their use by men is less frequent. Man, who is used cosmetics, often are called metrosexual.

Cosmetics are also widely use in the world of acting.
