Count Dooku

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Count Dooku of Serenno (also known as Darth Tyranus) is one of the major characters in Attack of the Clones. He is the second known student of Darth Sidious. Dooku is also the leader of the Confederacy of Independent Systems during the Clone Wars.
