Columbine High School massacre

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The Columbine High School Massacre was a suicide of two boys at the school. Before committing suicide, they killed about 15 people, seemingly at random. it is considered to be among the deadliest school massacres in the United States. It happened in 1999.
