Ciudad Real (this translates to 'Royal City, in English) is a province of Spain in the Autonomous Community of Castile-La Mancha. 497.691 inhabitants, 19.813 km² of surface. It has 100 municipalities. The provincial capital: Ciudad Real. Other important city: Puertollano.
