Cleveland, Ohio

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Cleveland is a city that is located in northern Ohio, USA. It is home to over four hundred thousand people. It was named for General Moses Cleaveland in 1796, but a mistake in a local newspaper dropped the only "a" in its name, hence the present spelling. Its metropolitan area makes it the urban division in the state.

Pro sports teams that make their home in or near Cleveland include the Cleveland Indians, who play baseball; the Cleveland Browns, who play football; and the Cleveland Cavaliers, who play basketball.

Cleveland is one of the important industrial centres of the United States.
