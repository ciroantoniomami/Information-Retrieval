Clark Gable

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

William Clark Gable (February 1, 1901 - November 16, 1960) was an American actor. He is best known for playing the part of Rhett Butler in the 1939 film version of Gone with the Wind. Gable also won an Academy Award for playing Fletcher Christian in the 1935 film version of Mutiny on the Bounty.

The last film that Gable starred in was The Misfits in 1961. This film also starred Marilyn Monroe and was written by Arthur Miller. He died shortly afterwards in Los Angeles, California, after having had a fourth heart attack.
