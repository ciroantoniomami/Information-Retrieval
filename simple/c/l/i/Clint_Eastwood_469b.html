Clint Eastwood

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Clint Eastwood in 2005

Clint Eastwood is an American movie actor and director. He was born in San Francisco on May 31 1930. He has appeared in many films since the 1950s, most famous being the Dollars Trilogy of Spaghetti Westerns (1964-1966), Dirty Harry (1972), Any Which Way You Can(1979) and other similar movies. He also won critical acclaim as a director with the movies Unforgiven (1992), Midnight in the Garden of Good and Evil (1996), Space Cowboys (2000), Mystic River (2003), and Million Dollar Baby (2005). He lives with his family in Carmel-by-the-Sea, California. He was the mayor of Carmel-by-the-Sea for two years, starting in 1986.
