Céline Dion

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Dion singing "God Bless America"

Céline Dion (March 30, 1968-) is a popular French-Canadian pop singer. She was born in the town of Charlemagne in Quebec and is the youngest of 14 children. When she was 12, her brother sent a recording to a manager named René Angélil, whom she later married. With his help, she released her first album in 1981, and her first English album in 1990.
