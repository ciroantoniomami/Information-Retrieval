Ceará is one of the 26 states of Brazil. It is located in the northeastern part of the country, on the Atlantic coast. Ceará has many beaches which brings it a lot of tourists.

[edit] Geography

Ceará has an area of 148,016 km². It shares borders on the east by the states of Rio Grande do Norte and Paraíba, on the south by Pernambuco state, and on the west by Piauí. Its northern border is the Atlantic Ocean.
