Central Intelligence Agency

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The Central Intelligence Agency (CIA) is a part of the United States government.

The CIA is made up of several groups that each do different things. Their goal is to protect the US people.

Many people feel that the CIA does more bad than good.

CIA has many clandestine, or secret, operations that people will never, ever know about.
