Jessica Simpson

Jessica Simpson (born July 10, 1980 in Abilene, Texas) is an American singer and actress. She was the star of the MTV reality TV show, Newlyweds (2005). She is the daughter of Tina and Joe Simpson and older sister of singer and actress Ashley Simpson. She was married to singer Nick Lachey from August 2002 until November 2005. Simpson grew up in Richardson, Texas.

As a singer, she has 7 Billboard Top 40 hits, 5 gold albums and 2 platinum albums.

She has starred in the films The Dukes of Hazzard (2005) and Employee of the Month (2006)

She has cosmetics line (product) called Dessert Treats and Dessert Beauty.
