Jane Seymour

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Jane Seymour (c. 1508 or 1509 – October 24, 1537) was the third wife of Henry VIII of England. Never had an official coronation, and was married to Henry for only a year and a half before she died (May 30th 1536-October 24th 1537). Jane was a very calm, gentle, modest person and a very submissive wife. She was the mother of Edward VI of England, Henry VIII's only male heir, and died eight days after giving birth to him. She should not to be confused with the contemporary actress of the same name.
