[edit] Please review everything on this page...

I suggest the whole page be reviewed. There is incorrect information there, for example:

"The Japanese changed Buddhism many ways, starting ideas such as Zen."

No, that is not correct. The "Zen" form of Buddhism started in China with the monk called Bodhidharma. Only later Zen spread to other countries, including Japan. "Zen" is the Japanese pronunciation of the Chinese word "Chan". And "Daruma" is the way Japanese people call Bodhidharma. --The lack of a reference to the Shinto-religion (wich was/is the major religion in Japan) is also quite disturbing.
