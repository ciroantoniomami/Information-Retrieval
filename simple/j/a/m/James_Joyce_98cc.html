James Joyce

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

A monument of James Joyce next to O'Connell Street, Dublin

James Augustine Aloysius Joyce (February 2, 1882 - January 13, 1941) was a writer and poet. He was from Ireland.

Some books that Joyce wrote:

  • Dubliners (1914)
  • A Portrait of the Artist as a Young Man (1916)
  • Ulysses (1922)
  • Finnegans Wake (1939).
