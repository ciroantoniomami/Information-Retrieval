Jammu and Kashmir

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Jammu and Kashmir is a state in India. Jammu and Kashmir are a disptued region in the northern part of Pakistan and India. The two countries have been fighting over them in the past. Recently peace talks have been held but the issue is unresolved.
