John Candy

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

John Candy
Born October 31, 1950
Toronto, Ontario, Canada
Died March 4, 1994
Durango, Mexico

John Candy (October 31, 1950 – March 4, 1994) was a Canadian actor known for his comic roles in many films and TV shows including:

  • Camp Candy (TV Series)
  • SCTV (TV series)
  • 1941 (1979)
  • The Blues Brothers (1980)
  • Stripes (1981)
  • Heavy metal (1981)
  • National Lampoon's Vacation (1983)
  • Splash (1984)
  • Follow that Bird (1985)
  • Little Shop of Horrors (1986)
  • Spaceballs (1987)
  • Planes, Trains & Automobiles (1987)
  • The Great Outdoors (1988)
  • Uncle Buck (1989)
  • Who's Harry Crumb? (1989)
  • Home Alone (1990)
  • JFK (1991)
  • Cool Runnings (1993)
