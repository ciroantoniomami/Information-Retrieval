Joseph Goebbels

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Paul Joseph Goebbles (29 October 1897 – 1 May 1945) was a German politician and the minister of propaganda during the Nazi regime. He was a close friend of Adolf Hitler. Goebbles remained with Hitler in the Fuehrerbunker until Hitler's suicide on April 30 1945. Following Hitler's death Goebbles was chancellor for one day. During his final hours his wife, Magda Goebbles killed their six children. Shortly after Goebbles and his wife both committed suicide.
