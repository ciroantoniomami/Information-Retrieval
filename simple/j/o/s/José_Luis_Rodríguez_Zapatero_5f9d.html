José Luis Rodríguez Zapatero

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

José Luis Rodríguez Zapatero listen ? (born August 4, 1960 in Valladolid) is the President of the Government of Spain. His party, the Spanish Socialist Workers' Party (PSOE), won the general election on March 14, 2004. He succeded to Jose Maria Aznar. Notable actions of his government have included withdrawing Spanish troops from Iraq and legalizing same-sex marriages.
