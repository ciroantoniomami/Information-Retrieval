Josef Mengele

Dr Josef Mengele (March 16 1911 – February 7, 1979), was a German SS officer and a physician in the Nazi concentration camp Auschwitz. He did medical experiments on the people in the camps. Mengele was known as the Angel of Death. After war, he first hid in Germany, then escaped and lived in South America until drowning in Brazil.
