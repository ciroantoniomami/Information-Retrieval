Joey (TV series)

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Joey was a sitcom (a comedy set in a situation) starring Matt LeBlanc as Joey Tribbiani, his character from Friends. It consists of Joey, his sister, and his nephew.

[edit] Cast

  • Matt LeBlanc as Joey Tribbiani
  • Drea de Matteo as Gina Tribbiani (Joey's sister)
  • Paulo Costanzo as Michael Tribbiani (Joey's brother)
  • Jennifer Coolidge as Bobbie Morganstern
  • Andrea Anders as Alex Garrett
