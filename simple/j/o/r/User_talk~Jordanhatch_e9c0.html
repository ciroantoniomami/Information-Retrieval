[edit] Welcome!

First of all, take a look around. It takes time to get used to how things are done here, it's a bit different to EN Wikipedia. When you feel confident about writing Simple English, this list has some articles we really need writing or improving, as well as Wikipedia:Requested articles. Any questions you have, you can ask me or go to Wikipedia:Simple talk.

One thing you should be wary of is the amount of vandalism. Because we're a small community, the best contributors really get targeted and sometimes nastily attacked. I personally find it quite funny, but you might not, and putting 'do not vandalise' is pretty much suicide over here. I'm an admin if you need anything. Archer7 - talk 18:23, 16 July 2006 (UTC)

[edit] Admin

Well yes I suppose, but it's like that on the English Wikipedia too. The current guidelines here are 1000 edits and 3 months experience, but every case is assessed individually. If you have proved that you can be trusted and spend far too much of your spare time here, you can become an admin. However, being an admin is nothing special. OK, so you get to block people and get your user page replaced with offensive pictures occasionally, but that shouldn't be your only aim while editing here. I know it's easy to think that admins are somewhat 'higher' than everyone else, but they're not. It really isn't that big a deal. Archer7 - talk 18:43, 16 July 2006 (UTC)

That's another thing. We really don't like things that aren't that important or notable at the moment, something like Leyland would be on WP:RFD in less than an hour. User:XenoNeon doesn't edit over here very much at all, I'm sure he'd be very jealous. Archer7 - talk 19:00, 16 July 2006 (UTC)
