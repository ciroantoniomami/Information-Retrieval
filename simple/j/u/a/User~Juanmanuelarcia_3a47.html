User:Juanmanuelarcia

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Juan Manuel Arcia Salazar is a teacher of English language, (a person who gives other persons knowledge of English language). He does writings in Spanish, English, German, French, Portuguese and Simple English for Wikipedia. Juan Manuel Arcia Salazar is from Maturín, a long town of Venezuela. He went to Universidad Pedagógica Experimental Libertador, a school where future teachers get a learning. Juan Manuel Arcia Salazar is still in Maturín.
