Xbox

Xbox is Microsoft's first video game console, which came out after the Sony's PlayStation 2. It was also more powerful than the Playstation 2. The controller has been changed (the new one is shown in the picture) because it was too hard to use. The Xbox can play many different games on a special disk. The games can be anything from shooting games to racing games. The Xbox graphics are 3D. It can also play DVDs and audio CDs. Players also have the ability to play others over the Internet using a service called Xbox Live.

The next Xbox, called the Xbox 360, came out in November of 2005. The Xbox 360 is one of the newest generation consoles and competes with the Sony PS3 and Nintendo Wii
