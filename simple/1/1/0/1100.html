1100

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Centuries: 10th century - 11th century - 12th century

Decades: 1050s 1060s 1070s 1080s 1090s - 1100s - 1110s 1120s 1130s 1140s 1150s

Years: 1095 1096 1097 1098 1099 - 1100 - 1101 1102 1103 1104 1105

Note that 1100 is actually the last year of the 11th century; 1101 is the actual first year of the 12th century.


[edit] Events

  • August 5 - Henry I crowned King of England in Westminster Abbey

[edit] Births

  • May 23 - Emperor Qinzong of China (d. 1161)
  • Mary and Eliza Chulkhurst, English conjoined twins (died 1134)
  • Gilbert de Clare, 1st Earl of Pembroke (died 1148)

[edit] Deaths

  • August 2 - William II of England
