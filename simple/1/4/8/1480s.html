1480s

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Centuries: 14th century - 15th century - 16th century

Decades: 1430s 1440s 1450s 1460s 1470s - 1480s - 1490s 1500s 1510s 1520s 1530s

Years: 1480 1481 1482 1483 1484 1485 1486 1487 1488 1489

[edit] Events

  • War of the Roses in England.
  • Bartolomeu Dias of Portugal sails around Africa and on to India.
  • Spanish Inquistion begins.


[edit] Births

  • Martin Luther, German monk and reformer
  • Rafael, Italian painter and architect
  • Huldrych Zwingli, Swiss religious reformer
  • Catherine of Aragon, queen of Henry VIII of England


[edit] Deaths

  • Mehmed II, Ottoman Sultan
  • King Christian I of Denmark and Norway
  • Axayacatl, Aztec ruler of Tenochtitlan
