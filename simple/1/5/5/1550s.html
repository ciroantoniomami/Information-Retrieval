1550s

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Centuries: 15th century - 16th century - 17th century
Decades: 1520s 1530s 1540s - 1550s - 1560s 1570s 1580s
Years: 1500 1501 1502 1503 1504 1505 1506 1507 1508 1509

[edit] Events

  • Helsinki founded in Finland.
  • Rule of Bloody Mary, or Mary I, in England.
  • São Paulo founded in Brazil.
  • Religious wars continue in Europe.
  • Russia breaks peace with Sweden by attacking Finland.
  • The Shaanxi Earthquake, the deadliest earthquake in history.

[edit] Births

  • Sarsa Dengel, Emperor of Ethiopia
  • King Sebastian of Portugal
  • Nurhaci, Chinese founder of the Qing Dynasty


[edit] Deaths

  • Tabinshwehti, King of Burma
  • Edward VI, King of England
  • Ignatius of Loyola, Spanish founder of the Jesuit priests
