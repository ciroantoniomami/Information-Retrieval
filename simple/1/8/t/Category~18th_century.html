Category:18th century

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

This category is for articles and events specifically related to the 18th century, which began in the year 1701 and ended in the year 1800.

Note that the year 1800 is included in Category:1800s in the 19th century but is actually part of the 18th century.
