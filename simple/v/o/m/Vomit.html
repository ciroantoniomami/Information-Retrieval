Vomit

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Vomiting is when food or liquid in your stomach comes up and out of your mouth. It can be from infection. It can be from the brain being hurt. It can be from a block in the small intestines so food and liquid cannot go through. It can be from too much alcohol or other drugs. Also, people can make themselves vomit. If they do this when they are sad and not when they are sick it is called bulimia.
