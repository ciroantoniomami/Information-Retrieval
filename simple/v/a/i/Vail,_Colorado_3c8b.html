Vail, Colorado

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Ski area at Vail

Vail is a town in the American state of Colorado. It is best known for being one of the best places to ski in North America. The Vail Ski Resort is the largest ski resort with only one mountain in North American. In 2006, it was beleived that 4,859 people lived in Vail.[1]
