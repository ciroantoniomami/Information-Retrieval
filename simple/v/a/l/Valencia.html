Valencia

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Valencia (red dot) on a map of Spain

Valencia is a city in Spain, on the Mediterranean coast. It is the capital of the Community of Valencia. It has 807,396 people, making it the third-largest city in Spain.
