Wikipedia:Vandalism in progress

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Shortcut:
WP:VIP

Vandals, on Wikipedia, are people who ruin pages on purpose. If you see a vandal, list them here!

People who vandalise a lot can be stopped from editing. In the case of large levels of vandalism (like a vandal bot), please tell a developer.

You can remove vandalism by reverting a page. A lot of vandals will stop by simply putting a message warning them on their talk page. If it is just a user test, just putting {{test}} or {{subst:test}} on their talk page will do. You can also use {{stopvandal}} if they are vandalising and haven't stopped after using {{test}}. More...

To better assist administators, please use the {{vandal}} template on this page:

Example:
  • {{vandal|bad user}} bad_user (talk • contribs • page moves • block user • block log)

New vandals are at the top
