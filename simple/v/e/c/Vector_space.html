Vector space

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

A Vector space is a concept from Mathematics. In a vector space, there are objects. These objects are called vectors. Two operations are defined: Addition of two vectors and Multiplication of a vector with a scalar ( Multiplication results in scaling).
