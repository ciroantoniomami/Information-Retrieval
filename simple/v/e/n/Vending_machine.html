Vending machine

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

A Vending machine is a machine that sells things. Those things are usually food, drinks, or sometimes other useful items like condoms, newspapers or cigarettes. The machines usually work, when some money (usually coins) is put in. Then, a button needs to be pushed, or a lever pulled. If there is enough money in the machine, the selected item will be dropped to a tray, where it can be taken out.
