Vehicle

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

An automobile is a type of vehicle.

A vehicle is a form of transport to move persons from one place to another. It is not alive; a horse can bring a person but it is not a vehicle. A vehicle carries things too.

Some vehicles may be petrol or diesel driven with a steering wheel. Usually on four wheels but some three wheeled vehicles do exist. Some vehicles are powered by humans like bicycles. Some vehicles are pulled by animals like chariots. Some vehicles can fly.
