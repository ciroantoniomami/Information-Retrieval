Victoria

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The word Victoria is a woman's name. In Wikipedia, it can mean:

  • Victoria, Australia is a state in Australia
  • Victoria, British Columbia is a city in British Columbia, Canada
  • Victoria, Seychelles is the capital of Seychelles
  • Victoria of the United Kingdom was a Queen
  • Roman version of the Greek goddess Nike
