Viridian

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

This box shows the colour viridian.
 

Viridian is a blue-green colour. The name comes the Latin word viridis which means "green". Viridian is a popular colour for painting dining rooms. Viridian is the name of a city in the English translation of Pokémon Red and Blue.
