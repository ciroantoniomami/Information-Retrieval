Virgil

Publius Vergilius Maro, also called Virgil by English-speaking people, (October 15, 70 BC - September 21, 19 BC) was a writer of poems in the Latin language. Virgil's most famous epic poem is called Aeneid. His poems are about gods and myths (called mythology).
