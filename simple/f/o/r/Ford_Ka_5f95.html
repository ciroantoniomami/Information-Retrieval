Ford Ka

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Ford Ka Kool
Exterior dimensions

The Ford Ka is a very small car made by the Ford Motor Company in Europe and Brazil. It has three doors and was first made in 1996. The Ka is very popular in Europe.

[edit] Engine data

4 cylinders in line; SOHC; 8 valves; electronic multipoint fuel injection

  • 1.0l Duratec (Brazil)

48 kw (65 PS) at 6.000 rpm 87 Nm at 3.250 rpm

  • 1.3l Duratec (1297 cc-Europe)

51 kW (70 PS) at 5500 rpm 106 Nm at 3000 rpm

  • 1.6l Duratec (1597 cc-Europe and Brazil)

70 kW (95 PS) at 5500 rpm 135 Nm at 4250 rpm
