Fark

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Fark is a comedy website and forums. Parts of it are free and parts of it cost money to join.

Fark was started by Drew Curtis 1999 as a weblog where users post current news articles and other information. Then other users make comments on these articles.
