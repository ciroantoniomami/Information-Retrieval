Fairy Tail is a shônen manga published in the Weekly Shônen Jump. It's created by Hiro Mashima.

[edit] Story

In the magic world, some people can use magic. They're called "mages". Lucy is a mage who can summon creatures thanks to special keys called "Gate no Kagi". In the town of Harujion, Lucy is looking for a magic store, but this is a little town where only some people can use magic, so there is only one. After having bought a key, she heard that a known mage, called Salamender is in the town. She approach the group which is around him and feels strange. Her heart is beating fast. It stops when a boy knock into her. His name is Natsu, and with is cat Happy, he tryes to find a dragoon, called Igneel. Lucy invite them in a eating place because they helped her to escape from the magic of Salamender, called "Charm" and who attract all the girls. She explains them that she is a mage, and that she wants to join Fairy Tail, one of the most popular guild in the world. The guilds are associations of mages. Later, she meet again Salamender, who want to invite her to his party. She doesn't want but he says that if she comes on the party, he will try to help her to enter in Fairy Tail, because he is actually into. She accepts. But in fact, the ship where the party takes place is for slaves. Natsu arrives in the ship but feel sick because he dislikes all sort of transportation. Lucy, who is out of the ship thanks to Happy, use a key to bring the ship back to the port. Without the waves, Natsu feels better and we discovers that he's also a mage, using fire magic. In fact, he is the real Salamender, a mage of Fairy Tail, and the other is Bora. Natsu win the fight, doing big damages to the port. The guard arrives and Natsu has to leave the town, but he brings Lucy with him. When she asks where they go, he simply says "You want to join Fairy Tail, right ? Come with me."

That's the beginning of their adventures in the famous guild of Fair Tail, with the strange but very cool mages.

[edit] Charcaters

Natsu aka Salamender : Natsu is a fire mage, he can eat fire, use fire and he resists to fire. That is why he is called "Salamender".
