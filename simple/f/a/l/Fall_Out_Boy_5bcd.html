Fall Out Boy

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Fall Out Boy is a popular band (as of 2005) from Chicago, Illinois, USA. They have a few successful songs, including "Sugar, We're Goin' Down" and "Dance, Dance". They belong to the pop-punk style, or genre, of music.

"Sugar, We're Goin' Down" is the first song on their album From Under the Cork Tree. It has peaked at number eight on the Billboard charts.
