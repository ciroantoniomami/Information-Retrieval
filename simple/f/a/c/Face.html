Face

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Two girls with painted faces

The face is the front part of the head. It contains the eyes, the nose, and the mouth.

Wikimedia Commons has media related to:
Faces
  • A face can also be the flat surface of a pyramid, cube, or other solid shape.
  • Face can also be an action. When you face something, you turn yourself or place yourself so that your face is pointed at it.
