Family Guy

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Family Guy is an animated comedy television series created by Seth MacFarlane. It originally aired on FOX, but did not become popular until reruns were shown on Cartoon Network and TBS, and episodes were sold on DVDs. Fox chose to cancel the show because it was not very popular, but after lots of DVDs were sold, Fox renewed it.

The show is about the lives of Peter Griffin; his wife, Lois; their three children, 16-year old Meg, 13-year old Chris, and genius baby Stewie; and their smart dog, Brian (who can talk and acts like a human). The Griffins live in Quahog, Rhode Island. The show puts humor into common everyday situations and is famous for their "flashback" style of comedy.

Peter Griffin works at a Toy Factory in Quahog, the Happy Go Lucky Toy Factory. Lois works as a piano teacher. Brian never finished university, Meg is not liked by many people because of her looks, Chris likes drawing, and Stewie thinks of ways to take over the world and kill his mother, who he does not like.
