Flour

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Flour is grains that have been dried and ground into powder. Flour is used to make many foods.

Foods that are made from flour:

  • Bread
  • Pasta
  • Cookies
  • Cakes
  • Tortillas
