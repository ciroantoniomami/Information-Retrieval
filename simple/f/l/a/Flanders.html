Flanders

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The territory of Flanders

Flanders (Dutch: Vlaanderen) is a region in the north of Belgium. The official language is Dutch, sometimes called Flemish. Flanders has 5 provinces. About 6.0 million people live there.
