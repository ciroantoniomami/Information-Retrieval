Fenerbahce

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Fenerbahçe is a sports club in Istanbul, Turkey. It was officially founded in 1907. Fenerbahce's home stadium is the Fenerbahce Sukru Saracoglu Stadium in Kadikoy Istanbul. They have won the Turkish Premier Super Football League 16 times and the Turkey Football Cup 4 times.

Fenerbahce is one of the single strong clubs in Turkey. The other clubs are Galatasaray and Besiktas JK.
