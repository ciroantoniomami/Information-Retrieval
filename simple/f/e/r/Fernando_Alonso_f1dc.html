Fernando Alonso

Fernando Alonso (born 1981) is a race car driver in Formula One. He is from Spain. His height is 171 cm. He won first F1 title in 2005.

Alonso drives in the formula 1 for the Renault F1-Team, with which he achieved the driver world championship in the year 2005 and had also a large portion of the victory of the technical designer World cup for his team. He is the youngest and at the same time the first Spanish Formula-1-Worldchampion of the past running history.
