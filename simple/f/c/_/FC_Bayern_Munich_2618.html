FC Bayern Munich

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

FC Bayern Munich, also known as Bayern München, is a well-known German football club. The club was founded in 1900 and has 104.000 paying members.

The footballteam is playing in the Allianz Arena.
