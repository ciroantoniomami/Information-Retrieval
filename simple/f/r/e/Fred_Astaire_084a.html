Fred Astaire

Fred Astaire in 1936

Fred Astaire, born Frederick Austerlitz (May 10, 1899 – June 22, 1987), was an American actor and dancer.

[edit] Trivia

  • He is one the famous people whom appears on the cover of The Beatles, Sgt. Pepper's Lonely Hearts Club Band album.
