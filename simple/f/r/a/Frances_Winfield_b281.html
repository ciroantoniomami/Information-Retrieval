Frances Winfield

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Frances Winfield is the wife of former administrator of Iraq L. Paul Bremer III and mother of their two children. She is also an author, teacher, and artist. Some of Mrs. Bremer's published books include Running to Paradise (published November 2000, ISBN 1892668246) and Coping With His Success: A Survival Guide for Wives at the Top (published March 1984, ISBN 0060152478).

A New Hampshire native, Mrs. Bremer met her husband at a Dixieland concert. The two became college sweethearts and married in 1966. A devout Roman Catholic, she and her husband, Paul Bremer, are Eucharistic ministers at their local parish in Maryland.
