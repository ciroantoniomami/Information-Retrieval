Frankfurt International Airport

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The railway station for the long-distance trains

The Frankfurt International Airport (in german: Rhein-Main-Flughafen) is the biggest airport in Germany and the third largest in Europe (in terms of Passenger, in Cargo second largest), it is located in Frankfurt, Hesse.

In 2005, 52 million passengers flew from Frankfurt and 1,89 million tonnes of cargo.

The airport has two train stations, the terminal for the commuter trains is under the terminal and the station for the long-distance trains is five minutes to walk.
