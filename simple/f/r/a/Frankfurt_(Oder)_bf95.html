Frankfurt (Oder)

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The red dot is where Frankfurt an der Oder is in Germany

Frankfurt (Oder) is a city in Germany. It lies on the Oder river, which marks the current border between Germany and Poland (The so-called Oder-Neisse line). It's in the east of the state Brandenburg. Since January 1999 the city added a prefix Kleiststadt to refer to Heinrich von Kleist, who was born there in 1777. Today, about 65,000 people live in the city.
