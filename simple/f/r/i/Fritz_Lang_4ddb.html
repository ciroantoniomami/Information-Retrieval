Fritz Lang

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Fritz Lang was a Austrian director, screenwriter, and film producer. He was born December 5, 1890. His movies include Metropolis, M, and Vampyr. He is one of the most famous German Expressionist filmmakers. He died August 2, 1976.
