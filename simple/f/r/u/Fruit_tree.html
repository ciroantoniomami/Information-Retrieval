Fruit tree

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Flowering almond tree

A fruit tree is a tree that produces edible fruits to hold their seeds. However, though every tree produces fruit, not all fruit is edible for humans.

[edit] Examples of tree fruit

  • Apple
  • Cherry
  • Citrus (Orange, Lemon, etc.)
  • Durian
  • Mango
  • Morello cherry
  • Pear
  • Peach and Nectarine
  • Plum
