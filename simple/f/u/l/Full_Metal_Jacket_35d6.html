Full Metal Jacket

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Full Metal Jacket is a 1987 movie directed by Stanley Kubrick, based on the Gustav Hasford book The Short-Timers. It is called Full Metal Jacket because of the full-metal jacketed bullets used in the military.

Actors in this film are Matthew Modine, Adam Baldwin, and Vincent D'Onofrio.
