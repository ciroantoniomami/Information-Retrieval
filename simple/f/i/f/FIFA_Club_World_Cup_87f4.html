FIFA Club World Cup

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The FIFA Club World Championship is the most important competition in the sport of Club football (soccer) (association football, or football). The Fédération Internationale de Football Association (FIFA) organizes the World Championship every one year.
