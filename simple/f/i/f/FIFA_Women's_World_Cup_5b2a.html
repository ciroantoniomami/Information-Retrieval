FIFA Women's World Cup

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The FIFA Women's World Cup is the most important competition in the sport of Women's football (soccer) (association football, or football). The Fédération Internationale de Football Association (FIFA) organizes the World Cup every four years.

[edit] List Champions

  • 1991:United States
  • 1995:Norway
  • 1999:United States
  • 2003:Germany
