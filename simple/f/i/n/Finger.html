Finger

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

A normal human hand with five digits
An abnormal human hand with five digits

A finger is a body part that sticks out from the hand. Each person normally has four fingers and one thumb on each hand.

Sometimes the thumb is called a finger. A finger is a type of digit. It is more careful to say that the hand has five digits:

  • four fingers
  • one thumb
