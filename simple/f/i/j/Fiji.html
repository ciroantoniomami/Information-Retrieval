Fiji

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Fiji is a small country in Pacific Ocean. It has 322 islands. The most important islands are Vanua Levu and Viti Levu. The capital city of Fiji is Suva. There are about 844 330 people in Fiji. The official languages of Fiji are Fijian, English and Hindustani (a mix of Hindi and Urdu].
