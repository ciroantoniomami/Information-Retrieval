[edit] simplification

Please do not copy any articles (or parts of articles) directly from en. It is not good enough to just copy the introduction of an article and call it a "stub" - every word and phrase must be translated into Simple English. In looking at some recent contributions of yours, it seems you are still not following this guideline. Articles like KGB, FSB (Russia), International Nuclear Event Scale are examples of what needs to be avoided. I understand that you want to contribute here, but perhaps you need to concentrate more on improving the articles we already have, rather than copying to create new ones. Work on the more "basic" articles - ones that are in the more general knowledge topics. We REALLY need to make those better. -- Netoholic @ 03:35, 20 May 2006 (UTC)
