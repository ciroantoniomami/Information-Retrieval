Edmund Hillary

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Sir Edmund Hillary after accompanying first plane to land at the Marble Point ground air strip - Antarctica

Sir Edmund Percival Hillary, (born July 20, 1919) is a New Zealand mountaineer and explorer. Along with Sherpa guide Tenzing Norgay, he was the first successful person to climb Mount Everest on May 29, 1953.
