Ejaculation

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Ejaculation is the process in which fluid (semen) is forced out of the end of the penis. This is usually due to stimulation (touching in sensual ways). However, there is also ejaculation in sleep (nocturnal emission) and sometimes spontaneous emission in which no physical stimulation is used.

[edit] Amount of semen

Most men ejaculate about 2 to 6 ml (milliliters). However, the amount and feel of the semen changes from person to person. Most semen is a sticky-white liquid color once ejaculated.

[edit] Dangerous diseases

There are many STD's [Sexually Transmitted (Sent) Diseases] which are sent by semen. These include syphillis and HIV.
