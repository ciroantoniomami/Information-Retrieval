Eric Bana

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Eric Bana (born Eric Banadinovich on August 9, 1968) is an actor from Australia. Eric Bana was a comedian for a long time, and he was in a show called The Comedy Company. He then became a serious actor in the movie Chopper which was about a criminal called Chopper Read. Eric Bana has also been in a movie called Troy.
