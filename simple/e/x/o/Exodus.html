Exodus

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Exodus is the second book in the Torah. Exodus in Hebrew is called Shemot. Exodus starts with The Ten Plagues and the Exodus from Egypt, and finishes with the Jewish people making the Tabernacle.

Exodus also includes The Ten Commandments and the Sin of the Golden Calf.
