Express

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

To express something can mean different things:

  • The most common meaning of it is using language or something equal to make something understood.

It may also mean:

  • To squeeze something out, means pushing something until liquid is coming out.
  • a very fast train which is also often called 'express'.
