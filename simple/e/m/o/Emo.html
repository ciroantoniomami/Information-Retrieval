Emo

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Emo is a kind of music and is short for emotive hardcore. It has its roots from skatepunk, punk and hardcore punk. The emo movement started in the United States in the mid-1980s in cities like Washington, D.C.. "Emo kid" is a name given to someone who is a fan of this kind of music, and not by what he/she looks like.

  • I, Robot
