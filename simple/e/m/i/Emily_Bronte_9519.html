Emily Bronte

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Emily Bronte was born on July 30, 1818 in Thornton, Hampshire, England.

She was a British author. Her most famous book is Wuthering Heights.

Emily had two sisters, Charlotte and Anne, who were also British authors. She attended Cowan School with her sister Charlotte.

Emily Bronte died on December 19, 1848.
