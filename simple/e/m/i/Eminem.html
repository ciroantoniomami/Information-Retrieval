Eminem

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Eminem (Real name: Marshall Bruce Mathers III,stage nameSlim Shady and Eminem) (born October 17, 1972) is a famous rap artist from Michigan. Eminem has a daughter named Hailie Jade Scott and an ex-wife named Kim Scott. He has 6 albums under his name that have been released worldwide. These are:

  • Infinite
  • The Slim Shady LP
  • The Marshall Mathers LP
  • The Eminem Show
  • Encore
  • Curtain Call: The Hits
  • Eminem Presents the Re-Up
  • Untitled upcoming album

He has also released 2 albums worldwide with his rap group, D12:

  • Devil's Night
  • D12 World
  • Untitled upcoming album

He is now thinking about what to do next; the rumor that he will retire may well be true, but has been dismissed by Eminem himself, adding he is just taking time out to see what he wants to do next. He has a movie called "8 Mile" which tells the story about himself. The song "Lose Yourself", which was from the movie, won the Oscar award and was his biggest hit.
