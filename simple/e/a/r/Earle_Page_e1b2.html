Sir Earle Christmas Grafton Page (8 August 1880 - 20 December 1961) was the eleventh Prime Minister of Australia of Australia. He became Prime Minister when Joseph Lyons died in office. He remained until a new leader, Robert Menzies, was elected by the United Australia Party.
