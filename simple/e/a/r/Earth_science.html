Earth science

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Earth science makes scientific experiments on things that are unique to Earth but are not alive (if they are alive it becomes ecology or biology). This includes:

  • Air of Earth
  • Crust of Earth
  • Oceans of Earth
  • Rivers of Earth
  • Rocks of Earth
  • Soil of Earth
