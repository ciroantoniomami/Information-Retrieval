Earthbound 64 (Japanese: Mother 3) was a game that was being made for the Nintendo 64 and then for the Nintendo 64DD, though the game was never sold. A few years after, Nintendo said that they will make a similar game for the Game Boy Advance. It is not known how similar it is to the N64/64DD games. However, it is known that Earthbound 64 had a cowboy as one of the characters, and the pictures of the GBA Mother 3 also had a cowboy, so it seems that things were taken from the N64/64DD games.

[edit] Characters

  • Flint - A cowboy
  • Lucas - A twin
  • Klaus - the other twin
  • Boney - the dog
