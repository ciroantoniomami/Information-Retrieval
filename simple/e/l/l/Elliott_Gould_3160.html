Elliott Gould

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Elliot Gould (real name: Elliott Gouldstein) (born August 29, 1938) is a well-known American actor. He is mostly known for his roles in many films including M*A*S*H (1970), Bob & Carol & Ted & Alice (1970), and Ocean's Eleven (2001). He is still a popular actor and has also made guest appearances on many television shows as well. From 1963 to 1970, he was married to the famous singer and actress, Barbra Streisand and they had a son named Jason.
