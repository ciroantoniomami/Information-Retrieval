English: Boeing 747-200 of El Al, Israeli airlines (registration number 4X-AHQ).
Picture by Przemysław "Blueshade" Idzkiewicz.

Landing approach to runway 11 at Warszawa Okęcie airport on August 24th, 2004.
The paint style on this airplane is historic now and not used for El Al's newer aircrafts.
