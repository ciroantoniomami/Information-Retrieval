Elbe

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The Elbe River (Czech Labe  listen ?, Sorbian/Lusatian Łobjo, Polish Łaba, German Elbe) is one of the major waterways of central Europe. It originates in the North West Czech Republic. Then it goes through much of Germany and finally it flows into the North Sea.
