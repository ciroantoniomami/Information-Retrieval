Everybody Loves Raymond

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Everybody Loves Raymond is a popular sitcom starring Ray Romano. The show airs on CBS.

[edit] Overview

Ray Romano plays Ray Barone, a sports writer. Patricia Heaton plays his wife, Debra. Ray and Deborah have a very intrusive set of neighbors: Ray's parents. Robert, Ray's "loser" brother, is a police officer, and is very unlucky in love. By the end of season seven, however, he finds himself married to a woman named Amy. The finale to the show aired on May 16, 2005.
