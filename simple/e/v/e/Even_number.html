Even number

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

An even number is a number which is a multiple of two. If it is divided by two the result another whole number. Zero is an even number because zero multipied by two is zero. The next four bigger even numbers are two, four, six, and eight.

A number that is not an even number is an odd number.
