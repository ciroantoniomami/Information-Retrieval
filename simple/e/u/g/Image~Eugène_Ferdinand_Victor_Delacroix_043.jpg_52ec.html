Frédéric Chopin as portrayed by Eugène Delacroix in 1838. This portrait was originally part of a larger painting showing both Frédéric Chopin and Georges Sand. The Chopin painting is now in the Louvre, Paris, while this George Sand protrait is in the possession of the Ordrupgaard museum in Copenhagen, Denmark.

This George Sand portrait was once part of the same painting.
