Euzebiusz Smolarek

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Euzebiusz Smolarek is a Polish football player who plays for Borussia Dortmund. He is a striker. He was born in Lodz in 1981. In 2004 he came to Dortmund together with Bert van Maarwijk, who is his trainer. Before that, he played for Feyenoord Rotterdam under van Maarwijk. He is also in the Polish national team.
