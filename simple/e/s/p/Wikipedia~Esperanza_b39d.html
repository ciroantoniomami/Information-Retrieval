Esperanza is a project which seeks to bring peace to all our users. Our name comes from the Spanish word for hope, meaning that we hope our project holds together.

[edit] Welcome

Also be sure to welcome users to Esperanza, through adding the following template:

[edit] {{Welcome Esperanza}}

Welcome, Esperanza, to Esperanza! As you may know, all the members have one important goal, which is the success of this encyclopedia. To do this we want to make the community better, and be the nice side of Wikipedia.

If you have any questions, comments, or general ideas, use the Esperanza talk page! We thank you for joining Esperanza, and look forward to working with you in making Wikipedia a better place to work!

[edit] Welcoming

You probably noticed someone put a welcome message on your talk page when you signed up. At Esperanza, we like to make users feel welcome. For new users, use {{subst:welcome}} ~~~~ on their talk page, and for new IP addresses, use {{subst:anon}} ~~~~. If you spot a new Esperanza member, use {{subst:Wikipedia:Esperanza/Welcome message|~~~~}} on their talk page. This will put the official welcome template on their user page, as you can see on this talk page.

[edit] Leadership

[edit] Charter

We are led by an Admin general, who is an administrator. He/she can be put out of office by Esperanza at any time if he/she is caught not doing what he is supposed to. He/she is elected by putting the candidate's username below. If 60% of our members oppose within the next week we put up another user to be elected by the community. He/she will serve a 3 month term if elected.

[edit] After getting 50 members

After getting 50 members we will elect an advisory council, which will help lead us.
