Eyeglasses

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

A man wearing eyeglasses

Eyeglasses are pieces of glass (or clear plastic), called lenses, in a frame that holds them in front of your eyes. Corrective lenses allow people who have problems seeing to see better. Sunglasses are a kind of eyeglasses that keep too much sunlight from getting in people's eyes.
