World of Warcraft

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

World of Warcraft is a MMORPG computer game. It is commonly called WoW. It was created by Blizzard Entertainment, a company which makes many games. The game takes place in a setting Blizzard has used for other games using the name "Warcraft"

Warcraft: Orcs and Humans was an real-time strategy game. So were Warcraft 2 and its expansion, Warcraft 2:Tides of Darkness and Warcraft 3 and its expansion, Warcraft 3:The Frozen Throne. But WoW is not like these games. It is of the style of gaming known as Massively Multiplayer Online Role-Playing Game (MMORPG) instead.

You get many items in World of Warcraft by killing monsters and other enemies. Bosses in dungeons, such as Nefarian, drop items of better quality than normal enemies . Nefarian is a dragon.
