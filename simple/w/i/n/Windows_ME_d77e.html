Windows ME

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Windows ME is the Millenium Edition of the Microsoft Windows operating system. Windows ME was an effort to make money from Microsoft. It was not very secure and had many mistakes. It was called "Windows Mistake Edition" by some people.
