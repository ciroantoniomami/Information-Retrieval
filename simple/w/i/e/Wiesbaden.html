Wiesbaden

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Wiesbaden railroad station, built from 1904 till 1906

Wiesbaden is a German city near Frankfurt. It is the capital of the ferderal state Hesse. Wiesbaden is situated on the right (north) bank of the Rhine (German: "Rhein"), near the city of Mainz (about 10 kilometers away from Wiesbaden centre) the opposite side of the river, and a short distance, about 20 miles (30 kilometers), from Frankfurt am Main, to the east. Wiesbaden has about 274,000 inhabitants (2005).
