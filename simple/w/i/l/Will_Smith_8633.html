Will Smith

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Will Smith listens intently to a reporter's questions at the media scrum for the 2006 Time 100, as covered by video blog Rocketboom.

Will Smith (September 25, 1968) is an American actor and rapper from Philadelphia, Pennsylvania. He got his start as part of the rap duo DJ Jazzy Jeff and the Fresh Prince. He became an actor when he starred on the television show "The Fresh Prince of Bel-Air" as Will Smith, a teenager from Philadelphia sent to live with his rich relatives in Southern California. He has appeared in many movies including Independence Day, Men in Black, Ali, and Wild Wild West.
