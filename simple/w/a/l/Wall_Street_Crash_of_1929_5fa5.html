Wall Street Crash of 1929

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The Wall Street Crash of 1929 was a stock market crash on the New York Stock Exchange on October 29, 1929. It was a tuesday so now it goes by the name "Black Tuesday".
