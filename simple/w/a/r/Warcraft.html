Warcraft

WarCraft is a real-time strategy (RTS) computer game where you have Humans and Orcs battle. It requires gathering resources for building a better army. The game needs a lot of thinking to win. There have been a few more WarCraft games since such as WarCraft 2 and later WarCraft 2 Battle.net Edition.

Later in the summer of 2002 WarCraft 3 came out and was an amazing game.Warcraft 3 was the first game of the series to be in 3-D graphics. It added the two new races Night Elf and Undead. In 2003 an expansion pack was released called The Frozen Throne.

In 2004, World of Warcraft came out, which was the first game in the series to be a non RTS game. World of Warcraft is a Massively Multiplayer Online Role-Playing Game, which allows players to create a character on a realm, where thousands of other players come together and play.
