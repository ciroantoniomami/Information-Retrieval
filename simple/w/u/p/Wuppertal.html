Wuppertal

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Wuppertal is a city in the west of Germany, in the state of North Rhine-Westphalia. It is neighboring to Düsseldorf. Wuppertal has about 280,000 inhabitants and an university. A well-known tourist attraction is the Wuppertaler Schwebebahn, a tram line hanging at a steel way 10 m over the ground.
