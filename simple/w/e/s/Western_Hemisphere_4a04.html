Western Hemisphere

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The geographic Western Hemisphere is yellow

The Western Hemisphere is a part of the world. Pretend that the earth is cut in half, from the North Pole, through England, to the South Pole. The Western Hemisphere is the half to the west.
