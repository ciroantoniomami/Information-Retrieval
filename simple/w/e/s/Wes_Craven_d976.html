Wes Craven

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Wes Craven born August 2, 1939 in Cleveland, Ohio is an American movie director, well-known for horror classics including A Nightmare on Elm Street and Scream.

[edit] filmography

  • Together (1971)
  • The Last House on the Left (1972)
  • The Hills Have Eyes (1977)
  • Summer Of Fear aka Stranger In Our House (1978)
  • Deadly Blessing (1981)
  • Swamp Thing (1982)
  • A Nightmare On Elm Street (1984)
  • Deadly Friend (1986)
  • The Serpent and the Rainbow (1988)
  • Wes Craven's Shocker (1989)
  • The People Under the Stairs (1991)
  • Wes Craven's New Nightmare (1994)
  • Scream (1996)
  • Scream 2 (1997)
  • Music of the Heart (1999)
  • Scream 3 (2000)
  • They (2002)
  • Cursed (2005)
  • Red Eye (2005)
