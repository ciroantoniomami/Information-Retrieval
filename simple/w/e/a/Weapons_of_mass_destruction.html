Weapons of mass destruction

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Weapons of mass destruction are weapons that can cause widespread destruction to a large number of people. In general, people see the following as weapons of mass destruction:

  • Chemical weapons, like poisonous gases (eg. nerve gases)
  • Biological weapons. Those are usually germs, or other organisms that may cause a disease. Eg. Smallpox or Anthrax.
  • Nuclear weapons
