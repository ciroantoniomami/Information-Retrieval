W. H. Auden

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

W.H. Auden (right) with Christopher Isherwood in 1939.

Wystan Hugh Auden (February 21 1907 – September 29 1973) was an Anglo-American poet. He wrote poems like Funeral Blues. He signed his works W. H. Auden.
