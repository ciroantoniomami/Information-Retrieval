Whale shark

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

a Whale shark

Whale shark is the largest known fish.Whale sharks can be almost 15 meters long and weight 15 tons. They are not dangerous to people. They only eat plankton or very small fish because they have small teeth. Whale shark swim very slowly.
