The White House of the United States of America

The White House can refer to the home of the President of the United States of America. It is located at 1600 Pennsylvania Avenue in Washington, D.C.. It is the official residence of the President and his family during his administration. When a new President is elected, the old one moves out. It also has the offices where the President helps run the country.
