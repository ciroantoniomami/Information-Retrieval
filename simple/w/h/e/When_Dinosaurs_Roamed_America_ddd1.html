When Dinosaurs Roamed America

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

When Dinosaurs Roamed America is a 2001 television movie from the Discovery Channel. The movie was narrated by John Goodman. The backgrounds of the movie are really parts of America. The movie has a lot of attacks with huge dinosaurss.

[edit] Featured creatures

[edit] Late Triassic Segment

  • Coelophysis
  • Rutiodon
  • Desmatosuchus
  • Icarosaurus
  • Traversodon
  • Unidentified mammal, probably Morganucodon or Megazostrodon
  • quoll (live-acted, probably appeared by accident)
  • grasshopper

[edit] Early Jurassic Segment

  • Dilophosaurus
  • Megapnosaurus (identified as Syntarsus, which it was called at the time)
  • Anchisaurus (in terms of size and general appearance it resembles a Massospondylus)

[edit] Late Jurassic Segment

  • Camarasaurus
  • Allosaurus
  • Apatosaurus
  • Stegosaurus
  • Dryosaurus
  • Ceratosaurus
  • Unidentified pterosaur, probably Pterodactylus

[edit] Middle Cretaceous Segment

  • Nothronychus
  • Dromaeosaurus (identified as dromaeosaur)
  • Zuniceratops
  • Coelurosaurus (identified as coelurosaur)

[edit] Late Cretaceous Segment

  • Tyrannosaurus
  • Triceratops
  • Anatotitan
  • Quetzalcoatlus
  • Purgatorius (live acted by opossum)
  • Ornithomimus (not identified, revealed on web)
