The Nintendo GameCube is a video game console made by Nintendo. The console before that was the Nintendo 64 (N64 for short). Nintendo's next console will be the Nintendo Wii that will be out sometime during late 2006.

The GameCube's controllers have two joysticks. In many games, one of them is used to control a character. The yellow joystick replaces the C buttons on the controller of the Nintendo 64. There is four buttons A, B, Y and X, another one over the L and R. The wireless version of the controller is called "Wavebird".
