Galatasaray

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Galatasaray is a sports club in Istanbul, Turkey. It was started in 1905.

They became the first Turkish club to win the UEFA Cup and the UEFA Super Cup in 2000. Its colors are red and yellow. Turkish people also call it "Cim Bom". Galatasaray is one of the "big three" clubs in Turkey. The other clubs are Fenerbahçe and Beşiktaş.
