Gwinnett County, Georgia

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Gwinnett Countyis a county in the American state of Georgia. The county seat of Gwinnett County is Lawrenceville. The county was created on December 15, 1818. In 2006, the Census bureau says the population of Gwinnett County was 757,104[1].

The county was named for Button Gwinnett, one of the people who signed the United States Declaration of Independence for Georgia.
