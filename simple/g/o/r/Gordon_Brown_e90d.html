Gordon Brown

James Gordon Brown, (born February 20, 1951) is the Chancellor of the Exchequer (the person in charge of the country's money) of the United Kingdom and is in the British Labour Party.

He was born in Glasgow Scotland and is married to Sarah Macaulay, who he has had two children with. Many people think he will become the Prime Minister of the United Kingdom when Tony Blair retires.
