Giancarlo Fisichela

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Giancarlo in a Racedriver

Giancarlo Fisichella (* 14 January 1973 in Rome) is an Italian running driver and drives at present in the formula 1 with the Renault F1-Team.
