Gilbertville, Ontario

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Gilbertville is a hamlet in Norfolk County, Ontario, Canada that is in between Delhi and Pine Grove. There is a fish market, a tractor shop, a swamp, a car repair shop, and farmland to the south. In addition to all this, Gilbertville is also the site of a former army base and was founded by the Gilbert family in the 19th century as a place to rest horses when travelling to and from the towns of Simcoe and Delhi.
