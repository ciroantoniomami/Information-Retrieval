Guinea-Bissau

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Satelite image of Guinea-Bissau
Where Guinea-Bissau is in the World

Guinea-Bissau is a country in Africa. The official language is Portuguese, and the capital is Bissau.

About 1.4 million people live in Guinea-Bissau
