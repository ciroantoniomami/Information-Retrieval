Gulf of Mexico

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The Gulf of Mexico is a sea, but it is called a gulf. Land in North America and Central America is around much of it. The United States of America, Mexico, and Cuba are some of the countries around it. It is also connected to the Atlantic Ocean and the Caribbean Sea.
