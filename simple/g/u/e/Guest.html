Simple English Wiktionary has a dictionary definition (word meaning) for:
guest

A guest is a visitor to whom hospitality is extended. The term is often used to describe those visiting somewhere, such as a Hostel or Hotel.

If Person A comes to Person B's house, Person A is the guest and Person B is the host.
