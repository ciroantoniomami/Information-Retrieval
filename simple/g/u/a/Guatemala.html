Guatemala

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Guatemala is a country in Central America. It is the country with the largest population in Central America, and its neighbors are the countries Belize, Mexico, El Salvador and Honduras.

More than fourteen million people live in the 22 Guatemalan departments. Guatemala is famous in the world for its natural beauty, and is one of the favorite tourist destinations in the world.
