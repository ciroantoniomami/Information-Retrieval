Ghana

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The Republic of Ghana (formerly the Gold Coast) is a country in West Africa. Twenty-one million people live there; its capital is Accra.

Kofi Annan, the president of the United Nations, is a native of the country.
