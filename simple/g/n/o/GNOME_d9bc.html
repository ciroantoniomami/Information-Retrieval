GNOME

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Reading the Wikipedia using GNOME while browsing files

GNOME is a computer desktop environment. It was designed for GNU/Linux, but many other operating systems can use it too. GNOME aims to provide an easy way to use a computer. GNOME is a free software project.

GNOME has many applications written for it. Some of them include:

  • Abiword - A word processor, used for writing documents
  • Calculator - For doing maths
  • Epiphany - Surf the Internet.
  • Gaim - Talk to your friends online.
  • Gnucash - Manage your money
  • Gthumb - View your photos.
  • The GIMP - Art and Photo software.
  • Totem - Play music and video!.
