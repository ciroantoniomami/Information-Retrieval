George Michael

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

George Michael (born Georgios Kyriacos Panayiotou on June 25, 1963) is a English singer-songwriter. He co-founded and was a part of the pop band WHAM!. After he got in trouble with the police, he announced his homosexuality.

Some of his songs include "Wake Me Up Before You Go-Go" and "Last Christmas".
