Gerhard Schröder

Gerhard Schröder was born on April 7, 1944 in Mossenberg. He was the chancellor of Germany. He first became chancellor in 1998. After elections in 2005, he was replaced by Angela Merkel. He is a member of the SPD (Social Democratic Party of Germany).
