Germanium

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

A piece of germanium

Germanium is a chemical element. It has the chemical symbol Ge. It has the atomic number 32. It is a shiny, hard, silver-white metalloid. The chemistry of Germanium is quite like tin. Germanium forms a large number of organometallic compounds. It is an important semiconductor material used in transistors.
