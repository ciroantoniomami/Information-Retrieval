Dravidian languages

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Language families of the world. Places where Dravidian languages are spoken are this color.

The Dravidian languages are a language family. They are mainly spoken in South India. There are approximately 26 languages in this family.

[edit] List of Dravidian languages

  • Kannada language
  • Malayalam language
  • Tamil language
  • Telugu language
  • Brahui language
