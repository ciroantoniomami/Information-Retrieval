Dragon Ball Z

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Dragon Ball Z is a animated Japanese cartoon (Anime) like Pokemon created by Akira Toriyama.

[edit] Characters

[edit] Good Guys

  • Goku (孫 悟空|, Son Goku, Kakarot): Goku is the main hero. He is a Saiyan, with large, black, pointy hair. He and Vegeta are the only Saiyans to have reached Super Saiyan 4. His wife's name is Chi-Chi, and his two sons are Gohan and Goten. His grandaughter's name is Pan. His rival is Vegeta
  • Vegeta: Vegeta is the rival of Goku. He also became a Super Saiyan.
