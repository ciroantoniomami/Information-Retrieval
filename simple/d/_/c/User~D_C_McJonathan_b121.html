David Clarkson McJonathan-Swarm likes to think of himself an in his and and his interests reflect this. Technically 'retired' and collecting a pension following the physically disabling injuries sustained from the impact of a tractor trailer to his in 1991. His wife alleges that "he is busier now than when he worked full-time and we had five sons at home." To many he is known simply as "Mack" or "Doc."

Fascinated by history his entire life, a medical researcher, photographer, and biographer he started writing bios for some of the 'famous' listings at which he discovered from a search on. Finding many persons that he had known, socially and through broadcasting in New York City listed, he started to submit 'Better Bios' on the thread. Many times his reference searches would bring up which finally led to his presence here. After his friend and partner's obituary appeared in and on wire feed it was included in recent deaths on Wikipedia, but the lack of a biography was noted for both him and their common mentor. These were his first new articles.
