DDR (disambiguation)

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

DDR is a three-letter abbreviation with multiple meanings, including:

  • Deutsche Demokratische Republik, the former German Democratic Republic, also known as East Germany
  • Dance Dance Revolution, a music video game series
  • Dial-on-demand routing
  • Distributed dynamic routing
  • Disarmament, Demobilization and Reintegration - activities designed to facilitate disbanding military fighters and easing their transition back into society.
    • For example Afghan Disarmament, Demobilization and Reintegration Program
  • Direct to Disk Recording of moving images
  • Double data rate, in computing, is a type of computer bus;
  • DDR SDRAM, the most common type of random access memory in new computers.
