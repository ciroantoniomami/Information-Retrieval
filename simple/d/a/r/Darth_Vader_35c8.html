Darth Vader

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Darth Vader is a fictional character in the Star Wars films. He appears as a 2 meter-tall man dressed in black armor and a cape. He appears for the first time in A New Hope. His face is covered with a mask, which has a helmet on top. Vader is the main character of the Dark Side of the Star Wars series. Born as Anakin Skywalker on Tatooine, he was converted by Palpatine to join his cause and accepted to learn the ways of the dark side of the Force, defying his master Obi-Wan Kenobi. Darth Vader died in Return of the Jedi, but not before returning to the light, thanks to his son, Luke Skywalker.
