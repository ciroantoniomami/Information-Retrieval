Davy Jones (actor)

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Davy Jones (December 30, 1945-) is a British singer and actor, he was born in England in the city of Manchester. His father originally wanted him to be a jockey, but he wanted to be a performer. He appeared in the TV show,Coronation Street and in the London and Broadway versions of "Oliver!". In 1966, he joined The Monkees, a musical group created for a TV show of the same name. After the TV show was cancelled in 1968 and the group disbanded in 1970, he still continues to perform.
