David Ogden Steirs

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

David Ogden Steirs (born October 31, 1942) is an American actor and voice artist. He is most famous for his role as Charles Emerson Winchester III on the TV show, M*A*S*H. He also did the voices in several Disney animated movies, most notably Cogsworth in Beauty and the Beast, Sir John Radcliffe in Pocahantas, and Dr. Jumba Jookiba in Lilo and Stich.

He also plays the voice of Escher in the Myst V (End of Ages) video game.
