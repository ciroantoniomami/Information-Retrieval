Damascus

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Damascus at night

Damascus is the Capital city of Syria. It is the largest city in Syria with about 4.5 million people living there. Damascus is thought to be one of oldest cities in the world that still has people living in it. It is beleived that people started living in Damascus as long ago as 8000 BC to 10000BC.
