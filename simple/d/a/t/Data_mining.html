Data mining

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Data mining is a term used mainly in Computer science. Sometimes it is also called Knowledge discovery in Databases (KDD). Data mining is about finding similarities in large sets of data. It is about automatically discovering patterns in large sets of data. Very often, such data is stored in some form of database. Some commn algorithms used can be classified as pattern-recognition, Bayesian or Neural Network.
