Data

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The word data means "pieces of information". Types of data include numbers, words, sounds, and images.

see also data processing, data mining, Data Warehouse, datasheet, Computer program

Other uses of this term include:

  • Data (Star Trek), a fictional android in the television series Star Trek: The Next Generation.
  • Data, a character in the film "The Goonies", played by actor Ke Huy Quan.
