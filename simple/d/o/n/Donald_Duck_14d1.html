Donald Duck

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Donald Duck is one of the most popular cartoon characters in the world. He was created by Walt Disney.

Donald is a white duck with an orange bill and legs. Many people think that Donald has been censured in Finland because he does not wear trousers, but it is an urban legend. He was created in 1934 by an unknow designer and his first debut in a cartoon was on 9 June. As says Don Rosa's family tree, Donald Duck is Quackmore Duck and Orthensia McDuck's son and therefore he is a nephew of Orthensia's brother, Scrooge McDuck. Now Donald Duck is most famous than many actors and actress, and he appears in the comic strips of all the world.
