Download

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

A Download is getting information from another computer. The opposite is upload, sending data to another computer. Usually we do not say "download" for a single web page (for example when you open this page on your computer). When we say we downloaded something, it is normally a bigger piece of information, or an executable program.
