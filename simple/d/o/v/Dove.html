Dove

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Pigeons in a tree

A dove is a kind of bird. Another word used for a dove is pigeon.

In the bible, after Noah released a white dove to find land after the flood, the dove returned with an olive branch. Usually, a white dove with an olive branch in its beak means peace and love.

There are 308 species of these birds.
