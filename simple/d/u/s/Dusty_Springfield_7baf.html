Dusty Springfield

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Mary Isabel Catherine Bernadette O'Brien or Dusty Springfield (April 16, 1939- March 2,1999) was a British pop singer. She recorded over 200 songs and her career lasted over 30 years, she died of breast cancer in 1999.
