DiC

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

DiC is an animation studio founded in 1971 in Paris, France. It now operates in Burbank, California, USA, and made many well known cartoons including:

  • Action Man
  • Adventures of Sonic the Hedgehog
  • Captain Planet and the Planeteers (co-produced with Ted Turner's TBS Productions)
  • Care Bears (first 21 episodes)
  • Dennis the Menace
  • Gadget Boy & Heather
  • Inspector Gadget
  • Liberty's Kids
  • Mary-Kate And Ashley In Action
  • Mummies Alive!
  • Sabrina, the Animated Series
  • Sailor Moon (English-language version, first 82 episodes)
  • Sherlock Holmes in the 22nd Century
  • Sonic the Hedgehog (SATAM)
  • Street Sharks
  • The Super Mario Bros. Super Show
  • The Littles
  • The Real Ghostbusters (co-produced with Columbia Pictures Television in 1987).
