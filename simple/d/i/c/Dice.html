Dice

Dice are objects used in games when something random needs to be done, such as moving a piece a random number of places on a board. Most dice are cubes that have the numbers 1, 2, 3, 4, 5 and 6 on the faces. Dice are sometimes other shapes than cubes, but the 6-sided die (die is the single of dice) is the most common. A die is usually rolled by the player’s hand.

Some games are played with two die but sometimes one. A game that is used with a die is a well-known British game called Monopoly.
