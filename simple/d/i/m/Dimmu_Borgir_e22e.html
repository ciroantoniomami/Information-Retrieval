Dimmu Borgir

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Dimmu Borgir is a black metal band founded in 1993 in Norway. Its name is Icelandic for a "dark castle".

The band's first recordings were in Norwegian, but nowadays most of the material is in English. The newest studio album is Death Cult Armageddon (2003).

Dimmu Borgir has performed in Ozzfest.
