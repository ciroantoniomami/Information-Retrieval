Diagnosis

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Diagnosis is when a doctor finds out what disease you have. The verb is to diagnose. Sometimes doctors can diagnose a disease by talking to you and looking at your body. Sometimes doctors do tests. Tests can look at your blood. Tests can be x-rays. Tests are ways to see how your body is working.
