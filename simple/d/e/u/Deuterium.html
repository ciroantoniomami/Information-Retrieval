Deuterium

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Deuterium is an isotope of hydrogen, the first element. Deuterium has one proton and one neutron. Hydrogen does not have a neutron, only a proton. The chemical symbol for Deuterium is 2H but D is also used often.
