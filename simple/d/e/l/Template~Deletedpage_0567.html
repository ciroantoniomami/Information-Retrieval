Template:Deletedpage

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

This page has been deleted, and protected to stop people from creating it again without a good reason.
  • Information about why it was deleted might be in the deletion log or the protection log.
  • You may ask for it to be unprotected by explaining on the talk page why the page is needed.
  • If you do not get a response in a short time, then you may check the protection log and contact the administrator who protected it. You can also contact another administrator.
  • Administrators may view the page history and content at Special:Undelete/Template:Deletedpage.
