[edit] BLHDA

I am a co-founder of the BLHDA (Bishop luffa hole digging association). I volentarily donate my time and effort to this organisation, which is a NGO (Non governmental organisation) that relies solely on donations to keep it running. The organisation provides disadvantaged children with the opportunity to socialise with others who are in the same situation as them. We encourage them to dig holes and make ornate water features like ponds. It gives them a real sense of achievment and satisfaction. We recently applied for charity regestration and our still awaiting for our approval. This permission will allow us to expand further, giving us national recognition and make us able to cater for more children. Click hereto visit the offical website. A password and username are required, which i will give on request. Due to the controversy regarding an encyclopedia entry about the BLHDA, I have decided not to re-create a page yet.
