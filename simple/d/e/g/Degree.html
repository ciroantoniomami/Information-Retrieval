Degree

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Degree generally means to have a level in something.

The word "degree" also mean the following things:

  • A degree is something that a university gives to people who graduate from it.
  • A degree is something that a school gives to people who graduate from it.
  • A degree is a unit of how we measure temperature.
  • A degree in geometry is 1/360th of a circle.
