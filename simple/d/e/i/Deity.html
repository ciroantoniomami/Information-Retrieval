Deity

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

This article is about gods. See also God.

Some people believe that gods are powerful spirits who control the nature things. A female god is called a goddess.

Followers of many ancient religions, and the modern religion of Hinduism, believe in many gods and goddesses.

Some famous gods and goddesses of different religions are:

  • From Ayyavazhi: Ekam, Ayya Vaikundar, Narayana, Sivan, Vethan, Thirumal
  • From the ancient Greek religion: Zeus, Apollo, Athena, Ares, Aphrodite
  • From the ancient Roman religion: Jupiter, Mars, Venus
  • From the ancient Norse religion: Thor, Odin
  • From Hinduism: Brahma, Vishnu, Shiva, Krishna, Durga, Ganesha
