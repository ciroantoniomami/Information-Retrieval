B'z

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

B'z is a Japanese rock band. It consists of Takahiro Matsumoto (guitar) and Koshi Inaba (singer). B'z debuted on September 21, 1988. The band has sold more than 70 million CDs. They have had 19 albums sell over 1 million copies each.
