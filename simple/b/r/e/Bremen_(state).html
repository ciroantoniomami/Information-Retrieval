Bremen (state)

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Bremen is a city-state with 404,23 km². There live 663.642 people. It is the smallest Bundesland in Germany. The official name is "Freie Hansestadt Bremen". It consists of the 2 cities Bremen and Bremerhaven. The local soccer club is named Werder Bremen.
