The wapen of Brasschaat

Brasschaat is a part of Belgium. It is located near Antwerp. Brasschaat is famous for its nature.

[edit] Military history

Brasschaat had always a big military force present in Brasschaat. After World War II there were even more things that reminded of the military force. Including the defence buildings made to stop the Nazi's from reaching The port of Antwerp. Now most of them are closed or are used to train the soldiers.

[edit] Green Brasschaat

Brasschaat is always called green, this is because of the many parcs and woods. A few examples: The Peerdsbos, the parc of Brasschaat, De uitlegger, De instlag.
