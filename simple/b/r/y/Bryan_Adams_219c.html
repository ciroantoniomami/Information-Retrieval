Bryan Adams

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Bryan Adams (November 9, 1959) is a Canadian rock singer. He is known for the songs Summer of '69 and (Everything I Do) I Do It For You. He has performed with many other musical artists such as Def Leppard, Tina Turner, Sting, Rod Stewart, Bonnie Raitt, Barbra Streisand, and Melanie C.
