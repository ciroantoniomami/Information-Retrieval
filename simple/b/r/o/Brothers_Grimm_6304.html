Brothers Grimm

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Wilhelm Grimm (left) and Jakob Grimm. Contemorary portrait by Elisabeth Maria Anna Jerichau-Baumann. done in 1855.
Image of the brothers on the 1000 Deutsche Mark bill

The Brothers Grimm, were two brothers. Jakob Grimm, and Wilhelm Grimm. They both became linguists. They are the inventors of German philology. They also collected fairy tales, and wrote them down. They published a collection of Fairy tales known as Grimm's Fairy Tales (Grimms MÃ¤rchen). They also did other language-related work, like publishing one of the first grammar books for the German language.
