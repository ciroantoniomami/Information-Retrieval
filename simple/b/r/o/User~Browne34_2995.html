Hi, my name is Eric. I live in Iowa in the United States. I have been focusing mainly on revising and adding government, military, and United States history articles. I urge anyone from the UK to look over and add to the Prime Minister pages I've done. I'm working off of English wikipedia, but I fear I'm not doing them justice. I also help with dealing with vandalism when I can. The only thing keeping me from becoming an addict to editing is my 8-5 job. I still pitch in with fighting vandalism at work on the sly. Please leave me a message on my talk page if you have questions or comments. I'm trying to become more active on this great project, and I'm still learning things like adding pictures, infoboxes, and other like stuff.
