Brown

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

This box shows the color brown.
 

Brown is the color that is made when opposite colors on the color wheel are mixed together in paint. Brown is not a color of light, and mixing all the colors of light together gives white. Brown is the color of:

  • Chocolate
  • Most Dirt
  • Wood
  • Cardboard
  • UPS Trucks
