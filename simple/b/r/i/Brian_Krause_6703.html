Brian Krause

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Brian Krause (born February 1 1969) is an American actor who grew up in El Toro, California. He is best known for his role as Leo Wyatt on the WB Network TV series Charmed from 1998 to 2006.

Krause made his film debut as Richard Lestrange in the film sequel Return to the Blue Lagoon (1991). A year later, he also starred opposite Alice Krige, playing Charles Brady in the horror film Sleepwalkers.

Brian and his wife Beth Bruce had their first child, Jamen, in 1996, but were later divorced in 2000. He dated co-star Alyssa Milano and is currently dating an unknown woman in the United States. During his time off the set, he enjoys being a handyman which was his job before acting (and, coincidentally, was his fake job when he played Leo Wyatt on Charmed), he also enjoys golf and auto racing.
