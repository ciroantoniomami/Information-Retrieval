Brittany

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Historical region of Brittany
Where the region is in France.

Brittany is a region in northwest France. In Breton it is called Breizh and in French Bretagne. Today the official administrative region has 4 departements:

  • Cotes d'Armor
  • Finistere
  • Ille-et-Villaine
  • Morbihan

The capital is Rennes.
