Britney Spears

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Britney Spears (born December 2, 1981 in Kentwood, Louisiana), is an American singer and actress.

[edit] Early success

Britney Spears was on a TV show for children in 1993 and 1994 called the New Mickey Mouse Club. Other people on the show during this time were singers Christina Aguilera, Justin Timberlake, and JC Chasez (who would later be members of the band *NSYNC), and actors Ryan Gosling and Keri Russell.

In 1998, she made her first song, "...Baby One More Time". The music video for the song is famous for showing Britney in a Catholic schoolgirl uniform.

[edit] Albums

Britney has made 6 albums. They are:

  1. ...Baby One More Time (1999)
  2. Oops!... I Did It Again (2000)
  3. Britney (2001)
  4. In The Zone (2003)
  5. Greatest Hits: My Prerogative (2004)
  6. B In The Mix: The Remixes (2005).

[edit] Movies

In 2002, Spears play the lead role, Lucy Ann Wagner, in the movie Crossroads.

She also had a small role in the movie Austin Powers in Goldmember in 2002.

[edit] Family life

On January 3, 2004, Spears married her childhood friend Jason Allan Alexander in Las Vegas, Nevada, United States. The marriage was annulled only 55 hours later.

On September 18, 2004, Britney married Kevin Federline. They had a son together on September 14, 2005 named Sean Preston Spears Federline. On September 12, 2006, Britney gave birth to her second child, another son named Jayden James Spears Federline.

In November 2006, Britney filed for divorce from Kevin.

[edit] Trivia

  • Spears cancelled her tour in 2004 after she received a knee injury.
  • She has recently launched a line of perfumes.
  • She recently shaved her head and spent over $50,000 on wigs.
