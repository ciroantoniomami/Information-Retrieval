Black Hand

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Black Hand or Crna Ruka, officially Ujedinjenje ili Smrt ("Unification or Death") was a secret group of Serbian nationalists (Pan-Serbians). It was founded in 1911 in Serbia.

Their aim was unification of countries Serbs lived in those days, including Bosnia-Herzegovina which was a part of Austria-Hungary since 1908. They killed the Archduke Franz Ferdinand of Austria in 1914. They killed the King Aleksandar Obrenovic and Queen Draga Masin too.
