About me

Hello, I am Jean-Paul , Wikipedian from Switzerland.

I am working on a variety of Wiki projects, but, my most active one by far is Wikipedia, in English. The other wikis that I work on are Commons, Wikinews, French Wikipedia, Wikipedia in Dutch, English Wikipedia, Wiktionary, Wikisource, Wikiquote and HRWiki

I work on Simple English wikipedia so that all people who have a hard time speaking English can also have an English Wikipedia.
