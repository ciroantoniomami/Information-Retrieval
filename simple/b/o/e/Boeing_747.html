Boeing 747

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Boeing 747 or Jumbo Jet is a type of airplane. It mostly used by people who are traveling across countries.

Boeing built it in the 1960s and was first commercially used in 1970. It is a doubledecker plane; and is one of the only Boeing aircraft that flies over seas.

These airlines have Boeing 747s:

Japan Airlines (60)

British Airways (57)

Korean Air (42)

Cathay Pacific (37)

Air France (32)

United Airlines (30)
