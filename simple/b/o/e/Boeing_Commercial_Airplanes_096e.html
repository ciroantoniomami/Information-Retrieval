Boeing Commercial Airplanes

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Boeing 737

Boeing is a company that makes airplanes from Chicago, Illinois, USA. Some kinds of Boeing are:

  • Boeing 707 (out of production)
  • Boeing 717 (out of production)
  • Boeing 727 (out of production)
  • Boeing 737
  • Boeing 747 (affectionately named the 'Jumbo Jet' because of its massive size)
  • Boeing 757 (out of production)
  • Boeing 767
  • Boeing 777 (named the 'Triple Seven')

The Boeing 787 Dreamliner is still in the development stage of production and the first flight is scheduled for 2008.

Their main manufacturing plant is near where the company was founded in Seattle, Washington.
