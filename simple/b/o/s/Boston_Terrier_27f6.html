Boston Terrier

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

A Boston Terrier puppy.

Boston Terriers are a breed of dog.

They are usually very small, but can also be bred as medium sized dogs. They are considered to be cute dogs and are getting ever more popular. They are in many commercials and many celebrities have them.

Boston terriers are a smart breed of dogs. They can be extremely hyper, but overall are well behaved and very laid back. Their excitement and enthusiasm is a perk of their personality, and they will leap up to love you! Their sweet nature allows them to play with other dogs and also play well alone. They enjoy to be snuggled and cuddled, but can also be very independent. They are compactly built with short heads, short tails, but strong limbs. They are smooth coated, with colors of brindle, seal or black and evenly marked with white.
