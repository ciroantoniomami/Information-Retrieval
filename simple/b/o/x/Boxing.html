Boxing

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Boxing is a kind of combat sport. It is usually held in a ring and two boxers (people who play the sport) wear special gloves and try to hit and make the other one fall.

  • Rubin "Hurricane" Carter

  • George Foreman
