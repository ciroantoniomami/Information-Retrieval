Boudica

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Statue of Boudica

Boudica(died 60/61 AD) was the queen of Iceni people in the Eastern part of England. She is most famous for leading her people in uprisings aginst the Roman Empire who controlled the island of Britain at the time. A statue of Boudica was put up in the Westminster area of London in 1902.
