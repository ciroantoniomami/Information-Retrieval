Bob Barker

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Bob Barker (December 12, 1923-) was the host of the television game show The Price is Right (from 1972 to 2006). He was also seen acting as himself in the 1996 Adam Sandler comedy movie Happy Gilmore.

Barker was the host of another game show, Truth or Consequences, from 1956 to 1975.
