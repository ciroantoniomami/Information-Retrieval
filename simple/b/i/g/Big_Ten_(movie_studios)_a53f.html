Big Ten (movie studios)

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The Big Ten refers to America's ten major movie production studios. Most of them have their offices in Hollywood.

The Big Six are the group of major companies who own them.

[edit] The studios

  • Warner Bros. - Time Warner
  • New Line Cinema - Time Warner
  • Sony Pictures Studios - Sony
  • MGM (Metro-Goldwyn-Mayer) - Sony
  • Walt Disney Pictures/Touchstone Pictures - Disney
  • Miramax Films - Disney
  • 20th Century Fox - News Corporation
  • DreamWorks - Viacom
  • Paramount Pictures - Viacom
  • Universal Studios - NBC Universal
