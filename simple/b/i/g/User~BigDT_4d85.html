[edit] General Wikiphilosophies

  • Have a short memory. A conflict today doesn't preclude working together tomorrow. By the same token, don't make it personal - it's about the product, not the personality.
  • Please think before you upload images. If you upload a non-free image that does not meet WP:FAIR, it will be deleted - you are only creating work for someone else.
  • Adminship ought to be easy come easy go. I will support the RFA of almost anyone who will list themselves at Administrators open to recall. Similarly, if there is any question whatsoever, I may oppose anyone who refuses to be listed.
  • Process is Important. While I don't mind a snowy delete when an article reaches the pile-on delete stage, out-of-process speedies without discussion can wind up taking more time on WP:DRV than they would have in the appropriate process and only result in hurt feelings.
  • Be careful with the term "cruft". Sometimes, it is what it is, but it can be offensive to refer to something that is important to someone else in that way.
