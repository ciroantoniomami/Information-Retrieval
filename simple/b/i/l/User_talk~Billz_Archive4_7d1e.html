[edit] How to revert

How do I revert and article? 71.231.130.56 has vandalised My new article Brenda Song. I can't re-write all of that stuff! It took me a long time. How do I revert it? --Kimberly Ashton 00:02, 7 September 2006 (UTC)

You live in Blasham you say? I visit Gloucestershire. I'd like to meet a Wikipedian in England but I suggest for the best of safety I keep the address which I settle in be kept secret. My computer is rather slow. --Kimberly Ashton 00:15, 7 September 2006 (UTC)
It is a good idea to keep your personal details. To revert an article, click on the 'History' tab, then on the time and date of the last good edit. You then click the 'Change this page' tab when you see the good edit. This will open your proper article for editing. Simply type 'rvv' (Revert vandalism) in the edit summary and click 'Save page'. You will be taken back to the article, where the vandalism should be removed. I hope this helps. Billz (Talk) 06:10, 7 September 2006 (UTC)
