[edit] Anon & welcome templates

I think we should go easy with the anon template. Eventually, old IP user talk pages must be deleted so that future users (or those where it's a shared IP) don't get confused. Also, if someone makes only a couple edits, it's fairly likely they will return on a totally different IP and never se the message. Reserve the anon template for users who look like they really want to contribute, say with a minimum of 10 edits. Be careful not to welcome vandals, so check contribs first.

Similarly, I think the Welcome template should only be used when a logged in account actually has made a couple edits. Some people make accounts to have watchlists, or just to check things out. There is a reason why a user talk page is not immediately created by the system. -- Netoholic @ 23:35, 17 September 2006 (UTC)

Yes, I agree. I will try my best to do these. I do check the contributions of each IP to ensure that they are not a vandal, however. Billz (Talk) 06:23, 18 September 2006 (UTC)

[edit] Category redirects

Hi again Bill :) I'm not so sure your redirect of Category:State in Asia was a good idea - category redirects hide the underlying category, so if someone were to mistakenly put an article in Category:State in Asia, nobody would notice. On the English Wikipedia, old categories are never redirected, but a manual redirect template: en:Template:Category redirect is used to point people at the new location. Even that isn't necessary, especially if the category is already empty, as in this case. The categories (State in Asia and State in Europe) also don't have any links pointing to them, so may I suggest a deletion? Cheers, Tangotango 16:16, 23 September 2006 (UTC)

I understand what you are saying. I will delete Category:State in Asia now. Thanks for bringing this to my attention. Billz (Talk) 17:14, 23 September 2006 (UTC)
Thanks for deleting the category :) If you get around to doing it, can you also do the same for Category:State in Europe please? Thanks! - Tangotango 03:34, 24 September 2006 (UTC)
Deleted. Billz (Talk) 07:39, 24 September 2006 (UTC)
