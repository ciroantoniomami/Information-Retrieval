Belfast

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Belfast (Irish: Béal Feirste) is the capital of Northern Ireland. It is the second largest city in Ireland, after Dublin. About 270,000 people live in the city. It became the capital when Northern Ireland was created in 1921. A famous ship called The Titanic was a built by the Belfast shipyard Harland and Wolff in 1911.
