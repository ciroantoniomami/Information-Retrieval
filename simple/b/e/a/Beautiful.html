Beautiful

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Something is beautiful if it pleases people's senses. Most often, beautiful things are good to look at.

However, something that has a good smell or tastes good can be called beautiful. Also, something that sounds good or feels can be called beautiful.
