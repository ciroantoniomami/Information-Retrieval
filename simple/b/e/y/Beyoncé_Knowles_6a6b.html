Beyoncé Knowles

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Beyoncé Giselle Knowles (born 4 September 1981) is an American R&B singer and actress. She was famous first as the lead singer of R&B girl group Destiny's Child, the best-selling female group of all time, with over 60 million albums and singles worldwide.
