Bavaria

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Position of Bavaria in Germany


Bavaria (German - Bayern) is the largest of the 16 states (Bundesländer) of Germany. The capital is Munich with 1,3 million people. About 12,4 million people live in Bavaria.

Village church in Franconia
Wies

Bavaria has seven parts: Oberbayern (Upper Bavaria), Niederbayern (Lower Bavaria), Schwaben (Swebia), Mittelfranken (Middle Franconia), Unterfranken (Lower Franconia), Oberfranken (Upper Franconia) and Oberpfalz (Upper Palatinate). Bavaria has 71 smaller parts and 25 free cities.

The highest point is the Zugspitze, which is also the highest mountain in all of Germany. The second biggest city is Nürnberg, which is famous for its gingerbread, a kind of Christmas bread.

Pope Benedict XVI was born in Bavaria.
