Baden-Württemberg is a federal state in Germany with 35 752 km². The capital is Stuttgart. The minister president is Günther Oettinger(CDU).

[edit] History

Baden-Württemberg was founded in 1952. Before then, Baden, Württemberg-Baden and Württemberg-Hohenzollern shared the area.

[edit] Tourism

The Black forest, the Swabian Alb and the Lake Constance are world famous holiday regions. The highest mountain is the Feldberg (1492m). Other important cities besides Stuttgart are: Freiburg, Heidelberg, Karlsruhe, Mannheim, Ulm and Heilbronn. The Danube, the Neckar and the Rhine are important rivers.
