Banana

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

A banana is a fruit that grows in clusters on tropical trees. There are several kinds of bananas in different colors. When most bananas are ripe, they turn yellow with brown patches. Many bananas have a curved shape, but some bananas are straight. Bananas have a lot of potassium. The potassium is helpful for the relief of cramps.

Bananas are grown around the world. The countries that export (send to other places for sale) the most bananas include India, Brazil, China, Ecuador and the Philippines.
