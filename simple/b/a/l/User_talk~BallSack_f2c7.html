[edit] Constructive Criticism

Hi, welcome to Simple.

You may want to put some more information in your new pages. It took me a while to learn this by trial and error:

  • You can put inter-wiki links in your pages. The easiest way is go to en and copy them from the article there.
  • You can put your pages in categories. You can do this by [[Category:XXX]] with XXX being the category in question. For example, I made your Pizza page into the Foods category. (Not all cats are made yet though. A good place to look is on similar pages.)
  • You can (most of the time) find a picture on Wikimedia that can help your article. Especially if it is a simple subject. The 'picture is worth a thousand words' idea is very true with English learners.
  • BE 1500 has a list of words that are good to use.

Good luck!-NickGorton 00:08, 23 August 2005 (UTC)

Please try to cat and iw your newly created pages so others do not have to go behind you and tidy up? Thanks. -NickGorton 00:42, 23 August 2005 (UTC)
