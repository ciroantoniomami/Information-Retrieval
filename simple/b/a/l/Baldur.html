Baldur

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Baldur's death

Baldur (Baldr, Balder) is Odin's second son. He is the god of innocence, beauty, joy, purity and peace. His wife is called Nanna. They have a son, called Forseti. Baldur also has a ship, which is the largest ship ever built. It is called Hringhorni. His hall is called Breidlabik.

Based on the Merseburg charms, his germanic name may have been Phol.
