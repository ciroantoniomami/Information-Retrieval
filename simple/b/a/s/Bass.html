Bass

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

This article is about the musical term. For other uses, see double bass, bass (singer), bass guitar, bass (fish), or bass (disambiguation).

Bass is low tones (low end frequencies) in music. It is usually made by a bass guitar or an acoustic bass (a double bass). It can also be an electronic sound, a deep singing voice, a baritone sax, or a deep bass drum. Bass, with treble (high tones), gives the well-rounded depth to music. Bass almost always is used with treble, made by instruments such as a guitar, piano, singing voice, horn, etc.
