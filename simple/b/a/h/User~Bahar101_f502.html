[edit] Short autobiography

I was born on the 16th of January 1993 in Columbus, Ohio to an Istanbulian family as their first daughter and second child. When we came back to Turkey, I had to learn Turkish, my mother tongue that I could not speak before. Playing the flute, athletics, dancing, reading newspapers and books are amongst my hobbies. I recently became an 8th grader. I would be happy if I could help to Wikipedia more. Greetings to everybody. Bahar (means spring in Turkish) Please don’t hesitate to write on my talk page. I would love to reply to your comments and questions about anything I can help and would teach Turkish at any level to those who are interested.
