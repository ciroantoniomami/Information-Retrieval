Bundesautobahn 45

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The Bundesautobahn 45 (in german: A 45), is an Motorway from the Ruhr area till Hanau.

The Highway begins in Dortmund, North Rhine-Westphalia and crosses Hesse. The A 45 ends in Bavaria.

[edit] Cities along the A 45

  • Dortmund
  • Hagen
  • Lüdenscheid
  • Siegen
  • Wetzlar
  • Gießen
  • Hanau
