Buzz Aldrin

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Colonel Edwin Eugene "Buzz" Aldrin, Jr., Ph.D (born January 20, 1930) is an American pilot and astronaut, and was the second human to land on the Moon (after Neil Armstrong) during the Apollo 11 mission. He was 39 when he set foot on the moon.
