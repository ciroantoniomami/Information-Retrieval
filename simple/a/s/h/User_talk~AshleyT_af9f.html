[edit] Definitions

Please do not create more articles for Category:Definitions. Many of those articles are going to have to go to wiktionary, according to our policy here where it says "Wikipedia is not a dictionary". Any article that is only a definition of a word, especially if it's a verb or an adjective, can be deleted as a 'dictionary definition', because that is the difference between a dictionary and an encyclopedia. You can pick up any hardback encyclopedia volume in a library and look through it, to get a rough idea of what kinds of things are in encyclopedias. Thanks.
