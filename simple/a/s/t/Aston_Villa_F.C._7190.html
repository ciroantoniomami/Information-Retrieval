Aston Villa F.C.

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Aston Villa Football Club, is an English football club.

The club plays in the FA Barclaycard Premiership.

They were started in 1874. They play at Villa Park in Birmingham.

[edit] Things Aston Villa have won

[edit] Cups

  • European Cup
    • 1982
  • European Super Cup
    • 1982, 1983
  • Inter-Toto Cup
    • 2001
  • FA Cup
    • 1887, 1895, 1897, 1905, 1913, 1920, 1957
  • League Cup
    • 1961, 1975, 1977, 1994, 1996
  • FA Youth Cup
    • 1972, 1980, 2002

[edit] Leagues

  • First Division Champions
    • 1893-1894, 1895-1896, 1896-1897, 1898-1899, 1899-1900, 1909-1910, 1980-1981
  • Second Division Champions
    • 1937-1938, 1959-1960
  • Third Division Champions
    • 1971-1972
