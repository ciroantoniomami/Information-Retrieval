Adult Swim

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Adult Swim (also called [adult swim]) is a group of television shows for adults that is shown on Cartoon Network. It began on September 2, 2001 and was created by Williams Street Studios. On March 28, 2005, Nielsen Media Research shows that Adult Swim is now separate from Cartoon Network (like Nickelodeon's Nick Jr. and Nick at Nite). Adult Swim is part of Turner Broadcasting System.

[edit] Friday

On Friday they have something called Friday Night Fix on AdultSwim.com. This began on September 16, 2005. However, on March 27, 2006, it was renamed Adult Swim Fix.

Adult Swim Fix starts at 6PM and ends at 6AM (eastern time). It shows new and favorite episodes of different Adult Swim shows. Recently, anime shows were shown on Adult Swim Fix.
