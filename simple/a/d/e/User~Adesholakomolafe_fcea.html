User:Adesholakomolafe

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Adeshola Komolafe holds a Bachelor Degree in Political Science and Masters in Public Policy and Administration from Bayero University, Kano. She attended different trainings such as Political Advocacy e- Training ( Global Youth Coalition on HIV/AIDS ), Reproductive Health and Peer Education Traini ng ( United Nations Children's Fund /National Youth Service Corps ), Peer Health Educators Training (Adolescent Health Project). She attended Alliance Francaise De Kano, where she took beginners classes in French. She published a book titled "SAVE OUR FUTURE". I took to writing (the book) because I felt teenagers needed to be educated on HIV/AIDS, the book was distributed free to young people in rural areas.

She is an accomplished researcher with several years of experience. She has spent some years doing research on issues affecting women in politics in Nigeria .She has also presented a number of papers to this effect, such as Women and Political Marginalization in Nigeria ; Politics Without Bitterness: Challenges of Nigerian Women in a Democratic Dispensation among others.

As the founder of SAVE OUR FUTURE (www.freewebs.com/saveourfutureng/) her group members spend their weekend volunteering at various communities where they educates women on HIV/AIDS and issues affecting women and politics. They also educate illiterates on issues affecting humanity i.e. conflict, violence, rape etc. She is a member of several organizations such as International Political Science Association (IPSA); Global Youth Coalition on HIV/AIDS; Taking It Global (TIG) and UNICEF Voice of Youth. She was one of the coordinators of the 2006 International AIDS Candlelight Memorial Service organized by Global Health Council and hopes to be one of the organizers in May 2007.

She joined Intermedia Group(www.intermediagroupng.com) in 2005 as a Field Officer. Ran the Dutse office of the company in Jigawa State and made a success of it. She is currently at the head of the research team, where she has been providing critical materials needed to provide excellent services for clients.
