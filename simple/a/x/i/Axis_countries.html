Axis countries

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The Axis countries is a name for some countries that were friends during World War II. They were evil dictatorships. They fought against the Allies. The Axis lost the war.

The most important Axis countries were:

  • Germany under Adolf Hitler
  • Italy under Benito Mussolini
  • Japan under Hideki Tojo and Emperor Hirohito

Smaller Axis countries were:

  • Hungary
  • Romania
  • Bulgaria

Countries that were not official Axis countries, but helped the Axis and fought at their side:

  • Finland
  • Thailand

Puppet countries that were made by the Axis:

  • Croatia
  • Manchukuo
  • Slovakia
  • Vichy France
