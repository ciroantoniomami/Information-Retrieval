Aquamarine

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

A cut Aquamarine.

Aquamarine is a gemstone, named for its blue-green color, which looks like some shallow seas. It is a type of beryl.

Wikimedia Commons has media related to:
Aquamarine

Aquamarine is also a color, a shade between green and cyan. It is named for the gemstone aquamarine.
