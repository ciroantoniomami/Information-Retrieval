AOL Arena

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

A wide view of the AOL arena in the north of Hamburg

The AOL arena is a concert and sports arena in Hamburg, Germany, which is owned and used by the Hamburger SV football team. Many Hamburg residents still refer to the arena as the Volksparkstadion (Peoples Park Stadium), though AOL bought the naming rights for 30 million marks or 15.3 million euros in 2001.

[edit] Museum

The stadium houses a museum, which presents the history of the Hamburger SV club.

The HSV museum is open daily from 10 am to 8 pm.
