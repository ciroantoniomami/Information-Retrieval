Types of anal sex can be: oral stimulation of the anus, using fingers for stimulation and using different objects for stimulation. Some people think that anal sex can be pleasant. First of all, anus is a part of the body that is tight and cannot lubricate itself. This is why a person should be careful when inserting something without damaging the walls of this organ. Secondly, the function of the anus is to cast out feces. On the other side, anus has many nerve endings that may give pleasant sensations to the person.

For a man pleasure comes from the stimulation of prostate that is found in anus. A woman gets satisfaction because anus and vagina are located close to each other. However, not everybody enjoys anal intercourse.

Anal sex is frowned upon in many countries and religions, however many men and women enjoy anal sex as it feels different than regular sex. In Islam, anal sex is strictly forbidden.

[edit] Health and anal sex

Although a woman cannot get pregnant through anal sex, sexually transmitted diseases can be spread unless a condom is used to cover the penis. One of the sexually transmitted diseases that can be transmitted from having anal sex without using a condom is HIV/AIDS. Possible tears in anus that could result from anal sex may open the way to many infections, which can cause serious health problems.
