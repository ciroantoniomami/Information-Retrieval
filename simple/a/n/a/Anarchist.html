Anarchist

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

An anarchist is someone who supports anarchism. They are opposed to hierarchy (few at the top, many at the bottom). They are against centralized government, wanting people to help each other through mutual aid and direct action. Some anarchist models include syndicalism and federations.

Some famous anarchists:

  • Abbie Hoffman
  • Alexander Berkman
  • Bob Black
  • Buenaventura Durruti
  • Emma Goldman
  • John Cage
  • John Zerzan
  • Mikhail Bakunin
  • Noam Chomsky
  • Starhawk
  • Leo Tolstoy

Anarchy throughout history:

  • Factory reclaiming in Argentina
  • Land reclaiming in the Spanish Civil War
