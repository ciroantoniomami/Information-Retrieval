Simple English Wiktionary has a dictionary definition (word meaning) for:
analysis

An analysis is what a person has when he or she has analysed something.

  • Analyse (or analyze) means to look closely and carefully at something or to study it. Other words with the same or similar meanings are evaluate, examine, and investigate.
  • Mathematical analysis is the name given to any branch of mathematics that looks at what functions are, how they behave, and what nice things can be done with them.
