[edit] February 2, 2006

Congratulations to everyone who has worked on Simple to bring it to this milestone of 7000 articles!
The Simple English Wikipedia now has 7,000 articles!
As of this milestone, Simple English has
  • 15,569 total pages (which includes "talk" pages, pages about Wikipedia, minimal "stub" pages, redirects, and others that probably don't qualify as articles).
  • 2,449 registered users. 11 of these are administrators.
  • 99,142 page edits. That comes to 6.37 average edits per page.


[edit] September 27, 2005

The Simple English Wikipedia now has 5,000 articles!

  • There are over 11,000 total pages in the Simple English Wikipedia.
  • There are over 1,600 register users, with 10 administrators (although only three of them are active).

[edit] September 16, 2004

The Simple English Wikipedia now has 2000 articles!

As of this milestone, Simple English has
  • 4448 total pages (which includes "talk" pages, pages about Wikipedia, minimal "stub" pages, redirects, and others that probably don't qualify as articles).
  • 532 registered users. 8 of these are administrators.
  • 17618 page edits. That comes to 3.96 average edits per page.

[edit] April 4, 2004

Register for Wikimania!

Wikimania, the first international Wikimedia conference, will be held in Frankfurt, in Germany. Register here: http://wikimania.wikimedia.org . All community members are invited to attend. Wikimania is an event from the community and for the community.

You can also offer to give a talk: see (http://wikimania.wikimedia.org/wiki/Call_for_papers).

If you want to help, rite us at http://meta.wikimedia.org/wiki/Wikimania:Feedback .
