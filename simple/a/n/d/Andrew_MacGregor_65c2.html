Andrew MacGregor

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Some people do not agree that this article is completely accurate.
They are talking about this on the talk page.


Andrew MacGregor is a former policeman who tried to find out what happened in the Australian Port Arthur massacre killings of April 27-April 29 1996 and decided that Martin Bryant, who police say killed people, did not do it. He talked about this with Wendy Scurr who was there, so that people would find out the truth.

On September 29 2004, in Launceston, Tasmania, Andrew MacGregor spoke to people [1], in which, he says that it was wrong for police to "Force the gunman to come to us" [2]. He has said that the Port Arthur massacre was an act of terrorism.

Andrew MacGregor has also said that he thinks that the London, England bombings were done by people who were in the London police force [3].
