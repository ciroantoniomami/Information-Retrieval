Please remember these simple rules when posting on my talk page:

Don't use "re:-" in your header!
Sign your posts with "~~~~" so I know who you are!
Be civil (nice)
Don't vandalise, for heavens sakes. Nobody cares if "my momma" is hot.
Try and give links in your header - it makes my life a lot easier.
Finally, make sure it's not a request to back you up in an argument - I like to remain neutral, and not get involved...so don't ask me!
