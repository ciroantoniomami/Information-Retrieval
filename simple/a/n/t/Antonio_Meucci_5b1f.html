Antonio Meucci

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Antonio Meucci is an early inventor of one of the first types of telephone. In 2002, the United States Congress ruled that he invented the first working telephone, although many people believe Alexander Graham Bell invented it.
