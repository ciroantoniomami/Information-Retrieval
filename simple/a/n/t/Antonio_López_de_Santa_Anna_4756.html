Antonio López de Santa Anna

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Antonio López de Santa Anna Pérez de Lebrón (February 21, 1794 - 21 June 1876) was a Mexican general and dictator. Under his ruleof Mexico, it lost half its territory. Santa Anna was 11 times president. He fought two wars against the United States. In the first war (1836) he won the famous battle of the Alamo, but later he lost the war and was taken prisoner by the Americans. He also lost the second war (1846-1848),
