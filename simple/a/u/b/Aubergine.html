Aubergine

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The aubergine (or eggplant) is a vegetable. It is in the Nightshade family of plants.It is related to the potato and tomato. Originally it comes from India and Sri Lanka. The fruit of the aubergine plant is commonly used as a vegetable.
