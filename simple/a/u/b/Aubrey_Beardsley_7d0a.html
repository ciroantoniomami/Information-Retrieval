Aubrey Beardsley

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Aubrey Beardsley (August 21, 1872 – March 16, 1898) was a British illustrator. He is best known for his drawings for Oscar Wilde's Salome.

[edit] Trivia

  • Beardsley is one of the famous people who appears on the cover of The Beatles, Sgt. Pepper's Lonely Hearts Club Band album.
