August

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

August is the eighth month of the year. It has 31 days.

August is named for Augustus Caesar. The month has 31 days because Augustus wanted as many days as Julius Caesar's month July.

August's flower is the Gladioli with the birthstone being Sardonyx. The meaning for the birthstone Sardonyx is Conjugal Felicity.
