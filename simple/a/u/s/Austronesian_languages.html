Austronesian languages

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Language families. Places where Austronesian languages are spoken are colored pink.

The Austronesian languages are a language family. They were originally spoken in Southeast Asia and the Pacific Ocean.

[edit] List of Austronesian languages

  • Anus
  • Bahasa Indonesia
  • Fijian
  • Hawaiian
  • Māori
  • Tagalog
  • Tuvaluan
