A.C. Milan

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

A. C. Milan are an Italian football club.

They were started in 1899 by an Englishman. They have won the Serie A football league 17 times and the Coppa Italia football cup 5 times. They have won more things than any Italian club except for Juventus.

They play at the Stadio Giuseppe Meazza Stadium in Milan, Italy.

[edit] Major wins

  • Italian Champion (17): 1901, 1906, 1907, 1951, 1955, 1957, 1959, 1962, 1968, 1979, 1988, 1992, 1993, 1994, 1996, 1999, 2004.
  • Italian Cup (5): 1967, 1972, 1973, 1977, 2003.
  • Italian Super Cup (5): 1988, 1992, 1993, 1994, 2005.
  • UEFA Champions League (6): 1963, 1969, 1989, 1990, 1994, 2003.
  • Cup Winners' Cup (2): 1968, 1973.
  • European Supercup (4): 1989, 1990, 1994, 2003.
  • World Club Championship (3): 1969, 1989, 1990
  • Latin Cup (2): 1951, 1956.
  • Mitropacup (1): 1982.
