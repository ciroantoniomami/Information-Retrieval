Alkaline earth metal

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Alkaline earth elements are related to the Alkali metals, but they do not react this acute, because they need more energy to remove two electrons. So they do not have to be stored in petrol. As ions they have a charge of +2.
