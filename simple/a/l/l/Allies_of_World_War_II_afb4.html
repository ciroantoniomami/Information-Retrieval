Allies of World War II

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

World Map of nations that fought in World War II.
The Allied nations are in green (those in light green entered after the attack on Pearl Harbor), the Axis nations in orange, and neutral countries in grey.

The Allied Powers (or Allies of World War II) were a group of nations that fought against the Axis countries, Nazi Germany, Facist Italy, and Japan, in World War II.

These nations were:

  • Soviet Union
  • The United States of America (drawn into the war after Pearl Harbor)
  • The United Kingdom
  • France (defeated early on)
  • Poland
  • China
  • Australia
