Alyson Michalka

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Alyson (left) performing with her sister Amanda

Alyson Michalka is an American actress and musician. She is most known for playing Keely Teslow in Phil of the Future on the Disney Channel. She has been in other Disney Channel productions like Now You See It... and Cow Belles. She takes part as half of the musical duo Aly & AJ, alongside her sister.

[edit] Personal life

Alyson was born in Torrance, California, United States on March 25, 1989. She plays guitar. She is of German ancestry. She has two dogs named Saint and Bandit.

[edit] Family

Her parents are Mark and Carrie Michalka. Carrie used to sing in a church band. This lead Aly into her singing career. Her father owns a company. Her sister, Amanda 'AJ' Michalka, is the other half of the musical duet Aly & AJ. Alyson is two years older than Amanda.

[edit] Acting appearances (Disney)

  • Phil of the Future (2004-2006)-- Keely Teslow
  • Now You See It... (2005 movie)-- Allyson Miller
  • Cow Belles (2006 movie)-- Taylor Callum

[edit] Trivia

  • She is 5 and 6 inches tall
  • She can play the piano
  • Belives in God and is studying Christianity
