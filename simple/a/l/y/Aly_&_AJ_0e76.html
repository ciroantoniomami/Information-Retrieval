Aly & AJ

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The sisters Alyson (left) and Amanda performing their infamous song Into the Rush

Aly & AJ are an American music group. The members are Amanda Michalka and her sister, Alyson who is two years older. The girls are famous for their song Rush.

[edit] Aly & AJ

[edit] Alyson

Alyson (nicknamed Aly) is seventeen years old. She helps writing the music and songs. She is one of the two guitarists.

[edit] Amanda

Amanda is fifteen year old. She helps writing the songs and contructing the music.
