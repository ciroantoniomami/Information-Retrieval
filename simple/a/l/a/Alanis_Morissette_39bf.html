Alanis Morissette

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Alanis Morissette

Alanis Nadine Morissette (born June 1, 1974) is a Canadian singer and songwriter. She was born in Ottawa, Canada. She began singing in Canada as a teenager in 1990. In 1995, she became popular all over the world.

As a young child in Canada, Alanis began to act on television, including 5 episodes of the long-running show, You Can't Do That on Television. She put out collections of songs, known as albums. Her first album was released only in Canada in 1990.

Groups of people have respected Alanis' music. In order to show respect for her music, people have given her prizes. Some of these prizes are known as Grammy Awards.

She also acted in several movies, including Kevin Smith's Dogma, where she played God.

[edit] Albums

Alanis has had many albums:

  • Alanis (Canada-only, 1991)
  • Now Is the Time (Canada-only, 1992)
  • Jagged Little Pill (1995)
  • Supposed Former Infatuation Junkie (1998)
  • Alanis Unplugged (1999)
  • Under Rug Swept (2002)
  • Feast on Scraps (CD/DVD, 2002)
  • So-Called Chaos (2004)
  • Jagged Little Pill Acoustic (2005)
  • Alanis Morissette: The Collection (2005)

Jagged Little Pill became a very popular album. Many people bought the album because they wanted to hear her deep, well-written songs. In the album, she sung songs about:

  • love (in the song "Head Over Feet")
  • life (in the songs "Ironic" and "You Learn")
  • her feelings (in the songs "Hand In My Pocket" and "All I Really Want")
  • sadness (in the song "Mary Jane")
  • anger (in the song "You Oughta Know")
  • frustration (in the songs "Not the Doctor" and "Wake Up")

The album Jagged Little Pill touched many people.

[edit] Songs

Alanis' most famous songs are:

  • "You Oughta Know" - This song is to Alanis's ex-boyfriend, a man Alanis once loved. In this song, Alanis is very angry. She wants her ex-boyfriend to know that he caused many problems after leaving Alanis for another woman.
  • "Ironic" - This song is about life. It contains several stories about unlucky people. In one of the stories, a man is afraid of flying on airplanes. He finally flies in one, but the airplane crashes.
  • "You Learn" - In this song, Alanis says that bad things happen in your life, but you learn from them. You can make bad things into good things. She wants you to try new things in life.
  • "Uninvited" - In this song, Alanis is not happy because she is famous. She does not know whether she wants to continue to be famous or not.
  • "Thank U" - In this song, Alanis thanks many things that have helped her. She thanks India, a country she visited and almost died in. She also lists ways she can improve herself.
  • "Hands Clean" - In this song, a man does something bad, and tells Alanis not to tell anyone else the bad thing the man did. Alanis hides the man's secret for many years.
