[edit] A Little History About Me

I first signed up as a registered user on at the English Wikipedia at Sunday, June 25, 2006. Although I naturally check in at the Wikimedia Foundation at least every day, Although I'm sort-of inactive here and don't contribute too much here, I'm planning to speed up my edits and become more active. But I contribute mostly in the main English Wikipedia which I began on May 1, 2006. I joined Esperanza on September 4, 2006.

Starting from mid-November 2006, I went on a wikibreak for 4 months until my return on February 11, 2007.

My favorite subjects are usually astronomy and meterology (which strongly includes tropical cyclones and tornadoes). Also if ya all been wondering why I put up a funny username like Alastor Moody is because, I made it as a joke after watching the movie, "Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire". If you thought I was a Harry Potter fan, you're correct. But if you thought of me as a dedicated fan (like a person who only thinks of Harry Potter and nothing else), no.

As of October 7, 2006, my userpage was vandalizied six times by an anyomous IP users by once screwing up an image, blanking my userpage, adding nonesense, blanking my userpage and adding annoying contents, adding nonsence again, and then calling me stupid. Administrators, whenever you see a vandal mess up my userpage, please revert it and I'll give you a reward; I really mean it. Thanx again and cheers:-)

A few things about me us that: I was born on March 15 during the mid-1990s in North Carolina, USA; I'm right now 12 years old and live in Los Angeles, California. My race is Asian-American for that my family came from South Korea, my oldest ancestors were probably origniated from western and central Asia. I'm also the first generation from my family to be born in the United States. But nowdays, I sort-of wish that I was rather born on October 2 instead.

My invented User Vandalism Scale invented.

  • Category 5: Catastrophic cases of vandalism; a danger to the wiki / full page protection necessary
  • Category 4: Severe cases of vandalism; a threat to the wiki
  • Category 3: Moderate cases of vandalism; urgent attention required / semi-portection page necessary
  • Category 2: Average cases of vandalism; attention recommened / administrators' & page patrollers' attention recommened
  • Category 1: Mild cases of vandlaism; little bit of attention necessary

[edit] How to become a sysop

  • Note: This information here isn't official and dosen't have to do any thing in the simplewiki.

Requirements

  • A user must have more than 800 edits in the simplewiki and at least 90 days of full experience to be a sysop.
  • For a user to become a sysop, he/she must have over 60% of all votes that support the nomiator.
  • A user must be familar with the rules on simplewiki to be a sysop.

Sysop article draft

  • "A user that wishes to be an administrator in the Simple Wikipedia, (a) must have a minimum experience of 4 to 5 months, (b) must have a minimum of 500+ number of edits, (c) and must have proved to be trusted on handaling admin tasks (example; reverting vandalism, greeting and helping other users, defending the wiki from vandals and users who repeatedly despited warned, give bad-faith edits, etc.,). In addition, he or she must qualify for at least two of these expected criteria in order to be an administrator and all candidates must have at least 2/3 of votes that says "support"." - Orginial copy
