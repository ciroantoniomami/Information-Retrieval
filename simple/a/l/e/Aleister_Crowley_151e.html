Aleister Crowley

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Aleister Crowley (October 12, 1875 – December 1, 1947) was a British mystic, occultist, writer, and mountain climber.

[edit] Trivia

  • Crowley is one of the famous people who appears on the cover of the Beatles,Sgt. Pepper's Lonely Hearts Club Band album.
