Arthropod

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Arthropods (phylum Arthropoda) are a group of related invertebrates, for example centipedes, millipedes, insects, arachnids, and crustaceans. Arthropoda comes from the Greek word for jointed feet, and it is the largest phylum of animals.
