Arnold Schwarzenegger

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Arnold Schwarzenegger (born July 30, 1947 in Austria) is an actor and the governor of the US state of California right now. He lives in Sacramento, the capital of California.

Schwarzenegger went to the United States in 1968 and, when he was older, became an actor. He has acted in many films, including the The Terminator series. In 2003, after Gray Davis was recalled (taken down from his position), he ran for governor of California and won.

Schwarzenegger is married to Maria Shriver.
