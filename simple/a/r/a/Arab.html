Arab

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Arab is a name for Semitic people who have Arabic as their mother tongue. They can natively be found in the Middle East or North Africa. There are between one and two hundred million Arabs living around the world. Most Arabs are Muslims, but there are also many Jews and Christians.
