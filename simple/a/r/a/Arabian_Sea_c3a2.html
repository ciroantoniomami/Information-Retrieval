Arabian Sea

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Ariabian Sea
Map of the Arabian Sea.

The Arabian Sea is a sea between Arabian Peninsula and Indian Peninsula. It covers around 4,600,000, km. It's one of the warmest seas.
