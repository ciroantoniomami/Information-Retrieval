Arabia

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Arabia is a peninsula in southwest Asia, across the Red Sea from East Africa. Arabia has a lot of oil. All of the countries in Arabia are Islamic. Mecca, Medina, and Jerusalem are the three holiest cities in Islam. Arabia has many countries (or states or nations). The largest country in Arabia is Saudi Arabia.
