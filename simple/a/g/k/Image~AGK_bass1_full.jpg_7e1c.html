Description

Photo of my double bass, front and side. It has a piezo pickup, as I use it mostly in a jazz band nowadays. Photos were taken by me on Dec 17, 2003 on a digital camera, had their colour adjusted, and edited together. This is pretty Q+D - lighting wasn't great and I turned the flash off to stop the bass looking like a toffee-apple.
