Age of Empires III

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Age of Empires III is a computer game made by the company Ensemble Studios. It is published by Microsoft. It is the third game of the Age of Empires games, and has better graphics than the ones before it. It is a Real Time Strategy game, which means that you control lots of people and build cities.

The plot is from 1500 to 1860.

[edit] Recommended to play

It is a new game (published on November 2005), so people will need very good computers to play the game, but it can be played on slower computers as well. It is only compatible with Microsoft Windows XP. The game is on three CDs. Your CPU should be above 1400 MHz, you need 256 MB RAM and 2 GB free space on the harddisk.

The developers said that they will publish the expansion Age of Empires III: The WarChiefs in October 2006.
