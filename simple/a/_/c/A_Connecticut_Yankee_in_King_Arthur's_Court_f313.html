A Connecticut Yankee in King Arthur's Court

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

A Connecticut Yankee in King Arthur's Court is a book written by Mark Twain, published in 1889.

In the story Hank Morgan, an American from late 19th-century Connecticut, travels back in time to King Arthur's Camelot. There he tries to improve the medieval society according to the morals and technologies of his time.
