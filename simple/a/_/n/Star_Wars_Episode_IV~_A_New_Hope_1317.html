Star Wars Episode IV: A New Hope

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Star Wars Episode IV: A New Hope was the first of the Star Wars films created by George Lucas. The movie was released in 1977 and is a mix of science fiction, fantasy, action and drama.

[edit] Plot

Princess Leia Organa (played by Carrie Fisher) is a prisoner to the evil Empire. She was captured by Darth Vader (played by David Prowse and James Earl Jones), who is helping the Empire stop a rebellion, by using the Death Star. Luke Skywalker (played by Mark Hamill), Obi-Wan Kenobi (played by Alec Guinness) and Han Solo (played by Harrison Ford) work together to rescue her. They are helped by the droids (or robots) R2-D2 and C-3PO. With the princess, they try to stop the Empire and bring peace and happiness to the galaxy.
