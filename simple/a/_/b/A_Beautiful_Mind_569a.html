A Beautiful Mind

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

A Beautiful Mind is a book and an Academy Award-winning film (starring Russell Crowe, Ed Harris, Jennifer Connelly, Christopher Plummer, and Paul Bettany). It is about the Nobel Prize (Economics) winning mathematician John Nash and his experiences of schizophrenia. The biography, was written by Sylvia Nasar. It was published in 1998. The movie, inspired by the biography of the same name, was released in 2001.
