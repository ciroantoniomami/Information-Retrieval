Airbus A380

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

An Airbus A380 lands in France.

The Airbus A380 is an aeroplane built by a company called Airbus. It is the biggest passenger aeroplane in the world. Even though it is big it is not the biggest airplane in the world (The Antonov 225 is the biggest in the world).

The Airbus A380 can carry over 555 people, and weighs over 250 tonnes. It is still being tested, so people cannot fly on it yet.
