Air force

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

An air force is the part of a country's military that fights in the air. People in the air force are called airmen. The air force is made up of airplanes, such as fighters and bombers. An air force may also have helicopters.

The biggest air force in the world is the United States Air Force (USAF).

Some navies also have their own air forces. These airplanes fly from aircraft carriers at sea.
