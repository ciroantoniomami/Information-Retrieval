Aircraft

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

An aircraft.

An aircraft is a flying machine. Aircraft are often said to belong to one of two groups:

Group 1 - Heavier than air

  • Have a motor
    • Airplanes (or aeroplanes)
    • Helicopters and autogyros
  • Don't have a motor
    • Gliders
    • Hang gliders
    • Paragliders
    • Parasails

Group 2 - Lighter than air include:

  • Balloons (do not have a motor)
  • Airships (always have a motor)
