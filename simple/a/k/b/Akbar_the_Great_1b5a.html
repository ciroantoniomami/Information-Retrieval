Akbar the Great

Jalaluddin Muhammad Akbar was the greatest Mughal ruler of India. He ruled the Mughal Empire from 1556 to 1605. He is also known as Akbar the Great (Akbar-e-Azam). He was born October 15, 1542 and died October 27, 1605. He was the son of Nasiruddin Humayun.
