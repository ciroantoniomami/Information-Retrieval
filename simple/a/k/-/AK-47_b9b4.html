AK-47

The AK-47 is a Russian assault rifle designed in 1947 by Mikhail Kalashnikov and first used in 1949. It and two updated versions called the AKM and AK-74 were used by the Soviet Union's Military. The AK-47 and its successors continue to be used by many of the world's armies. It is a cheap, reliable, and easy to use weapon.
