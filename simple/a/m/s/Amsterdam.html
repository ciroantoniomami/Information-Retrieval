Amsterdam

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Location of Amsterdam

Amsterdam is the capital and largest city in the Netherlands. It is famous for its canals and dikes.

Amsterdam has about 800,000 inhabitants, two universities and an international airport "Schiphol Airport".
