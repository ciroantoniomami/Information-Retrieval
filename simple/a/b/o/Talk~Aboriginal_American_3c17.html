Hi, I am wolfy (new message) from de-WP. I added the interwiki link to the German article named Indigene Völker Nordamerikas, which does not match exactly. A correct match (at the same level of abstraction) in German could be Ureinwohner Amerikas, which does not exist. The English meaning of the current target link is Indigenous Peoples of North America, treating this topic following the definition by United Nations Working Group on Indigenous Peoples (see in German: UNWGIP). So, to see The Whole Truth, you should have a look at the German Siehe auch: links (see also) for the American brothers and sisters I added there especially for you ;-)

  • Indigene Völker Mittelamerikas und der Karibik, Indigene Völker Südamerikas.
