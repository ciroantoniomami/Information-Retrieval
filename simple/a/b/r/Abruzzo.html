Abruzzo is one of the 20 regions of Italy. It's in the center of Italy on the Adriatic Sea.

[edit] Provinces

It is divided into four provinces:

  • L'Aquila (capital) in ialian: Capoluogo di regione
  • Chieti
  • Teramo
  • Pescara
