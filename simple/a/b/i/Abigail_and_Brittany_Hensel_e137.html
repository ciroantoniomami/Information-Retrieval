Abigail and Brittany Hensel

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Abigail and Brittany Hensel (born March 7, 1990 in Minnesota, United States) are dicephalic conjoined twins. They are joined at the pelvis and each one operates one side of their body, which means they have to do things together. Brittany is on the left, and Abigail is on the right. They have two spines that join at the pelvis. They have two stomachs and three lungs, and at birth they had a third arm in between their heads. The third arm was not properly developed, so it was amputated.
