Abdul Kalam

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Dr. Avul Pakir Jainulabdeen Abdul Kalam (born October 15, 1931) Indian scientist and engineer, is present (2006) President of India. He is well known by names like Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam or Abdul Kalam.
