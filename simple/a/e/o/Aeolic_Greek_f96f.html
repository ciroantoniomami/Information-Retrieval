Aeolic Greek

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Map showing where the Greek dialects were spoken around 400 BC. The area where Aeolic was spoken is light green.

Aeolic Greek was a dialect of the Greek language that was spoken on the island of Lesbos and nearby parts of Asia Minor.
