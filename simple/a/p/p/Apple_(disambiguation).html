Apple (disambiguation)

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

An apple is a kind of fruit. Apple may also refer to:

  • Apple Inc., a company that makes computers
  • Apple Corps is a company that founded by The Beatles
    • Apple Records, a record label and part of Apple Corps
  • See list of kitchen equipment for Apple Corer.
  • Apples, Vaud, is a village in the Swiss canton of Vaud
