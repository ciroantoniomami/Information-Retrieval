Aachen

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Aachen is a German city in the state of North Rhine-Westphalia. It has about 260,000 inhabitants and a well-known university, the RWTH-Rheinisch-Westfälisch Technische Hochschule and a bright history starting at the beginning of the middle-age.
