Aalborg

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Coat of arms

Aalborg is a city in Denmark. It has about 123,000 inhabitants.

Aalborg was founded about 700 A.D. and was mentionede first time in 1040. It got city rights in 1342 and seat of a Lutherian bishop in 1554.
