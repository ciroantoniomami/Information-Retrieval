Isabelle of Hainaut

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Isabelle of Hainaut (23 April, 1170 - 15 March, 1190) was a Queen of France.

She was born in Lille in 1170. Her father was Baldwin V of Hainaut. Her mother was Margaret I of Flanders. She married Philip II of France in 1180.
