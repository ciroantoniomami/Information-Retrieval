Ivy League

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Ivy League is a group of universities in the United States. They include:

  • Dartmouth
  • Yale
  • Columbia
  • Penn
  • Harvard
  • Princeton
  • Cornell
  • Brown
