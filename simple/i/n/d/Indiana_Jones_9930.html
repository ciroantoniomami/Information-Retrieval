Indiana Jones

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Dr. Henry "Indiana" Jones, Jr. is a fictional professor, archaeologist, and adventurer.

He originally appeared in a series of films produced by George Lucas and directed by Steven Spielberg in the 1980s, in which he was played by actor Harrison Ford.

Indiana Jones is noted for his trademark bullwhip, his fedora, and his great fear of snakes.

Indiana Jones is also the general name given to the series as a whole, which has three films, a TV series, novels, comics, video games, and other media.

  • Raiders of the Lost Ark (1981)
  • Indiana Jones and the Temple of Doom (1984)
  • Indiana Jones and the Last Crusade (1989)
