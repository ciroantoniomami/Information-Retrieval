Template talk:Infobox Officeholder

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

[edit] Documentation

{{Infobox Officeholder}} is used to create an infobox for various officeholders, including presidents, chancellors, governors general, prime ministers, and vice presidents.

The code below calls {{Infobox President}} as the subject is an American president; {{Infobox President}} is one of various alternatives all redirected to {{Infobox Officeholder}} (see template page). Note that not all fields are required.

{{Infobox_President 
| name=Harry S. Truman
| image         =Harry-s-truman-58-766-09.jpg
| order         =33rd [[President of the United States]]
| term_start    =[[April 12]], [[1945]]
| term_end      =[[January 20]], [[1953]]
| vicepresident =''None'' (1945–1949)<br|>
[[Alben W. Barkley]] (1949–1953)
| predecessor   =[[Franklin D. Roosevelt]]
| successor     =[[Dwight D. Eisenhower]]
| birth_date    =[[May 8]], [[1884]]
| birth_place   =[[Lamar, Missouri]]
| death_date    =[[December 26]], [[1972]], age 88
| death_place   =[[Kansas City, Missouri]]
| order2        =34th [[Vice President of the United States]]
| term_start2   =[[January 20]], [[1945]]
| term_end2     =[[April 12]], [[1945]]
| president2    =[[Franklin D. Roosevelt]]
| predecessor2  =[[Henry A. Wallace]]
| successor2    =[[Alben W. Barkley]]
| party         =[[Democratic Party (United States)|Democratic]]
| spouse        =[[Bess Truman|Bess Wallace Truman]]

|}}
