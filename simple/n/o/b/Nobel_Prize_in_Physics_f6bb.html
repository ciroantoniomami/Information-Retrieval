Nobel Prize in Physics

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The Nobel Prize in Physics is a Nobel Prize in the scientific area of Physics.

Some famous winners are:

  • Albert Einstein
  • Werner Heisenberg
  • Marie Curie
