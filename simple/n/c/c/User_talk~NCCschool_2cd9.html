[edit] Blanking v. Delete

Hey NCCschool,

Looks like you are doing a great job. I just wanted to let you know that blanking a page does not delete it. If you come across a nonsense page and think it should be removed add a deletion tag to it and an administrator will take care of it. For example, the article that you came across (2100), you should have just added {{d|Nonsense}}. That would have put the article in a category to be considered for speedy deletion. If you have any questions or need any help just let me know. Thanks, Psy guy 14:39, 10 May 2006 (UTC)
