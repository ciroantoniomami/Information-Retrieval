[edit] Greetings in English

Template:Wikipediholic Hallo, I know, you are peeping me, aren't you? :-) Heigh-ho! I'm a Wiki-fan in Bayern, Germany, would like to contribute what I know to Wiki to share the knowledge we have. As you see, I speak English, German, and Chinese, partly Cantonese and Japanese, can read a little Japanese and French. The following are what I created and modified in Chinese Wiki, that would be very kind of you, if you could leave several messages in my Talk Page. :-)

My interest lies in the "management information system", "marketing", and "consumer psychology" and so on.

Also in history, classical music, and cartoon...

btw: Nussknacker means nutcracker in German, 'cause the account "nutcracker" in English Wiki was already registered.

[edit] Introduction in Chinese

Nutcracker，胡桃夹子，来自德国的中国注册者。

个人兴趣和爱好：自己的专业，喜剧电影、日剧、韩剧、游泳、历史书、动漫、国际关系学。

最近打算：把德国DAX指数成分公司补全，然后把各主要投资银行补全，再后撰写一下德英法美的主要传媒。

最近的体会：字对字的翻译终非解决之道，发挥汉语的优势，德语英语的三四句浓缩成汉语的一句才是正途。

我说英语、德语、汉语和部分广东白话（粤语）、日语，能阅读部分日语、法语。

在Wiki上主打category:Money theory、category:Marketing、category:Development economics、category:Market psychology、category:Human resource。欢迎在我的对话页上给我留言。

最近很喜欢浏览德语英语Wiki，惊讶地发现英语Wiki已经突破一百万条目了，深深感到，汉语Wiki的空白太多了，很多领域的知识的框架还没有建立起来，觉得Wiki是实现人类知识机器积累和传播的好想法和实践，喜欢参与英语和德语Wiki的编辑，希望能让德语、英语国家和地区的人们多多了解中国。
