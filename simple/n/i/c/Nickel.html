Nickel

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

For other uses, see Nickel (disambiguation).
Small pieces of nickel in a glass tube

Nickel (chemical symbol Ni) is a magnetic transition metal. It has an atomic number of 28 and an atomic mass of about 59.
