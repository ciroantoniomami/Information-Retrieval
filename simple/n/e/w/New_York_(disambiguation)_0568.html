New York (disambiguation)

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

New York can refer to any of the following things:

  • A state of the United States,
  • A very large city in that state,
  • A county called New York County in that state that is the same as the Borough of Manhattan of New York City (rarely used), or
  • The Metropolitan New York covering New York City and several nearby counties in New York and New Jersey (rarely used).
