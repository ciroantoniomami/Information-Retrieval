Nepal

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Nepal is a country in South Asia bordering India and China. Mount Everest, the highest mountain in the world, is found there. Nepali is the official language. The capital city of Nepal is Kathmandu. Nepal is called one of the nicest travel destination because theres so many good things to see.
