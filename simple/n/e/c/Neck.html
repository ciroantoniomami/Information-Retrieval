Neck

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Neck can mean different things.

A neck is any narrow part of a thing. The neck of a bottle is where it gets smaller.

To neck is a slang term for when a boy and a girl kiss many times in a romantic way.

da Vinci drawing of the anatomy of the neck

In medicine, the neck is the part of the body that connects the head to the trunk.

The neck contains the cervical spine (the bones in your neck,) many important blood vessels, the trachea (what you breathe through,) the larynx (where your voice comes from,) the esophagus, lymph nodes, muscles, nerves, and connective tissue, and skin.
