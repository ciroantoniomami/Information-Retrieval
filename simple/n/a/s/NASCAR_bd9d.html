NASCAR

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

2004 Daytona 500

NASCAR(National Association for Stock Car Auto Racing) is a racing sport in the United States.The three largest racing series sanctioned by NASCAR are the NEXTEL Cup, the Busch Series and the Craftsman Truck Series.
