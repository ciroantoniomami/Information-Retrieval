Zuni

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The Zuni, also called Zuñi or Ashiwi are a Native American tribe which lives near the Zuni River, which flows into the Little Colorado River, in western New Mexico, USA.

They are famous for their language, which is not like other Pueblo or Native American languages. It is one of the few language isolates.
