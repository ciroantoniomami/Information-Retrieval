I am an atheist who believes in rebirth

I am an atheist who believes in rebirth (unusual, eh?). I am also drug liberal.

I like to study

Whatever interests me at the moment. Right now it is apples and squashplayers.

What I like to do

I like to bake (mostly bread, pastry and cakes). I like to train on getting a driver's license in class B (mostly I like to drive around in my family's car, but I also enjoy to study the theory). I like to play couronne on shockplay (my username there is The Zozza), play the board game power game (in reality). I also like to daydream and fantasize.

Writing

Writing is my biggest passion. I write poetry, short stories, lyrics, and articles on Swedish Wikipedia.

Music

Music is beside poetry my biggest passion, both listening to, and to make my own music (I make music I like to call noise rap). I listen to everything I think sounds good, which is too much to mention, but generally I listen to noise, hiphop (mostly Swedish), experimental music, different forms of hardcore, music with lyrics I find good (especially Morrisseys work, both solo and with The Smiths), and different kinds of instrumental music, especially Larry Adler (the chromatic harmonica master).

And when I sometime in the future have more free time on my hands, I very much would like to take care of a Neapolitan Mastiff, from puppy to the grave.

Exercise

I enjoy powerlifting, my favourite exercise is deadlift. I also like to swim breaststroke and take long walks.
