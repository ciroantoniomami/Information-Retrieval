Zombie

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The Zombies were a British psychedelic rock band in the 1960s.

A zombie, in some fiction and spiritual beliefs, is a dead person who has returned to life, as a walking corpse. Things that are like this are called undead. Zombies are often found in horror movies.
