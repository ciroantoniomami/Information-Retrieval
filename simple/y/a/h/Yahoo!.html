Yahoo!

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

 The English used in this article may not be easy for everybody to understand.

Yahoo! is a web portal and an internet content and service provider. It is known for its wide range of products and services including search engine, email, instant messaging etc and also contents like news, weather forecasts, financial news and other information.

Yahoo makes money off advertisments in their services.
