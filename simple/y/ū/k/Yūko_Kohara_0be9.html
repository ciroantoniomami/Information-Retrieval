Yūko Kohara

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Yūko Kohara(こはら裕子) (born August 4, 1977 in Nagasaki, Japan) is a Japanese manga artist. She writes in Ciao. She came out with Love Tension in 2002. She is known for having a warm style of painting.

  • Rakuen kids
  • Teruteru koiyohou
  • Mama♥trouble
