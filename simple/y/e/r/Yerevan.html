Yerevan

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Yerevan with Mount Ararat in the background

Yerevan (Armenian: Երեւան or Երևան) is the biggest city of Armenia. It is als the capital of Armenia. In 2004, about 1,088,300 people lived in Yerevan. Some people write Yerevan as Erevan. In past, Yerevan was called Erebuni or Erivan.
