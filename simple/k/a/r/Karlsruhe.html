Karlsruhe

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Karlsruhe is a city in the south-west of Germany, in the state of Baden-Württemberg. Karlruhe has about 285,000 inhabitants and an Technical University. It is the seat of the Bundesverfassungsgericht (Federal Constitutional Court, the Supreme Court of Germany).
