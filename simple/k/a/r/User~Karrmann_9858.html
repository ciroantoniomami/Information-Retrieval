I am a fanatic of the Gran Turismo series of games and I am very fluent at playing them.

I decided to join WikiProject Automobiles because I noticed that there are a lot of car articles that should be expanded.

[edit] Tails!

I have to be the biggest Tails fan on Wikipedia. It all started when I was 3, and my brother has a Sega Genesis, and Sonic 2. I instantly fell in love with the little fox following Sonic around the screen, and he has just stuck with me my whole life. I am still a raging Tails fan, even though my age is in the "growing out of it" group. I don't really get it, because I don't know if I will ever grow out Tails. I have gotten occasionally heckled by people because of this, as they feel that Tails is for little kids. I don't agree with that, and when they insult me for likeing Tails, I just stand tall, and stick up for myself and him. I am also a regular poster at a Tails forum, so I converse with other Tails fans. TAILS FOREVER!
