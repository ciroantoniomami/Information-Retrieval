Kevin Bacon

Kevin Norwood Bacon (born July 8 1958 in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania) is an American film actor who has starred in Stir of Echoes, Wild Things, JFK, and Apollo 13, among others. He has been married to actress Kyra Sedgwick since September 3 1988. They have two children, Travis and Sosie Ruth.
