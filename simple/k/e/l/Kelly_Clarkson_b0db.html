Kelly Clarkson

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Kelly Clarkson at the 2006 Winter Olympics.

Kelly Brianne Clarkson (born April 24, 1982) is an American singer who sometimes acts and has recorded two albums. She is recording her third album which will be released within a year. She was the winner of the first season of the television show American Idol.
