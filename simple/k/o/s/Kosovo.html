Kosovo

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The Kosovo ( Serbian Cyrilic:Косово и Метохија, Serbian Latinic : Kosovo i Metohija, Albanian: Kosova ) is a province in Serbia. Since the conflict that saw Yugoslavia fall apart, in 1999, it is run by the United Nations. There is Albania, Montenegro, and the Republic of Macedonia next to it. The largest city of the province, and its capital is Priština. About two million people live in Kosovo. Most of them are of Albanian origin.
