K is the eleventh (number 11) letter in the English alphabet.

[edit] Meanings for K

  • In chemistry, K is the symbol for potassium (from its latin name kalium).
  • in chess, K is a notation symbol for the king piece
  • In a deck of playing cards, the letter K is used to mark each of the kings.
