Kurdistan

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Kurdistan part of Iraq which works by itself since the 1991 Gulf War. Kurdistan is seeking independence from Iraq when the land is safer and once it is possible politically. Mostly Kurds occupy the land.
