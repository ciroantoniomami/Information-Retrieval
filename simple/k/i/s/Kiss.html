Kiss

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

KISS is the name of a heavy metal music group.
Kissing is the name of a town in Germany.

Kissing is an action done to display love or affection. Two people who like each other might kiss, or exchange kisses. When they do, they touch each other with their lips and their mouth. This can also be seen as a sign of friendship

Sometimes, in addition to touching each others lips, one person might put the tongue in the mouth of the other. This is called tongue-kiss or french-kiss.

Many people see kissing as an erotic gesture.
