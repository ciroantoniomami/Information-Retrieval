King of the Hill

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

King of the Hill is a 30-minute animated cartoon that feature a husband, a wife, a son, and their live-in niece.

[edit] Characters on King of the Hill

  • Hank Hill - An American propane and propane accesory salesmen living in Arlen, Texas, who believes in old-fashion ideals and watches modern society go down the drains.
  • Peggy Hill - a subsitute spanish teacher at Arlen high school, desperate to work and outdo other rivals. She writes a column in the local newspaper.
  • Bobby Hill - A strange Jr. High student who plays soccer, although Hank wants him to play football.
  • Luanne Platter - a hairdresser who lives with the Hills. Hank desperately wants her to go to college or get an apartment.
  • Dale - A conspiracy theorist who believes the government is out to get everyone. Dale always comes out with snappy comments to annoy Hank.
  • Bill - A constantly depressed victim of divorce, who is an Army barber.
  • Boomhauer - A ladies man who talks very fast, almost in giberrish, and loves old cars.
