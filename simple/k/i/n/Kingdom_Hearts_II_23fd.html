Kingdom Hearts II

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Kingdom Hearts II is a video game for the Playstation 2. It has the characters of the Final Fantasy series and Walt Disney characters; for example Donald Duck, Goofy and Mickey Mouse.

[edit] Story

The story of Kingdom Hearts 2 continues from Kingdom Hearts and Kingdom Hearts: Chain of Memories, for Playstation 2 and Game Boy Advance.

For the first three hours of the game, the player plays as Roxas. Roxas is a youth who lives in a quiet town. He is part of a gang. The gang has two other boys, Hayner and Pence, and a girl called Olette. There is an opposing gang, made up of three boys, called Seifer, Rai and Vivi, and a girl called Fuu. The player does simple thingss and watch cutscenes. Next, the player gets to play as Sora. Then Sora's story picks up from where it leaves you in Kingdom Hearts Chain Of Memories.
