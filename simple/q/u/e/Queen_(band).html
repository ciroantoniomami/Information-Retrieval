Queen (band)

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Queen

Queen is an English rock band. They are one of the most popular musical groups of all time. Its members were Freddie Mercury (vocals), Brian May (guitar), John Deacon (bass), and Roger Taylor (drums). Queen is not disbanded, even though Freddie Mercury is dead. He died of AIDS on November 24, 1991. John Deacon retired to spend more time with his kids, and the other two members of Queen are touring with Paul Rodgers starting in 2005.
