Pluto (god)

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Pluto is the god of the Underworld in Roman mythology.

Pluto took Proserpina, who was the daughter of Ceres. Ceres was very upset and cried and this caused the season to change to Winter. Pluto took Proserpina because he wanted to marry her.
