Plectrum

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Guitar picks

A plectrum or pick is an object used to play certain types of musical instruments. The plectrum is used to pluck a string which causes the instrument to make sound. The instrument most commonly plucked is the guitar, but other instruments include the harpsichord and lute.
