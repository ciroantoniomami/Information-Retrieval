Pikachu

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Pikachu is an small yellow animal from the fictional Pokémon world that was created by Satoshi Tajiri. Pikachu is an electric Pokémon, so it can attack other Pokémon using electricity. It can to evalute (to change its type as Pokémon). It can to say only three sounds "Pi" , "Ka" and "Chu" for diffrent emotions.

Pikachu is the most popular of all of the Pokémon because it is the mascot of the series. It is also one of the main stars of the Pokémon cartoon and comic books. On one of the first pokemon games, Pokemon Yellow, it is the starter pokemon.
