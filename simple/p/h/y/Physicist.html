Physicist

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

A Physicist is a scientist who studies Physics.

Some famous physicists are:

  • Albert Einstein
  • Stephen Hawking
  • Isaac Newton
  • Rene Descartes
  • Marie Curie
