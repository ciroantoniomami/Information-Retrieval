Paul Gardner Allen (born January 21, 1953) is an American who created Microsoft with Bill Gates. He is the fifth richest person in the world, and he currently lives in Mercer Island, Washington.

Allen also owns two sports teams, the Seattle Seahawks and the Portland Trail Blazers.
