Paul

Paul is a common male name. Some men who were called Paul are:

  • Saint Paul, the apostle;
  • Paul of Russia, a Russian Tsar;
  • Prince Paul of Yugoslavia ("Pavle"), a prince of the Kingdom of Yugoslavia in the 1930s
