Patricia Nixon Cox

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Patricia walking down the wedding aisle with her father, Richard Nixon

Patricia Nixon Cox was born on February 21, 1946 in Whittier, California. She is the daughter of former US president Richard Nixon and Pat Nixon. She is the older sister of Julie Nixon Eisenhower.

Unlike her sister, she performed many ceremonial jobs, like going with her father to campaign stops and state visits. She married Edward F. Cox on June 12, 1971.

She tutored inner-city children.

She lives a private life, staying home to look after her son, Christopher Nixon Cox, who was born in March 1979.
