Parthenon

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The Parthenon is a temple in the middle of the Acropolis in Athens, Greece. It was a temple to Athena for nearly 1000 years.It is considered one of Greece's greatest arcitecture accomplishments.
