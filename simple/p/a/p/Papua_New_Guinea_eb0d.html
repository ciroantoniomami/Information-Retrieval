Papua New Guinea is a country near Indonesia. It is on the east side of the island New Guinea; which is made up of Western New Guinea, Papua, previously called Irian-jaya, and Papua New Guinea, with some more near by islands. The capital city of Papua New Guinea is Port Moresby.
