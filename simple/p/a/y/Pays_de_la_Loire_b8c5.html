Pays de la Loire

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Pays de la Loire is in red

Pays de la Loire (or Pays-de-la-Loire) is a region of France. Its capital is Nantes. 3.2 million people live in the region. 710.000 of them live in the urban area of the capital.
