Pyongyang

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Pyŏngyang metro system

Pyongyang (평양 직할시 in hangul, 平壤直轄市 in hanja, 'Phyŏngyang chighalshi' in romanization) is the capital and biggest city in the Asian country North Korea. It is one of the few places in North Korea that foreigners (other than Americans and South Koreans) can travel to, because the government is so secretive.
