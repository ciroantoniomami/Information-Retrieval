Potsdam

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Potsdam as seen from above

Potsdam is a German city near Berlin. It is located 26km south-west of Berlin. It is the capital of Brandenburg. In 2005 about 146,635 persons lived in this city.
