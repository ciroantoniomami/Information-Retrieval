Polonium

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Polonium is a rare radioactive metalloid. It is chemical element 84 on the periodic table and its symbol is Po.

Polonium is radioactive and toxic making it difficult to handle even in very small amounts. It is also the element that killed the Russian spy Alexander Litvinenko also it is fuel for nuclear reactors second only to uranium
