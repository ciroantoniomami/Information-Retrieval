Porsche

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Porsche 911 Cabrio

The Dr. Ing. h. C.F. Porsche AG is a German sportscar manufacturer with seat in Stuttgart Zuffenhausen. Porsche was created in 1931 as a design engineering department.
