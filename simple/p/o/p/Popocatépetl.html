Popocatépetl

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Clouds around Popocatépetl.

Popocatépetl is an active volcano and the second highest mountain in Mexico after Pico de Orizaba (5,610m). People often call it "Popo". Popocatépetl is the Aztec word for "Smoking Mountain".

It is 5,426 metres (17,802 feet) high.
