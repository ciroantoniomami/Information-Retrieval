[edit] question (again)

how do you use the defcon?

like raise or lower levels?— This unsigned comment was added by Michael223 (talk • contribs) .

To change the defcon level, you can go to the specific subpage (User:PullToOpen/WDefcon) and change the image. The image names are easy to guess (Image:Dc_four = Defcon 4, Dc_one = Defcon 1 etc.), so you can just change the wording slightly to get a new image. You can also leave a small caption if you want to. There aren't a whole lot of people who use this, though. Also, don't forget to sign your posts on talk pages with four tildes (~~~~). PTO 01:34, 15 February 2007 (UTC)
