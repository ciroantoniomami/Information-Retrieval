Pumpkin

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Some pumpkins

A pumpkin is a gourd. Pumpkins are usually orange, although they can also be white, and are shaped like spheres or stretched-out spheres.

Pumpkins can be eaten, and are often turned into pies. Pumpkins can also be used for decoration, like when they are turned into jack-o-lanterns.
