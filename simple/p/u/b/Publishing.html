Publishing

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Publishing is making information available for public view. It means as well the company that makes and sells literature or information. In some cases, authors may be their own publishers.

Traditionally, the term really means the distribution of printed works such as books and newspapers, but it is possible in the internet, too.
