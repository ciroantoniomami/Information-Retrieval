Persian language

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Persian, also called Farsi, is the official language of the Islamic Republic of Iran, or Afghanistan, and of Tajikistan. In the past, all of these countries together were called Persia.

Its alphabet is very similar to Arabic, and it has taken on many French words. Persian has many dialects. One of these is Dari.
