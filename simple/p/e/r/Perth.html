Perth

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Perth is a city in Australia. It is the capital city of the state of Western Australia. Nearly 1.5 million people live in the city. It is the fourth biggest city in Australia, behind Sydney, Melbourne and Brisbane. Perth was founded in 1829 by Captain James Stirling.
