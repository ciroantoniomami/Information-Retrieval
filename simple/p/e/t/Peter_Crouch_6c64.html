Peter Crouch

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Peter James Crouch is a English football player. He born in 1981 and he is 201 cm tall. Crouch is currently playing for Liverpool F.C. and England. He scored 11 goals in 16 games, and he is the first player to ever score 10 goals for England in a single year. He is known for his famous robotic dance celebration, which he first performed after his goal against Hungary in May 2006.
