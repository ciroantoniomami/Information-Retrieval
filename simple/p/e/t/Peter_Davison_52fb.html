Peter Davison

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Peter Davison (born April 13, 1951) is an English actor. He was best known for his roles as Tristan on All Creature Great and Small and as the fifth Doctor, on Doctor Who from 1981 to 1984.
