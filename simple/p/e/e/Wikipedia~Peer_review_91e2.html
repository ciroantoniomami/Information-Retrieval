Wikipedia:Peer review

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

This page is a place to get comments and peer review from other Wikipedians on Wikipedia pages. Use this page to post links to articles that you would like comments and peer review on. If you see an excellent article here, add {{vgood}} to the page.

For comments on policies, other users, technology, and other miscellaneous concerns that are not directly related to reviewing article content, use Simple Talk.

[edit] Dolley Payne Todd Madison

I request for a peer review of Dolley Payne Todd Madison. It only has one red link. It also needs sources.Coffsneeze 19:12, 6 April 2007 (UTC)

  • Many terms need linking (too many to list). Several section need rewording. Her period as first lady is somewhat ambiguous and could mean that J.Madison was President during her term as First Lady rather than her being First Lady during his term as President (He was President while she was First Lady). The final sentence definately needs cleaned up. There is too much being said about trivial matters such as her father and son. They do not need 3-4 sentences to say they were poor and a problem child. (if they need to be mentioned by more than just a name anyway). Image needs to be thumbnailed (and likely the size removed - usage of the default size is usually best) -- Creol(talk) 07:32, 7 April 2007 (UTC)
