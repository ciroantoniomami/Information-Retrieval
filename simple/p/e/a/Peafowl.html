A Blue peacock spreading its feathers.

A peafowl is a kind of bird of the Phasianidae family. It can mean the Asiatic peafowl (Pavo) or the Congo Peafowl (Afropavo).

The male peafowls (called peacocks) have long, colorful feathers. Female peafowls (called peahens) have shorter, brown feathers. I think that they eat strange things, however I don't exactly know what Sorry:(
