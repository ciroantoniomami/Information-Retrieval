Provence-Alpes-Côte d'Azur

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Provence-Alpes-Côte d'Azur is in red

Provence-Alpes-Côte d'Azur is a region of France. It is all in the south, next to Italy. Its capital is Marseille. Very often its name is shotened to PACA or Paca. 4.5 million people live in the region, 1.35 millon of them in the urban area of the capital.

The region is currenlty 3rd as to economic output (After île-de-France and Rhône-Alpes). It is first as to tourism.
