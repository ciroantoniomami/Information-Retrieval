Prime Minister

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

A prime minister manages the politics of a country. They are most important in countries with kings and queens (known as Monarchs) because they are in charge of most areas of politics - an example of this is the United Kingdom. In countries with a president they manage the running of the state - an example of this is France.

Countries with a Prime Minister:

  • United Kingdom (with Monarch)
  • France (with President)
  • India (with President)
  • Canada (with Monarch)
  • Australia (with Monarch)
  • the Netherlands (with Monarch)
  • Thailand (with Monarch)
  • Singapore (with President)
