Princeton University

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Princeton University is a private university that is in Princeton, New Jersey in the United States. Princeton opened in 1746 as "the College of New Jersey". It was re-named "Princeton University" in 1896.[1] It was the fourth college/university in the United States[2] and is one of the 8 Ivy League schools.
