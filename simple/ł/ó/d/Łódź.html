Łódź
Łódź in Poland

Łódź is the second-largest city in Poland, with 776,297 inhabitants. It is in the Łódź Voivodship in the centre of country. In Polish the word also means "boat". The mayor of Łódź is Jerzy Kropiwnicki. The most famous person is Euzebiusz Smolarek . The football club there is called Widzew Lodz and ŁKS Lodz .
