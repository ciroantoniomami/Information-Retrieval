Category:6th century

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

This category is for articles and events specifically related to the 6th century, which began in the year 501 and ended in the year 600.

Note that the year 600 is included in Category:600s in the 7th century but is actually part of the 6th century.
