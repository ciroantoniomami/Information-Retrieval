Lindsay Lohan

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Lindsay Morgan Lohan (born July 2, 1986) is an American actress and singer. She was featured in films such as Freaky Friday, Parent Trap and Mean Girls.

[edit] Milestones/Career

Movies like Freaky Friday, Parent Trap and Mean Girls made Lindsay popular in America, and showed her talent in singing. She plays the guitar very well and has done many singing parts.
