List of astrophysicists

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

An astrophysicist is a person whose profession is astrophysics.

Hannes Olof Gösta Alfvén (Sweden, 1908 - 1995)
Subrahmanyan Chandrasekhar (India, United States, 1910 - 1995)
Herbert Dingle (United States, 1890 - 1978)
Frank Drake (United States, 1930)
Freeman John Dyson, (England, United States, 1923 - )
Arthur Eddington (England, 1882 - 1944)
Jaan Einasto (Estonia, 1929)
Thomas Gold (United States, 1920)
Stephen Hawking (England, 1942 - )
William McCrea (Republic of Ireland, 1904 - 1999)
Igor Dmitrievich Novikov (Russia, 1935)
Hubert Reeves (Canada, 1932)
Yakov Borisovich Zel'dovich (Russia, 1914 - 1987)
