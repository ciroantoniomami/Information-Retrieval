List of towns in Greenland

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

This is a list of the towns in Greenland that have over 1,000 people living in them (the first name is in Greenlandic, the second is in Danish):

  • Nuuk (Godthåb) Pop. 14.501
  • Sisimiut (Holsteinsborg) Pop. 5.350
  • Ilulissat (Jakobshavn) Pop. 4.533
  • Qaqortoq (Julianehåb) Pop. 3.144
  • Aasiaat Pop. 3.100
  • Maniitsoq (Sukkertoppen) Pop. 2.859
  • Tasiilaq (Ammassalik) Pop. 1.848
  • Paamiut (Frederikshåb) Pop. 1.817
  • Narsaq Pop. 1.764
  • Nanortalik Pop. 1.509
  • Uumannaq Pop. 1.366
  • Qasigiannguit Pop. 1.320
  • Upernavik Pop. 1.178
  • Pop. stands for population.
