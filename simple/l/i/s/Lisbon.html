Lisbon

Lisbon, the capital of Portugal, is a city. There are about two million people living there. Lisbon is on the River Tagus. It has a pleasant climate and has about 220 days of sunshine each year. There are many beautiful beaches close to the city. There are also many seafood restaurants, historical sites and monuments.
