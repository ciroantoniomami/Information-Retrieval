List of U.S. states by elevation

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

A topographical map of the United States.

This is a list of United States states by elevation. Elevation means the height something is at. The highest point in the U.S. is Mount McKinley at 20,320 feet (6,194 m). The lowest point in the U.S. is Badwater in Death Valley at 282 feet (86 m) below sea level.
