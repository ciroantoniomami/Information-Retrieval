what breed is a cat with more then 5 toes on the front paw (paw =1) (165.139.0.11 19:32, 9 October 2006 (UTC))

Those cats are called polydactyl cats. They actually aren't a specific breed. It is a mutation which can occur in any kind of cats. This includes the big cats like tigers and lions. I don't know of any cat breed defined by having more toes than normal, though. Badbilltucker 16:19, 29 October 2006 (UTC)

Is "felids" a typo or is it some word I don't know? Lilsaalex 20:29, 18 January 2007 (UTC)

It is a real word. It means "a member of family Felidae" - lions, tigers, house cats. Not exactly a common word though, and certainly not simple. -- Creol(talk) 20:35, 18 January 2007 (UTC)
