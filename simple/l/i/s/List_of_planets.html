List of planets

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

This is a list of planets.

Planets of the Solar System:

  • Mercury
  • Venus
  • Earth
  • Mars
  • Jupiter
  • Saturn
  • Uranus
  • Neptune

Pluto used to be called the ninth planet, but in August 2006, a group of astronomers decided it should be called a dwarf planet instead, along with Eris and Ceres.
