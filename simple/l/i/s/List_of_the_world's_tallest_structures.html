List of the world's tallest structures

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

This is an incomplete list of the world's tallest structures, past and present. Structures no longer standing are in italics. List is highly non-continous. Most of the tallest structures are TV masts, followed by a mix of the taller tower-type structure (Like the CN Tower) and the taller high rises buildings (like the Sears Tower). Scattered among these are other structures including oil platforms, electrical towers, bridge towers, etc.
See: List of masts, List of towers, List of Skyscrapers

A minimum height limit of 350 m is used for practical purposes to keep the list to a reasonable length.
