Liechtenstein

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Liechtenstein is a country in Europe. It is the fourth smallest country in the world and one of the only doubly landlocked countries in the world. Liechtenstein is located between Austria and Switzerland. The capital of Liechtenstein is Vaduz, a very small town of 5.000 people. Liechtenstein is famous for its many private banks.
