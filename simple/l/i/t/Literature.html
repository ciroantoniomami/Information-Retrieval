Literature

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Literature is the collection of written works of a language, time, or culture. Literature helps tell the stories of a people's values or interests. Many people consider literature to be one of the best ways to learn about other cultures.

Literature can also mean imaginative or creative writing, which is looked at for its artistic value.
