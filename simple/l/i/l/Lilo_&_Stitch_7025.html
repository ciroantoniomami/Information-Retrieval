Lilo & Stitch

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Lilo & Stitch is an animated movie, released by Disney in 2002. The movie was written by and directed by Chris Sanders and Dean DeBlois.

[edit] Plot

Spoiler warning: The text below is about parts of the story or how it ends.

An evil alien scientist named Dr. Jumba Jookiba creates many creatures. This is against the law of his planet, and he is arrested. One experiment, named "Stitch", escapes the planet. He flies a spaceship to Earth.

On Earth, a little human girl named Lilo adopts him (under the illusion he is a dog). Lilo lives in Hawaii, a series of islands in the United States of America. Lilo's mother and father have died, so her older sister Nani takes care of her.

Stitch becomes Lilo's best friend. Lilo tries to teach Stitch how to be good. She uses music by Elvis Presley in her lessons.

The Single used by Disney to promote the film was Suspicious Minds, peformed by Gareth Gates who shot to fame on the UK TV program, Pop Idol.
