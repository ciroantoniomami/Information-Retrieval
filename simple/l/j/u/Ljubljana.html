Ljubljana

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Ljubljana is the largest and capital city of Slovenia. It became the most important settlement in the area when Romans ruled Slovenia. There are many museums with things from that time in Ljubljana.

As of 2002, there are 265 881 people living in Ljubljana. University of Ljubljana is located in Ljubljana.
