Category:LGBT

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

LGBT is an acronym. It means Lesbian, Gay, Bisexual, and Transgender.

This is a community (group) of people who have sexual orientation or gender identity that is different from what is common.

Some people put other letters at the end:

  • Intersex
  • Questioning or Queer
  • Ally (someone not LGBT but who supports LGBT people)
  • TS for Two Spirit
