London Eye

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The London Eye at twilight

The British Airways London Eye, also known as the Millennium Wheel is the largest observation wheel (a type of Ferris wheel) in the world. It was opened in 1999. It is 135 metres high.
