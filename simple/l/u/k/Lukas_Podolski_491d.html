Lukas Podolski

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Lukas Podolski (born June 04 1985 in Gleiwitz, Poland) is a German footballer. At the moment he playes for Bayern München. From 2004 until 2006, he played for 1. FC Köln. On the German national football team he played 38 matches and made 22 goals.
