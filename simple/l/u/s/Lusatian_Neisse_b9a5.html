Lusatian Neisse

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The Lusatian Neisse is a river in the Czech Republic (Lužická Nisa), Germany (Lausitzer Neiße) and Poland (Nysa Łużycka). It is part of the Oder-Neisse line.
