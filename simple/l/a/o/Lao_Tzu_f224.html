Lao Tzu

Lao Tzu / Laozi (老子 Lǎozi, 600?/400? BC) was one of the most famous Chinese philosophers. He was the author of a book called Tao Te Ching (道德經). According to this book, Tao ("the Way") does not change and it is the universal truth. His Taoism has been enormously influential in China. Lao Tzu is also known as Senior Lord in Taoism of the general public.
