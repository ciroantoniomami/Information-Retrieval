Las Palmas de Gran Canaria

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Las Palmas de Gran Canaria (in red) on a map of Gran Canaria

Las Palmas de Gran Canaria is the most important city in the Canary Islands, in the Atlantic Ocean. The city, with a population of 378,000, is the eight-largest in Spain. The weather is sunny and dry because it is near the Sahara Desert.
