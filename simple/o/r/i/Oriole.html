Oriole

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

This is a Golden Oriole

An Old World oriole is any bird in the Oriolidae family. Old World orioles live outside of America. Another type of oriole is the New World oriole. New World orioles are members of the Icteridae family.

Orioles are birds with bright colored feathers.

The Golden Oriole is the only kind of Old World oriole which is not a tropical bird.

  • Yellow Oriole or Green Oriole, Oriolus flavocinctus
  • Dark-throated Oriole, Oriolus xanthonotus Template:StatusSecure
  • White-lored Oriole, Oriolus albiloris
  • Isabella Oriole, Oriolus isabellae Template:StatusEndangered

  • Black-headed Oriole, Oriolus larvatus

  • Silver Oriole, Oriolus mellianus Template:StatusVulnerable
  • Figbird Sphecotheres viridis
