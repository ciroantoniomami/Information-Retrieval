Orange (fruit)

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

For the city in New South Wales, Australia, see Orange, New South Wales
Oranges

An orange is a type of citrus fruit. The kind usually seen in stores is the "sweet orange", but other kinds include tangerine, clementine, and mandarin oranges. There are many other different kinds of oranges, and orange juice is very popular for breakfast all over the world.

The color orange takes its name from the fruit.

"Orange" is one of only a few English words that does not rhyme with anything.
