Old Possum's Book of Practical Cats

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Old Possum's Book of Practical Cats is a book of poems about cats. The cats in these poems act like humans. It is by T. S. Eliot. It was first sold in 1939. Andrew Llyod Webber used most of the poems in his Broadway musical called Cats
