Oldham Cricket Club are a cricket team which currently plays in the Central Lancashire League.

Their home ground is The Pollards.

The club's youth teams had a very successful season in 2003. The Under 18s, captained by Liam Garnet and the Under 15s, captained by Luke Procter, both won their league championships.

[edit] Honours

First Division: 1909, 1916, 1957, 1979, 1982
Wood Cup: 1943, 1979, 1983, 1985, 1988
Second Division: 1902, 1930, 1944 (shared), 1983, 1985, 1986
Burton Cup: 1976
