Ostrów Mazowiecka is a head town of Ostrów County, Mazowsze Voivodship, Poland.

  • Population (as of 2004): 23 486
  • Area: 22,09 km²
  • Town status: Since 1434
  • Postal code: 07-300, 07-302
  • Current Mayor: Mieczysław Szymalski
  • Community: Ostrów
  • Twin town: Brembate di Sopra (Italy)
