Oxford

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Oxford is a city in England on the River Thames. It is a very old city, with the oldest buildings being from before the twelfth century.

Oxford has two universities, Oxford University which is oldest university in an English speaking country, and 'Oxford Brookes' which is a lot younger. Oxford is filled with history from its universities to its wonderful museums.
