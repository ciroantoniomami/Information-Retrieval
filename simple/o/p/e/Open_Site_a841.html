Open Site

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Open Site is a free internet encyclopedia created by many editors. Anyone can apply to become an editor or donate content.

[edit] Categories

At Open Site all the content is separated into categories. There are eleven main categories which each have many subcategories. These are the main categories:

  • Arts - has information about art and entertainment.
  • Business - has information about economics.
  • Computers - has information about computers.
  • Games - has information about games.
  • Health - has information about health.
  • Home - has information about home and families.
  • Recreation - has information about leisure.
  • Regional - has information about countries.
  • Science - has information about science.
  • Society - has informaiton about society and people.
  • Sports - has information about sport.

[edit] Public Forum

The Open Site Public Forum is a way that someone can talk about Open Site and anything that Open Site has information about. It was created by Open Site editors so that editors and other people could talk to each other.

It was started in May 2004 by Chris Poskitt and Robert Oschler. Both people are editors at Open Site. There are also other editors who help manage the forum so that it is not abused.

[edit] Childrens' Encyclopedia

Open Site started an encyclopedia for children in August 2004. The writing and pictures on the childrens' encyclopedia are simpler and easier to understand than other encyclopedias. The encyclopedia contains tools and facts as well as articles.
