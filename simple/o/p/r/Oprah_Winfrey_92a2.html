Oprah Winfrey

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Oprah Gail Winfrey (born January 29, 1954) is an African-American businesswoman. Winfrey owns a number of books, magazines and movies in addition to what she is most famous for, her television talk show The Oprah Winfrey Show which has been on television throughout the United States since September 8 1986.
