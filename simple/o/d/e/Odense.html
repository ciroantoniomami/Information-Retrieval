Odense

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Odense Palace

Odense is the third largest city in Denmark with about 146,000 inhabitants and the capital of the island of Funen.

It was founded in 988 A.D. and was sometimes seat of the parliament. In 1805 it became capitol of the island Funen.
