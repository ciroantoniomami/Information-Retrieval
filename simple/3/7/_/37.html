[edit] Events

[edit] By place

[edit] Roman Empire

  • March 18 - The Roman Senate annuls Tiberius' will and proclaims Caligula Roman Emperor.
  • Caligula's attempt to have himself deified creates friction between himself and the Senate
  • An earthquake destroys Antioch.

[edit] By topic

[edit] Religion

  • Abilene is granted to Agrippa I.
  • Saint Peter founds the Syrian Orthodox Church. (traditional date)
  • Probable year of the conversion of the Pharisee Saul of Tarsus to Christianity after a vision. After 39 he is recognised as Saint Paul.

[edit] Births

  • December 15 - Nero, Roman Emperor.
  • Josephus, Jewish historian.

[edit] Deaths

  • March 16 - Tiberius, Roman Emperor, strangled by Macro, Praetorian Prefect.
  • May 1 - Antonia Minor, daughter of Mark Antony and Octavia Minor; grandmother of Caligula.
  • Marbod king of the Marcomanni
